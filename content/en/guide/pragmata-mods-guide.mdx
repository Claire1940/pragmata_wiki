## Core Mechanics: Mod Uptime and Slot Management

Before diving into specific builds, every player must understand the concept of "Uptime." In this **pragmata mods guide**, uptime refers to the percentage of time a mod's benefit is actually active during combat. A mod with 100% uptime, such as **Hardened Suit**, provides its bonus constantly regardless of your positioning or enemy state. Conversely, a mod like **Close Quarters** only activates when you are within a specific range of a target.

To maximize your efficiency, you must purchase additional mod and node slots from the terminal. You do not start with a full suite of options; investing currency into your mod capacity is often more valuable than upgrading a single weapon's raw damage early in the game.

| Mod Category | Primary Benefit | Recommended Uptime |
|---|---|---|
| **Stat-Based** | Flat increases to HP, Defense, or Attack | 100% (Always active) |
| **Conditional** | Buffs triggered by distance or specific actions | 50% - 70% (Skill dependent) |
| **Synergy-Based** | Mods that require other mods or nodes to function | Variable (Build dependent) |

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize unlocking at least three mod slots before the mid-game boss encounters. Having a variety of active buffs is more effective than one highly leveled mod.

## The Top 7 Mods for Normal Mode

The following mods have been identified as the most effective tools for clearing the game's standard difficulty. They offer a mix of survivability and high-damage potential that can carry you through the majority of the lunar campaign.

### 1. Close Quarters
This is arguably the strongest mod for players who favor aggressive playstyles. It directly benefits the shotgun and the late-game shockwave gun. Since most enemies in *Pragmata* attempt to close the distance to the player, the uptime for this mod is naturally high, allowing you to deal massive burst damage without complex setups.

### 2. Nice Nodes
Hacking is one of the most powerful mechanics in the game. **Nice Nodes** increases the drop rate of hacking nodes from defeated enemies. If you are running a build centered around Diana’s hacking abilities, this mod is non-negotiable. It ensures you always have the resources necessary to manipulate the battlefield.

### 3. Untapped Potential
This mod provides a dual benefit: it increases both your attack damage and your defense after a successful hack. Because it offers raw stats without complex conditions other than playing the game's core loop, it is a staple for any balanced build.

### 4. Hardened Suit
For beginners, **Hardened Suit** is the gold standard. It provides a flat 10% increase to your total HP. With 100% uptime, it is the most reliable way to increase your "effective health" and survive unexpected difficulty spikes.

### 5. Heat Transfer
This mod accelerates the rate at which the heat gauge builds up on enemies. When used in isolation, it is decent, but when paired with specific hacking nodes, it becomes part of a devastating crowd-control loop.

### 6. Cursed (The Glass Cannon)
For veteran players, **Cursed** offers a 20% damage increase at the cost of 50% of your total HP. This is the ultimate high-risk, high-reward modification. It is best used in Lunatic mode once your suit is fully upgraded, giving you enough base HP to survive at least one or two hits even with the penalty.

### 7. Long Range Targeting
The polar opposite of Close Quarters, this mod is essential for sniper builds. It rewards players for maintaining distance, making it the perfect companion for the slower, high-damage rifles that require charge time.

## Advanced Build Synergies

The true depth of the **pragmata mods guide** lies in combining these individual components into a cohesive combat strategy. Here are three high-level builds that utilize mod synergies to trivialise difficult encounters.

### The Overheat Executioner
This build focuses on the "Heat" mechanic to down enemies and deal massive area-of-effect (AoE) damage. By stacking heat-related mods, you turn every encounter into a series of explosions.

1.  **Heat Transfer**: Increases heat gauge build-up speed.
2.  **Combust Mode**: Further accelerates the heat gauge and adds a burning DoT.
3.  **Critical Shot**: Allows for an execution move once an enemy is overheated.
4.  **Collateral Damage**: Causes 30% of your Critical Shot damage to splash to nearby enemies.

### The Sniper Specialist
Snipers in *Pragmata* are powerful but slow. To make them viable against fast-moving lunar threats, you must use mods that compensate for their long charge times.

*   **Long Range Targeting**: Increases damage based on distance.
*   **Overclocked Weaponry**: Significantly reduces the time required to charge a shot.
*   **Hardened Suit**: Necessary to survive if an enemy manages to close the gap before you can fire.

### The Hacking Commander
This build relies on Diana to do the heavy lifting. By maximizing node drops and hacking efficiency, you can control the flow of battle without firing a single bullet.

| Requirement | Mod/Node | Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| **Resource Generation** | **Nice Nodes** | Constant supply of hacking energy |
| **Stat Scaling** | **Untapped Potential** | Buffs suit stats every time Diana hacks |
| **Crowd Control** | **Shock Node** | Stuns enemies, allowing for safe hacking windows |

> **Warning:** Hacking builds are extremely vulnerable during the "upload" phase. Ensure you have invested in suit defense or have a high-mobility mod equipped to avoid being interrupted.

## Upgrading and Firmware Priorities

As you progress through the game, you will earn currency that can be spent at the terminal. While it is tempting to dump all your resources into weapon damage, a balanced approach to firmware updates will yield better results in the long run.

1.  **Mod Slots**: Your first priority. More slots mean more simultaneous buffs.
2.  **Diana's Hacking**: Upgrading Diana's level unlocks more complex nodes and faster hacking speeds.
3.  **Suit HP/Defense**: Essential for the late game, especially if you plan on using the **Cursed** mod.
4.  **Primary Unit Damage**: Only upgrade this once your utility and survivability are stable.

Remember that weapons in *Pragmata* are not permanent. Once you exhaust your ammunition, that weapon is gone until you find more. Your suit mods, however, are permanent fixtures of your build. Always invest in the pilot and the suit first, as they are the only constants in the ever-changing lunar environment.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the best mods in Pragmata?**

A: Most high-tier mods are obtained by completing specific story milestones or purchasing them from the upgrade terminal using lunar currency. Some specialized mods like **Cursed** may require completing secret combat trials found in the open-world sections.

**Q: How many mod slots can I have in total?**

A: By default, you start with limited capacity, but you can upgrade your suit to hold up to five mod slots simultaneously. This **pragmata mods guide** recommends prioritizing these slot upgrades as early as possible to maximize your combat flexibility.

**Q: Is hacking better than using traditional weapons?**

A: Hacking is arguably the strongest damage-dealing method in the game when fully optimized. However, it requires specific mods like **Nice Nodes** and **Untapped Potential** to be truly effective. A hybrid build that uses shotguns for defense and hacking for offense is generally considered the most balanced approach.

**Q: What is the best mod for a "Glass Cannon" build?**

A: The **Cursed** mod is the core of any glass cannon build, providing a 20% damage boost. To make this viable, you should pair it with high-mobility mods and ensure your hacking nodes are set up to stun enemies before they can reach you.
