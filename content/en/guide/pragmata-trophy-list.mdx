## Combat and Hacking Achievements

A significant portion of the **Pragmata trophy list** revolves around the synergy between Hugh and Diana. While Hugh handles the heavy firepower, Diana’s hacking capabilities are the key to maximizing damage and controlling the battlefield.

### Mastering the Hacking Grid

To unlock trophies related to Diana’s hacking, you must become proficient with the real-time grid puzzles. These appear when aiming at enemies and require you to connect blue nodes to a green finalization node.

*   **Multi-Hack Specialist:** Use a Yellow Multi-Hack node to affect three or more enemies simultaneously.
*   **System Overload:** Successfully complete a hack on a boss-level enemy without taking damage as Hugh.
*   **Node Runner:** Connect 10 or more blue nodes in a single hacking sequence for maximum damage bonus.

### Weapon Proficiency Trophies

Hugh’s arsenal is divided into color-coded categories. To earn the "Master at Arms" achievement, you will need to find and utilize every weapon type found within the lunar base.

| Weapon Category | Example Weapon | Trophy Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| **Green (Utility)** | Energy Net | Trap 50 enemies in stasis. |
| **Red (Power)** | Railgun / Shotgun | Eliminate 3 enemies with a single charged Railgun shot. |
| **Standard** | Infinite Pistol | Defeat a major boss using only the standard pistol. |

## The Gifting System: Filling Diana's Sketchbook

Perhaps the most unique aspect of the **Pragmata trophy list** is the Shelter interaction system. As you collect "rem" (data and energy) during missions, you can use the Luna Filament system to 3D print gifts for Diana. These gifts trigger unique cutscenes and add pages to her sketchbook, which serves as a visual record of her growing humanity.

### Essential Gifts for Completionists

To earn the "Home Sweet Home" gold trophy, you must transform the cold, metallic shelter into a vibrant living space. This requires finding and gifting specific items scattered throughout the game's simulations.

1.  **The Globe:** Teaches Diana about Earth's geography. Trigger the interaction where she tries to guess Hugh's home.
2.  **The CRT TV:** A funny interaction where Diana uses her android powers to change channels but resorts to "caveman tactics" when the signal drops.
3.  **The Children's Slide:** A bittersweet moment where Diana realizes Hugh is too large to play with her, highlighting their unique bond.
4.  **The Sketchbook:** Likely the final item or a progressive reward that tracks all other interactions.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Some gift items are found in simulation areas that may become inaccessible after certain story beats. Check your map for "High-Value Rem" signatures before leaving an area.

## Luna Filament and Suit Upgrades

Progression in Pragmata is tied to the Luna Filament currency. This resource is dropped by hostile AI and found in crates. Trophies in this category focus on maximizing Hugh’s survival capabilities and Diana’s support protocols.

*   **Apex Astronaut:** Fully upgrade Hugh's suit, including jump height, hover duration, and dash distance.
*   **Overdrive Master:** Trigger Diana's "Overdrive Protocol" (ultimate blast attack) 25 times.
*   **Scholar of the Moon:** Collect all text dumps and hollow recordings to unlock the full history of the Deli Corporation.

### Upgrade Priority Table

| Upgrade Type | Resource Cost | Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| **Stamina Gauge** | Medium | Increases dash and hover time for better verticality. |
| **Hacking Speed** | Low | Reduces the time needed to navigate the grid in combat. |
| **Filament Capacity** | High | Allows you to carry more currency for high-end 3D printing. |

## Platinum Roadmap: Step-by-Step

Achieving the Platinum on the **Pragmata trophy list** will likely require at least one full playthrough and a partial cleanup on a higher difficulty or via chapter select.

1.  **First Playthrough (Any Difficulty):** Focus on the story and enjoy the relationship between Hugh and Diana. Try to collect as much Luna Filament as possible and gift items to Diana whenever you return to the Shelter.
2.  **Exploration Cleanup:** Use the chapter select or the "Escape Hatch" checkpoint system to find any missing text dumps or weapon categories.
3.  **The Sketchbook Grind:** Ensure every gift has been printed and every unique interaction has been triggered. This is often the final hurdle for the Platinum.
4.  **Combat Challenges:** If there are specific "Kill X enemies with Y weapon" trophies remaining, the simulation levels are the best place to farm these.

A: No, achievements and trophies are typically reserved for the full retail release. However, the skills you learn in the demo—such as the hacking grid and perfect dodging—will directly contribute to your ability to earn trophies quickly when the full game launches.

**Q: Is there a trophy for playing on the hardest difficulty?**

A: Based on previous Capcom titles, a "Hard" or "Professional" difficulty trophy is highly likely. These usually unlock after completing the game once, requiring a second playthrough with upgraded gear.

**Q: How many trophies are in the total list?**

A: While the final count is pending, most major Capcom releases feature between 40 and 50 trophies, including the Platinum. Expect a healthy mix of Bronze, Silver, and Gold milestones.
