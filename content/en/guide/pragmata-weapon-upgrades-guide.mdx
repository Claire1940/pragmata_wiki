## The Shelter: Your Hub for Upgrades

Deep within the sprawling lunar station lies the Shelter, a vital safe haven where Hugh and Diana can regroup, repair, and most importantly, upgrade their gear. Accessible via conveniently placed escape hatches throughout the levels, the Shelter acts as your de facto headquarters and a critical checkpoint. While the demo build might have excluded this feature, developers have confirmed its full presence in the complete 2026 release.

The Shelter is equipped with advanced 3D printers, powered by Lunafilament technology, which are the cornerstone of Pragmata's upgrade system. These printers allow you to enhance not only Hugh's weapons but also Diana's hacking nodes, creating powerful synergies that are essential for combat efficiency. Regular visits to the Shelter are crucial for maintaining your edge against Idis's ever-evolving robotic army.

## Fueling Your Firepower: Lunum and Lunafilament

Every upgrade in Pragmata hinges on a unique lunar mineral known as Lunum (or Luna Filib). This valuable resource is the primary currency for all maintenance and enhancement activities within the Shelter. Without a steady supply of Lunum, your weapons will remain at their base effectiveness, leaving you vulnerable.

Acquiring Lunum is straightforward:
*   **Exploration:** Scour every nook and cranny of the research station. Lunum caches are often hidden in less obvious locations, encouraging thorough exploration.
*   **Enemy Drops:** Defeating hostile robots and larger mechanoids will frequently yield Lunum. More formidable enemies often drop larger quantities or rarer forms of the mineral.

The 3D printers in the Shelter utilize this raw Lunum to produce Lunafilament, the material necessary for fabricating and enhancing your weapons and hacking nodes. Strategic resource management is key; decide whether to invest in immediate repairs or save up for a significant weapon upgrade that could define your playstyle.

With a limited supply of Lunum and a diverse range of weapons and hacking nodes to enhance, careful planning of your **Pragmata weapon upgrades guide** is crucial. Your chosen strategy should reflect your preferred playstyle and the challenges you anticipate.

*   **Aggressive Playstyle:** Focus on upgrading primary damage-dealing weapons like the Primary Unit and Armor-Piercing Unit. Invest in Diana's hacking speed to quickly create "Open State" opportunities.
*   **Defensive Playstyle:** Prioritize upgrades for Hugh's suit integrity and potentially the Shockwave Gun for crowd control. Enhance Diana's hacking to gain damage buffs, allowing Hugh to quickly eliminate threats.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I upgrade my weapons in Pragmata?**

A: You can upgrade your weapons in the **Shelter**, which features 3D printers that utilize Lunafilament technology. The Shelter is accessible via escape hatches found throughout the game's levels.

**Q: What materials do I need for Pragmata weapon upgrades?**

A: The primary material for all upgrades is **Lunum** (also known as Luna Filib). You can acquire Lunum by exploring the moon station and by defeating enemy robots.

**Q: How do weapon upgrades synergize with Diana's hacking?**

A: Diana's hacking makes enemies vulnerable, entering an "Open State." Upgrading Hugh's weapons, especially those that deal high damage, allows you to maximize the damage output during these critical windows, making your combined attacks far more effective.

**Q: Do specialized weapons have unlimited ammo after upgrading?**

A: While upgrades can significantly increase the ammo capacity and durability of specialized weapons, some might still retain limited ammunition or the risk of breaking. Strategic use and understanding of your **Pragmata weapon upgrades guide** are crucial for managing these powerful tools.
