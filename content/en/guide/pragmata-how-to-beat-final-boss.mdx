## Understanding the Sector Guard Protocol

The final boss, identified as the Sector Guard Protocol, is a massive security construct designed to neutralize "unregistered guests." Unlike previous encounters, this boss utilizes a "Scanning" mechanic that allows it to adapt to your combat style. Before engaging, ensure your suit’s primary power is fully reconnected and your tactical modules are optimized for both long-range chip damage and heavy melee finishers.

The arena at Eda’s is circular, offering several vantage points but very little hard cover. You must remain mobile. The boss’s primary goal is to initiate a lockdown, which restricts your movement and forces you into tight corridors of fire. To prevent this, you must consistently damage its sensory arrays located on the upper chassis.

## Phase 1: The Tactical Shoot Out

When the battle begins, the boss will disengage its safety locks and initialize the sector guard protocol. In this initial stage, the boss relies heavily on projectile fire and area-of-effect (AoE) denial. This is where your knowledge of **pragmata how to beat final boss** mechanics is first put to the test. You must balance aggressive shooting with defensive positioning.

The boss will frequently shout "Neutralizing threats" before unleashing a spread of energy bolts. Your goal here is to "Break them up"—specifically, the drone clusters that orbit the boss. By destroying these smaller units, you reduce the boss’s defensive shield and open a window for a "Smash" attack.

## Phase 2: Overdrive and Aggression

Once the boss's health drops below 60%, it will enter an aggressive state. The dialogue "Stupid bastard... you, let me try" signals a shift in the boss's AI logic. It will begin moving faster and using melee strikes that can shatter your suit's integrity in a single hit. This transition is often where players fail, but understanding **pragmata how to beat final boss** patterns here makes the fight manageable.

The key to Phase 2 is the **Overdrive Protocol**. As the music intensifies, Diana will initiate an energy surge. You will see a prompt for "Overdrive protocol initiated." This is your cue to go all-out. Your weapons will deal increased damage, and your "Smash" attacks will have significantly more impact.

### Maximizing Damage During Overdrive

1. **Wait for the Prompt**: Do not waste your high-tier consumables until the Overdrive protocol is active.
2. **Focus on the Limbs**: Damaging the legs will temporarily "Break them up," causing the boss to stumble.
3. **Synergy Attacks**: Use the "Take this!" command to coordinate a heavy strike with Diana.
4. **Energy Restoration**: If your levels are low, look for the "Energy levels restored" notification after a successful synergy hit.

> **💡 Tip:** During the Overdrive phase, your dash has no cooldown. Use this to phase through the boss's physical sweeps and reposition behind its weak points.

## Phase 3: The Final Stand

As the boss nears 10% health, it will attempt a final "Finish" move. The arena will become cluttered with debris, and the boss will fire everything it has left. This is the most chaotic part of learning **pragmata how to beat final boss** techniques. The boss will scream "NOW TO SMASH" and attempt a devastating ground slam.

You must stay calm. The boss is "so close" to defeat, but rushing in recklessly will lead to a quick death. Use your remaining tactical charges to stay airborne. When the boss finishes its heavy slam, it will be vulnerable for a few seconds. This is your window to "Take this" and deliver the final blow.

## FAQ

**Q: What happens if Diana takes too much damage during the final boss fight?**

A: Diana cannot be "defeated" in the traditional sense, but if she takes heavy fire, the Overdrive protocol will take longer to charge. You should actively draw aggro away from her to ensure your offensive buffs are ready as soon as possible.

**Q: Is there a way to skip the scanning phase of the Sector Guard Protocol?**

A: No, the scanning phase is a scripted part of the encounter. However, you can use this time to reload your weapons and pick up any energy canisters scattered around the arena before the "Neutralizing threats" phase begins.

**Q: What is the most effective weapon for pragmata how to beat final boss strategies?**

A: The Lunar Railgun is generally considered the best choice due to its high burst damage. However, players who prefer a faster playstyle may find the Plasma Carbine more effective for clearing the drone clusters in Phase 1.

**Q: Can I beat the final boss without using the Overdrive protocol?**

A: While theoretically possible, it is not recommended. The boss has high damage reduction during the later stages of the fight that can only be efficiently bypassed by the power boost provided during the Overdrive protocol.
