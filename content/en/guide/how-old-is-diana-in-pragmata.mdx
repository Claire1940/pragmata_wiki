## Diana’s Role in Gameplay and Combat

Diana is not a passive NPC. In Pragmata, she serves as the primary tool for environmental interaction and enemy debuffing. While Hugh handles the heavy weaponry, Diana provides the technical edge needed to bypass the "Cradle" security protocols. She hitches a ride on Hugh's back, allowing the player to trigger her abilities without switching characters.

### Hacking and IDUS Interaction

Most enemies in the game are shielded by high-frequency armor that Hugh's standard weapons cannot penetrate. This is where Diana's hacking mini-game becomes essential. By aiming at an enemy, you trigger a tactical overlay where Diana can "Decode" or "Multihack" targets.

| Ability Name | Gameplay Effect | Tactical Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Decode** | **Increases damage taken by 50%** | Best used on Bosses or heavy "Metal Gear" units. |
| **Multihack** | **Disables armor on up to 3 targets** | Ideal for clearing groups of "Basic Walkers." |
| **Scanning** | **Highlights weak points and loot** | Use this in dark corridors to find upgrade nodes. |
| **IDUS Breach** | **Stuns all enemies in a 10m radius** | Hugh's ultimate defense when surrounded. |

### The "System Strain" Mechanic

Using Diana's powers comes with a risk. In the 2026 build, Capcom introduced "System Strain." If you force Diana to hack too many high-level targets in quick succession, she may temporarily glitch or shut down. During this window, Hugh loses all hacking support and must protect a vulnerable Diana until she reboots.

## Relationship and Progression: Building the Playroom

One of the most unique aspects of Pragmata is the "Base of Operations" progression. Between missions, Hugh and Diana return to a safe zone within the lunar facility. Here, players can see the "wholesome" side of their bond. As you find items on Earth or within the labs—such as crayons, old books, or toys—you can give them to Diana to unlock new dialogue and abilities.

1.  **Collect Earth Artifacts:** Find items like "Seashells" or "Old Photos" in the ruins.
2.  **Upgrade the Playroom:** Use currency to turn the stark lab into a cozy living space.
3.  **Unlock Hacking Nodes:** Diana learns new skills based on the "knowledge" she gains from these items.
4.  **Character Skins:** Progression unlocks various outfits, including reinforced jackets for Diana and specialized EVA suits for Hugh.

This progression system emphasizes that despite the mystery of **how old is diana in pragmata**, her development is tied directly to the player's willingness to explore and "teach" her about the world they are trying to reach.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the official Pragmata Diana age?**

**Q: Is Diana a playable character?**

A: Diana does not have a traditional health bar. If Hugh fails to protect her during a "System Strain" event or a specific security breach, she can be captured or deactivated, leading to a mission failure.

**Q: Why does Diana glitch in the trailers?**
