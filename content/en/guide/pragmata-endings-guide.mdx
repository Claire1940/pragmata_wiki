## The Standard Campaign Ending: What to Expect

Before you can dive into the complexities of secret mods and post-game missions, you must first complete the primary narrative arc. The standard ending occurs after the final confrontation in Sector 6. This encounter tests everything you have learned regarding Diana's support abilities and the protagonist's heavy-duty arsenal.

Upon defeating the final boss for the first time, you will witness the "Standard Ending." This conclusion sets the stage for the wider mysteries of the Pragmata universe but leaves several questions unanswered regarding the origin of the signal and the ultimate fate of the lunar colony. Once the credits finish, the game will prompt you to save your data. **Do not overwrite this save**, as it is the key to accessing all subsequent content.

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep a backup of your completed save file in a separate slot before starting the post-game to ensure you don't accidentally lose progress on specific sector completion percentages.

## Accessing the "Unknown Signal" Mode

To begin your journey toward the secret ending, you must return to the title screen after finishing the story. You will notice a new option: **Unknown Signal**. This mode is essentially the "Master Quest" or "Hard Mode" of Pragmata, but it functions more as an epilogue that integrates directly with your existing progress.

When you select Unknown Signal, the game will ask you to load a completed save file. Doing so will place you back into the world just before the point of no return, but with the entire lunar base now altered by a mysterious transmission. This mode is the primary vehicle for everything covered in this **pragmata endings guide**.

## The Road to the Secret Ending: Unknown Signal Missions

Unlocking the secret ending is not as simple as beating the boss a second time. You must fulfill a series of rigorous requirements within the Unknown Signal mode. The core of this process involves completing 10 specific "Signal Missions" that appear across various sectors.

These missions are located at the various pods scattered throughout the game. As you approach them, the requirements for unlocking the specific mission will pop up. Common requirements include:
1. **100% Sector Exploration**: Finding all data logs and collectibles in a specific area.
2. **Enhanced Boss Takedowns**: Defeating upgraded versions of early-game bosses.
3. **Timed Challenges**: Navigating through hazardous zones within a strict time limit.

It is highly recommended to max out your primary weapons and Diana’s resonance abilities before attempting the final two missions.

## How to Unlock the Secret Ending (Step-by-Step)

Once you have progressed through the 10 Unknown Signal missions, follow these exact steps to trigger the secret ending. Many players miss the final requirement, which results in simply seeing the standard ending again despite the increased difficulty.

1. **Obtain the Secret Mod**: This is the "final prize" earned after finishing the 10th Unknown Signal mission. It is often referred to as the "Catalyst Mod" in the community.
2. **Equip the Mod**: Go to your equipment menu or the Shelter upgrade station and ensure the Secret Mod is active in your primary slot.
3. **Return to Sector 6**: Take the tram back to the final area of the game.
4. **Defeat the Enhanced Final Boss**: The fight will be significantly harder than your first encounter. The boss possesses new attack patterns and higher health pools.
5. **Watch the Credits**: Do not skip any scenes. After the standard credits, a new story snippet and cinematic will play, officially completing the **pragmata endings guide** requirements.

> **Warning:** Attempting the final boss in Unknown Signal mode without Level 5 equipment is extremely difficult and is not recommended for a first-time post-game run.

## FAQ

**Q: Is there more than one secret ending in Pragmata?**

A: Currently, there is only one secret ending, which is unlocked via the Unknown Signal mode. However, the "story snippet" you receive can vary slightly based on your total completion percentage of the lunar sectors. Following a **pragmata endings guide** ensures you see the most complete version of this scene.

**Q: Do I have to play the entire game again in Unknown Signal mode?**

A: No. Unknown Signal mode allows you to load your complete save data, which places you near the end of the game with all your previous upgrades. You then use the tram system to travel between sectors to complete the 10 new missions.

**Q: What is the "The Right Man for the Job" trophy?**

A: This is a gold trophy/achievement earned for completing the Unknown Signal mode and successfully triggering the secret ending by defeating the enhanced final boss with the Secret Mod equipped.

**Q: Can I unlock the secret ending on my first playthrough?**

A: No. The "Unknown Signal" mode only appears on the title screen after you have seen the standard credits at least once and saved your game data. This ensures players experience the natural progression of the story before tackling the high-difficulty post-game content.
