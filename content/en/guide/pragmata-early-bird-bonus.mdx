## How to Redeem the Pragmata Early Bird Bonus

Redeeming your **pragmata early bird bonus** depends on your platform of choice. Capcom has streamlined the process for 2026, ensuring that digital purchases automatically link the rewards to your account. However, if you are purchasing a physical copy, follow the steps below to ensure you don't miss out.

### For PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S
1. Navigate to the **Store** on your console dashboard.
2. Select **Redeem Code** from the menu options.
3. Enter the 12-digit (PS5) or 25-character (Xbox) code found on your receipt or insert.
4. Download the "Early Bird Pack" from the manage game content menu.
5. Launch Pragmata and check your "Shelter" storage box to equip items.

### For PC (Steam/Epic Games Store)
1. Open your game client and go to the **Library**.
2. Click on **Add a Game** in the bottom left corner (Steam) or your profile icon (Epic).
3. Select **Activate a Product** and enter your digital key.
4. The **pragmata early bird bonus** will appear as DLC in your game properties.
5. Ensure the DLC checkbox is ticked before launching the game.

> **Warning:** Early bird codes are typically region-locked. Ensure your game disc region matches your store account region to avoid redemption errors.
