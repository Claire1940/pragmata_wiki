## Gameplay Mechanics and Mission Objectives

The demo follows Hugh Williams and Diana as they attempt to restore power to the solar plant to contact Earth. This journey introduces several layers of gameplay that differentiate Pragmata from traditional third-person shooters.

### The Hacking Matrix
A core component of the gameplay is the hacking system. To make enemies vulnerable to gunfire, you must reach green EXE nodes within a hacking matrix. This requires holding the interact button (typically the mouse button or controller trigger) while navigating a cursor across a grid. 

> **💡 Tip:** Look for yellow hacking nodes during the puzzle phase. Passing through these can temporarily boost your damage output or stagger enemies more effectively.

### Resource Gathering: Lunum
While exploring, you will encounter a mineral unique to the moon known as **Lunum**. This resource is refined into Luna Filament, which is essential for upgrading your gear and suit integrity at the shelter. While the demo only gives a glimpse of the upgrade system, collecting Lunum is vital for players who want to see how the resource economy functions.

### Combat and Tools
You start with a standard firearm but quickly unlock the **Shockwave Gun**. This tool is essential for frontal suppression, offering powerful close-range blasts that stagger heavy robotic units.

## Technical Performance Analysis

Testing on 2026 mid-range hardware shows that Pragmata is well-optimized for closed-in environments. On an **RTX 5060 Ti 16GB**, players can expect over 100 FPS at 1440p with DLSS set to Balanced. 

### Ray Tracing: On vs. Off
The visual difference when enabling Ray Tracing is most noticeable in the glass-heavy environments of the solar power plant. 
*   **RT On:** Provides detailed reflections of the player character and the surrounding room. Indirect lighting appears more natural, filling dark corners with bounced light.
*   **RT Off:** Uses standard screen-space reflections. While the game still looks excellent, the character will no longer appear in mirror-like surfaces, and some "ghosting" may occur during fast movement.

> **⚠️ Warning:** If you are using an 8GB VRAM card (like the older 5060 Ti 8GB variant), enabling Ray Tracing at 1440p may cause texture swapping or frame time spikes. Stick to 1080p if your VRAM is limited.

## Mastering the Solar Power Plant Mission

To get the most out of your time in the demo, follow these steps to navigate the solar power plant efficiently:

1.  **Follow Diana's Lead:** Use the C key frequently. Diana will point out the three locks required to progress.
2.  **Manage Your Thrusters:** Jumping and dodging share the same gauge. If you hover for too long, you won't be able to dodge an incoming projectile.
3.  **Prioritize Staggers:** Use the Shockwave Gun on larger enemies to open them up for a hack. A hacked enemy is a dead enemy.
4.  **Check Every Corner:** Lunum is often hidden behind breakable crates or in high alcoves reachable only by hovering.
