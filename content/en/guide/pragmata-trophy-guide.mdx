## Combat and Skill Mastery

The combat in *Pragmata* rewards players who utilize hacking nodes and environmental hazards. To secure the Platinum, you must master both Hugh’s firearms and Diana’s support abilities.

### Advanced Combat Strategies
For the **IT'S OVER 6000!** trophy, you must deal 6,000 gunshot damage in a single second. The most effective way to achieve this is by using the **Charge Piercer**. Wait until you are in Training Sim 7, where multiple bots line up in a narrow corridor. A fully charged shot through three or more enemies will easily clear the damage threshold.

Another tricky combat trophy is **Courage in Desperation**. During your trek through the Lunum Mines, the LunaDigger will ambush you multiple times. To earn this, you must deal enough damage to "repel" it during these early encounters. If you simply run away and wait for the final boss fight at the end of the level, the trophy will not trigger.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Sweeper Bot (required for **You're Not Getting Away That Easy**) is very skittish. If you spot one in the Mass Production Array, use a **Stasis Net** immediately to prevent it from disappearing.

## Exploration and Collectibles

Exploration is the heart of the *Pragmata* experience. You will spend a significant amount of time collecting **Read Earth Memories (REMs)** and interacting with Diana at the Shelter to increase your bond and unlock high-tier gear.

### Finding Mini Cabins and Holo-Walls
Every sector contains three hidden **Mini Cabins**. These are small, glowing collectibles that must be shot to be registered. Finding all of them across every sector unlocks the **Mini-Hunter Supreme** trophy. Additionally, keep an eye out for flickering air—these are **Holo-Walls**. Walking through or shooting these reveals hidden rooms containing rare REMs and Pure Lunum.

### The Shelter and Stamp Club
To unlock the **Blackout!** trophy, you must complete a full Cabin Stamp Board. You earn stamps by:
1. Giving Diana REMs found in the world.
2. Completing Training Simulations.
3. Opening Safe Boxes using Hacking Nodes.

Completing these boards rewards you with **Red Gate Keys**, which are required for the **Red-Zoned and Loving It** trophy. These keys open high-difficulty combat arenas found within the main sectors, providing the final collectibles needed for 100% sector progress.

## Equipment and Post-Game Content

Once the credits roll, your journey is far from over. You will unlock **Lunatic Difficulty** and the **Hidden Chamber**, which contains the game's most difficult challenges.

*   **Lunar Supremacy:** This requires a full playthrough on Lunatic. In this mode, enemies deal significantly more damage, and Hugh’s health regeneration is slowed. Prioritize upgrading your armor and Diana’s healing abilities before attempting this.
*   **The Right Man for the Job:** This trophy requires you to complete the "Unknown Signal" mission string. These missions are only available after you have reached 100% progress in every sector and defeated the "Plus" versions of the campaign bosses.
*   **Can't Stop, Won't Stop:** You must print every weapon, hacking node, and ability. This will require a large amount of **Lunafilaments**, which are best farmed in the later Training Sims.

## FAQ

**Q: Does this Pragmata trophy guide cover the PlayStation 5 and Xbox versions?**

A: Yes, the requirements for trophies on PlayStation 5 and achievements on Xbox Series X|S are identical. The only difference is the presence of a Platinum trophy on the PlayStation platform.

**Q: Can I get all trophies in a single playthrough?**

A: No. You must complete the game at least once to unlock the Lunatic difficulty setting, which is required for the **Lunar Supremacy** trophy.

**Q: Are there any missable collectibles in the Lunum Mines?**

A: While no collectibles are permanently missable due to chapter select, the **Courage in Desperation** trophy is easily skipped if you don't aggressively attack the LunaDigger during its initial ambushes.

**Q: How do I unlock more Training Simulations?**

A: Training Simulations are unlocked by progressing through the story and reaching specific Shelter levels. Ensure you are giving Diana REMs and upgrading the Shelter's core to access all 10 simulations required for **Master of the Simulator**._
