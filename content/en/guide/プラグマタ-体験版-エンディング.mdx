Capcom's long-awaited sci-fi adventure has finally provided a glimpse into its lunar mystery through the *Pragmata* Sketchbook demo. While many players are satisfied with simply reaching the credits, the real depth of this vertical slice lies in the hunt for every **プラグマタ 体験版 エンディング** (Pragmata demo ending) variation. To fully master the demo, players must unlock eight unique secret illustrations drawn by the companion character, Diana. These rewards are not just for show; they serve as a testament to your mastery of the game’s combat, exploration, and speed mechanics.

Achieving a specific **プラグマタ 体験版 エンディング** requires more than just luck. The game tracks your performance across various metrics, including damage taken, total playtime, and how effectively you utilize Diana’s support abilities versus your own arsenal. ## The 8 Ending Illustrations: Unlock Conditions

Unlocking all eight illustrations requires multiple playthroughs. Some conditions, such as the Pacifist run and the Crowd Control challenge, require fundamentally different playstyles that cannot be achieved in a single sitting. The following table breaks down the specific criteria for each reward displayed on the final result screen.

| Illustration No. | Condition Name | Specific Requirements to Achieve |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **No. 1** | **Pacifist Support** | Use only Diana's abilities. Do not fire your gun except for the mandatory first shot. |
| **No. 2** | **Elite Encounter** | Trigger and defeat the high-level Orange/Red robots in the final elevator. |
| **No. 3** | **Completionist** | Collect every Yellow Cube and open all Large Treasure Chests in the demo. |
| **No. 4** | **Perfect Guard** | Complete the entire demo without taking a single point of damage. |
| **No. 5** | **Speed Demon** | Reach the final result screen in under **10 minutes** total playtime. |
| **No. 6** | **Crowd Control** | Use the Green Gun to bind two enemies together with a single shot. |
| **No. 7** | **Scavenger** | Collect over 80% of all general resources scattered throughout the facility. |
| **No. 8** | **Survivor** | Standard completion of the demo without meeting other special criteria. |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** For Illustration No. 1, you must fire one shot at the very beginning to trigger the initial cutscene. Immediately after, unequip your firearm to ensure you don't accidentally void the Pacifist run.

## How to Trigger the Secret Ending No. 2 (Elite Encounter)

The second illustration is widely considered the most difficult to obtain because it relies on a hidden "Variety Kill" counter during the elevator sequence leading to the final boss. Under normal circumstances, the elevator spawns standard drones and basic robots. However, by demonstrating mastery over your entire toolkit, you can force the spawn of rare, high-level Orange Robots.

To secure this **プラグマタ 体験版 エンディング** reward, follow this exact sequence in the elevator:

1.  **Eliminate Drones:** Use your standard pistol or rifle to quickly take down the flying units as they appear.
2.  **Disable the First Robot:** Instead of shooting, command Diana to use her hacking ability to neutralize the first ground-based robot.
3.  **Execute the Second Robot:** Switch to the **Green Gun** (Static/Control Gun) and use a tether shot to finish off the second standard robot.
4.  **Confirm the Spawn:** If done correctly, two high-level Orange Robots will warp in. Defeating them guarantees the No. 2 illustration.

## Mastering the Speedrun: Illustration No. 5

Clearing the demo in under 10 minutes is a daunting task that requires intimate knowledge of the lunar facility's layout. You must ignore non-essential combat and focus entirely on the "Emergency Lockdown" terminals and power cell puzzles.

| Objective | Required Action | Time-Saving Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Initial Breach** | Clear the first wave of intruders. | Use **Overdrive** immediately to wipe the room in seconds. |
| **Power Cell 1** | Reroute the top three protocols. | Sprint past small crates; do not stop to scavenge resources. |
| **Power Cell 2** | Manual reboot of the cell. | Ignore the rabbit-type enemies; their aggro range is short. |
| **Elevator Ride** | Clear intruders to keep moving. | Prioritize drones first, as they can stall the elevator's progress. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Once you descend into the maintenance shafts, you cannot return to the upper levels. Ensure you have met your time or collection goals before dropping down the final chute.

## Exploration and Resource Management (No. 3 & No. 7)

The lunar base is filled with environmental storytelling and hidden caches. To earn the **Completionist** reward (No. 3), you must locate every Yellow Cube. These cubes provide lore entries and are often hidden behind destructible walls or on high rafters that require Diana’s "Lift" ability to reach.

In contrast, the **Scavenger** ending (No. 7) focuses on volume rather than rarity. You need to pick up the vast majority of common crafting materials. While the HUD doesn't provide a percentage counter, a good rule of thumb is to clear every hallway before interacting with the next progression terminal.

## Combat Mastery with the Green Gun (No. 6)

The Green Gun is a unique tactical tool that fires tethers. While it can be used to stun enemies, Illustration No. 6 requires you to use it for "linking." By waiting for two enemies to align, you can fire a shot that tethers them together, causing them to collide or share damage.

The best place to achieve this is the second encounter area right after clearing the first emergency lockdown. Multiple robots spawn in a narrow corridor, making it the perfect opportunity to "Control" two units with a single precise shot.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I unlock multiple ending illustrations in a single playthrough?**

A: Yes, many conditions are compatible. For example, you can achieve a "Speedrun" (No. 5) while also maintaining a "Perfect Guard" (No. 4). However, you cannot combine "Pacifist" (No. 1) with "Crowd Control" (No. 6), as the latter requires using a specific weapon.

**Q: Does taking damage during the final boss fight void the No. 4 illustration?**

A: Yes. To secure the No. 4 **プラグマタ 体験版 エンディング** reward, you must maintain a zero-damage record from the moment the demo starts until the final result screen. Using Diana’s shield ability frequently is highly recommended for this challenge.

**Q: Where can I view the illustrations I have already unlocked?**

A: Currently, in the *Sketchbook* demo, illustrations are only displayed on the post-game summary screen. To see them again, you must either complete the demo under those conditions again or view them via your platform's screenshot gallery if you captured them manually.

**Q: Is the Orange Robot spawn in the elevator random?**

A: No, it is a deterministic spawn. By using three different methods to kill the first three enemies (Standard Gun, Diana Ability, and Green Gun), you trigger a "Variety Bonus" that forces the Elite Encounter to occur every time.
