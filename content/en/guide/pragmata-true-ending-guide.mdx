## Step 1: Completing the Initial Campaign

Before you can even think about the true ending, you must finish the main story path. This involves navigating the various sectors of the moon, protecting Diana from the robotic "Hostiles," and defeating the final boss of the standard campaign. Once the credits roll for the first time, do not turn off your console or exit to the main menu immediately.

Upon the conclusion of the credits, the game will present you with a pivotal choice. You will see two primary options:
1. **Start a New Game:** This resets your progress for a fresh run.
2. **Unknown Signal Save:** This is the path required for the true ending.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Choosing "New Game" without creating an "Unknown Signal" save may overwrite your current progress, forcing you to play through the entire campaign again to reach the endgame content.

Selecting the "Unknown Signal" option will load your current save data back into the game world, but with significant changes to the environment and mission availability. This state is essentially a "New Game Plus" variant that introduces the final narrative arc.

## Step 2: Understanding the Unknown Signal

The Unknown Signal is more than just a difficulty spike; it is a narrative bridge. Once you load this save, you will notice that the game world feels different. Communications from Earth become more erratic, and Diana’s abilities may show signs of further evolution.

| Feature | Standard Campaign | Unknown Signal Phase |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary Goal** | Escape the Lunar Base | Investigate the Signal Source |
| **Sector Access** | Linear Progression | Open Exploration |
| **Enemy Difficulty** | Scaled to Level | **Maximum Difficulty** |
| **Secret Areas** | Locked/Hidden | **Hidden Chamber Unlocked** |

During this phase, your map will indicate a new frequency. However, you cannot simply fly to the source of the signal. The game requires you to prove your mastery over the existing sectors before the final path reveals itself.

## Step 3: Achieving Full Sector Progress

One of the most common hurdles players face when using a **pragmata true ending guide** is the requirement for "Full Sector Progress." The Hidden Chamber—the final area of the game—is locked behind a completion wall. You must revisit the previous sectors (The Industrial Zone, The Residential Hub, and The Research Labs) and complete all remaining objectives.

To achieve 100% completion in each sector, you generally need to:
*   Collect all data logs and lore fragments.
*   Complete all "Stability Missions" (side quests).
*   Find all hidden equipment upgrades for the protagonist's suit.
*   Clear out any remaining Hostile nests.

## Step 4: Entering the Hidden Chamber

Once the game detects that you have achieved full progress across all sectors, a notification will appear stating that the "Unknown Signal" has been localized. A new sector, the **Hidden Chamber**, will appear on your orbital map.

The Hidden Chamber is a specialized zone that contains the most challenging combat encounters in [Pragmata](https://www.capcom.com/pragmata/). This area is designed to test your mastery of the game's mechanics, specifically the synergy between the protagonist’s heavy weaponry and Diana’s support powers.

### Hidden Chamber Mission Structure
The missions within this sector are narrative-heavy. Unlike the standard missions, these focus on the history of the lunar colony and the origin of the "Pragmata" phenomenon. 
1. **The Descent:** A high-intensity combat gauntlet.
2. **The Memory Core:** A puzzle-solving segment involving Diana's hacking abilities.
3. **The Final Echo:** A boss fight against a mirror version of the protagonist.

> **💡 Tip:** Ensure your suit's "Quantum Shield" is fully upgraded before entering the Hidden Chamber. The enemies here deal significantly more chip damage through standard blocks.

## Step 5: The Epilogue and True Ending

Completing the missions in the Hidden Chamber triggers a series of new cutscenes that were not present in the original ending. These scenes provide the necessary context to understand the protagonist's true identity and Diana's purpose on the moon.

The **pragmata true ending guide** concludes with the "Epilogue" sequence. After the final boss of the Hidden Chamber is defeated, a new interaction prompt will appear near the central terminal. Activating this will trigger the true final cinematic. This ending is notably longer and offers a more optimistic resolution than the standard "Escape" ending.

| Ending Type | Narrative Outcome | Post-Credits Scene |

## Combat Strategies for the True Ending Path

The path to the true ending is fraught with "Alpha-Class" Hostiles. These enemies have regenerative armor that can only be broken using specific ammo types.

*   **Use the Railgun for Armor:** The Railgun is the most effective tool for stripping the outer plating of Hidden Chamber enemies.
*   **Diana's Pulse Burst:** Coordinate with Diana to use her Pulse Burst when enemies are grouped. This creates a momentary stun window that is vital for survival.
*   **Resource Management:** In the Unknown Signal phase, health drops are significantly rarer. Focus on "Perfect Dodges" to build your special meter without taking damage.

As you progress through the Hidden Chamber, you will unlock the "Origin Suit," which provides the highest defense stats in the game. This suit is almost mandatory for the final encounter of the true ending path.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the true ending on my first playthrough?**

A: No. The **pragmata true ending guide** requires you to finish the game once to unlock the "Unknown Signal" save state. The Hidden Chamber and the epilogue are exclusive to the post-game content.

**Q: What happens if I miss a collectible in the first run?**

A: You can still collect it during the Unknown Signal phase. In fact, you must collect all remaining items to reach 100% sector progress, which is a requirement for unlocking the Hidden Chamber missions.

**Q: Is the true ending boss harder than the final boss of the main story?**

A: Yes, the final encounter in the Hidden Chamber features more complex attack patterns and a much larger health pool. It is recommended to have your gear at the maximum level before initiating this fight.

**Q: Do I need to play on a specific difficulty to see the true ending?**

A: No, the true ending is accessible on all difficulty settings, provided you complete the Unknown Signal requirements and achieve full sector progress. However, the Hidden Chamber naturally scales the difficulty higher regardless of your initial setting.
