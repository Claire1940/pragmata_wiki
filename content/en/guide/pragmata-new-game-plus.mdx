To access the advanced features of the endgame, players must first complete the primary campaign on any difficulty setting. The transition to the moon's more dangerous "Second Cycle" involves several key changes to the game world. Enemy encounters are reshuffled, and the IDUS protocols become significantly more aggressive, granting robots "controlled violence" permissions earlier in the narrative.

In **pragmata new game plus**, the moon itself seems to conspire against you. These foes require precise coordination between Hugh’s shooting and Diana’s hacking.

The hacking puzzles, which utilize the D-pad on the controller, become more intense. This adds a layer of tension that turns every encounter into a high-stakes tactical puzzle.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The "Moonquake" events occur more frequently in NG+. These environmental hazards can damage Hugh's oxygen supply and disrupt Diana’s hacking connection, requiring immediate relocation.

## Combat Strategy: Mastering the Bullet Ballet

To succeed in **pragmata new game plus**, you must treat combat as a "bullet ballet." This means moving beyond simple evasion and actively engaging with the enemy's projectile patterns. Hugh's ability to create openings is directly tied to how well Diana can identify weaknesses.

In the higher difficulty tiers, enemies will often have "Shielded Cores" that are immune to standard gunfire. You must protect Diana while she solves the hacking puzzle to drop these shields. The D-pad inputs must be entered perfectly; a single mistake can reset the hack, leaving you vulnerable to the IDUS attack protocols.

## FAQ

**Q: Does Pragmata New Game Plus have a level cap?**

A: While there is no hard cap discovered in the launch window, most players find that Hugh and Diana's stats reach diminishing returns around level 99. However, gear upgrades continue to provide significant power increases well into the third and fourth cycles.

**Q: Can I change my character's appearance in New Game Plus?**

A: Yes, once you unlock **pragmata new game plus**, the "Shelter" hub allows you to swap between any unlocked cosmetic suits for both Hugh and Diana at any time.

**Q: Are there any missable items that I can only get in the first playthrough?**

A: Most items can be obtained in either run, but certain "First Contact" achievements and specific lore logs are easier to find when the enemy aggression is lower during your initial playthrough.

**Q: How do the D-pad hacking puzzles change in the endgame?**

A: In the second cycle and beyond, the puzzles introduce "Decoy Nodes." These are incorrect inputs that appear on the screen to distract you. You must ignore the red prompts and only follow the blue sequence to successfully hack the IDUS units.
