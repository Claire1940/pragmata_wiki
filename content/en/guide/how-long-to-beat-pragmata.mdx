## Factors Influencing Your Playtime

Several core gameplay pillars influence **how long to beat pragmata**. Unlike traditional linear shooters, the game incorporates light RPG elements and environmental puzzles that can slow down progress if you aren't prepared.

### Hacking and Environmental Puzzles
A significant portion of the gameplay involves Diana, the young girl who accompanies the protagonist. Her hacking abilities are required to disable emergency lockdowns and open sealed areas. If you are unfamiliar with the puzzle logic, these sections can add several hours to your total playtime over the course of the game.

### Combat with "The Lost"
The primary antagonists, known as The Lost, appear in various forms throughout the lunar base. Combat is tactical; simply "running and gunning" often leads to death and repeated checkpoints. Players who master the aggressive counter-system will find they can clear rooms much faster than those who play defensively.

| Encounter Type | Average Duration | Difficulty Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Minor Lost Patrols** | **2–3 Minutes** | Low impact; mostly resource gathering. |
| **Power Terminal Bosses** | **10–15 Minutes** | High impact; requires pattern recognition. |
| **Emergency Lockdown Events** | **5–8 Minutes** | Medium impact; timed survival challenges. |

## The Completionist Path: Achieving 100%

If your goal isn't just to see the credits but to conquer every aspect of the game, your quest for **how long to beat pragmata** will extend significantly. The completionist run involves several layers of activity beyond the main questline.

1. **Lunar Log Collection**: There are dozens of audio logs and data pads scattered across the moon. These provide essential lore about the fall of the colony.
2. **Diana's Skill Tree**: Fully upgrading Diana's hacking and support abilities requires specific "Energy Levels" found in hidden caches.
3. **Weapon Mastery**: Unlocking every attachment for your protagonist's modular rifle requires completing specific combat trials.
4. **Secret Bosses**: Several high-level "Lost" entities reside in optional areas of the power plant and residential sectors.

> **Warning:** Some collectibles are located in "point of no return" areas. Always check your map for missing items before initiating a manual reboot of a major power plant control.

## Difficulty Settings and Their Impact

Pragmata offers three standard difficulty levels at launch, each affecting the health and aggression of enemies. While the puzzle complexity remains the same, the time spent in combat varies wildly between these settings.

*   **Explorer (Easy)**: Enemies are less aggressive, and Diana's hacking cooldowns are shorter. Ideal for those who want to experience the story in under 12 hours.
*   **Survivor (Normal)**: The balanced experience. Expect a playtime of 15–18 hours.
*   **Vanguard (Hard)**: Enemies have significantly more health and deal massive damage. This mode can easily push the main story length to 20+ hours due to the necessity of stealth and careful resource management.
