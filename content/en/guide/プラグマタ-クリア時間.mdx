## Factors Influencing Playtime

Several gameplay elements directly impact the **プラグマタ クリア時間**. Unlike a traditional shooter, Pragmata requires a balance of tactical thinking and reflexes.

### Combat and Hacking Synergy
The game introduces a unique "Shooting x Hacking" system. While shooting handles the immediate threats, hacking is required to disable shields or manipulate the environment. Players who struggle with the puzzle-like nature of the hacking nodes may find their clear time extending as they navigate the lunar data centers.

### Difficulty Levels
Pragmata offers multiple difficulty settings that change enemy behavior and damage output.
*   **Easy/Normal:** Standard experience where resources are plentiful.
*   **Hard:** Enemies are more aggressive; hacking nodes have tighter time limits.
*   **Lunatic:** Unlocked after the first clear. This mode is mandatory for the Platinum trophy and requires a second full playthrough, significantly increasing the total volume.

### Exploration and Collectibles
The lunar surface is littered with "Earth Memories" and "Mini Cabins." These collectibles provide world-building lore and upgrade materials for your equipment.

| Collectible Type | Purpose | Impact on Playtime |
