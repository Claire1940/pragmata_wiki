## Understanding How Many Endings Does Pragmata Have

When evaluating **how many endings does Pragmata have**, it is important to distinguish between the standard credit roll and the "Extended" or "Secret" conclusion. Strictly speaking, the game features one primary narrative path, but it offers a significant "Story Snippet" or secret epilogue that functions as a second ending for dedicated players.

The standard ending occurs after your first successful defeat of the final boss in Sector 6. However, once the credits roll, the game does not allow you to continue in that same world state. Instead, you are prompted to load your save right before the final encounter or transition into specialized post-game modes. To see the "complete" vision of the story, you must engage with the Unknown Signal mode.

## The Road to the Secret Ending: Unknown Signal Mode

To truly answer the question of **how many endings does Pragmata have**, one must look at the Unknown Signal missions. This is a dedicated post-game mode that unlocks after you have finished the main campaign. By selecting this mode from your completed save data, you are placed back just before the final boss, but with access to a brand-new series of challenges.

The Unknown Signal consists of 10 high-difficulty missions. These missions are not just combat trials; they are the gateway to the "Secret Mod," which is the key item required to trigger the game's final narrative revelation.

### Unlocking the Unknown Signal Missions

Progressing through these missions requires more than just combat skill. You will need to interact with the game's various sectors and fulfill specific completion criteria.

1.  **Beat the First Mission**: This unlocks the tram system, allowing you to backtrack to previous sectors.
2.  **100% Sector Completion**: Many missions are locked until you have fully explored specific areas.
3.  **Enhanced Bosses**: You will be required to defeat "Enhanced" versions of earlier bosses to prove your mastery.
4.  **Shelter Upgrades**: Returning to the shelter with the Level 5 Shelter Key (obtained from Big Cabin) allows for final equipment upgrades.

> **💡 Tip:** Collectibles like Mini Cabin Figures do not count toward sector percentage, but you should collect them anyway for the associated trophy and completionist rewards.

## Steps to Unlock the Final Story Snippet

Once you have navigated the complexities of the post-game, you are ready to view the final piece of the puzzle. This is the closest the game comes to a "True Ending." If you are wondering **how many endings does Pragmata have** in terms of unique cinematic content, this secret snippet is the definitive second piece.

Follow these steps to ensure you don't miss it:

## New Game Plus vs. Lunatic Difficulty

After witnessing the extended ending, players often ask **how many endings does Pragmata have** across different difficulty levels. While the story remains the same on higher difficulties, the gameplay loop changes significantly. You have two main options for your subsequent playthroughs: New Game Plus (NG+) and Lunatic Difficulty.

### New Game Plus: The Power Trip
NG+ allows you to carry over all your weapon upgrades, suit enhancements, and storage capacity. It is designed as a "casual power trip" where you can breeze through early sectors with late-game gear. You can even skip the intro and start right at the shelter.

### Lunatic Difficulty: The Ultimate Challenge
Lunatic mode is a fresh start with a brutal twist. You do not carry over your power, but you gain the ability to use **Lunafilament** and **Purelumen** to print high-level items much earlier than usual. This mode is required for those seeking 100% trophy/achievement completion.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Beating the game on Lunatic is a separate requirement from the Secret Ending. You can achieve the Secret Ending on any difficulty as long as the Secret Mod is equipped.

## Post-Game Rewards and Economy

The endgame of Pragmata introduces a new economy to help you prepare for the final challenges. If you find yourself struggling with the final boss during the Secret Ending run, you can farm resources in the previous sectors.

*   **Cabin Coins**: Once your stamp book is complete, spare coins can be traded for 500 Lunafilament each.
*   **Lunafilament**: Used for high-end printing and upgrading weapons to Level 5.
*   **Purelumen**: A rare resource found in Unknown Signal missions, necessary for the strongest suit mods.

## FAQ

**Q: How many endings does Pragmata have in total?**

A: Pragmata has one main ending and one "Extended" ending (often called the Secret Ending). The extended version is triggered by defeating the final boss a second time while having the Secret Mod equipped after completing the Unknown Signal missions.

**Q: Can I get the secret ending in my first playthrough?**

A: No. The Secret Mod required for the extended story snippet is only obtainable through the post-game "Unknown Signal" mode, which unlocks after you have already completed the story once.

**Q: Does New Game Plus change the story or endings?**

A: New Game Plus does not change the narrative or the number of endings. It is primarily a gameplay mode that allows you to keep your upgrades. To see the secret story snippet, you must fulfill the Unknown Signal requirements regardless of whether you are in a standard save or NG+.

**Q: What is the best order to complete the post-game content?**

A: The most efficient order is to complete the story, enter Unknown Signal mode, finish all 10 missions while 100%ing sectors, defeat the final boss with the Secret Mod, and then finally start a New Game Plus or Lunatic run with all your rewards carried over.
