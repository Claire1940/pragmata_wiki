## Mastering the プラグマタ体験版 エンディング: All 8 Illustrations

To unlock the full gallery of Diana’s sketches, players must complete the demo under specific conditions. Some of these requirements are mutually exclusive, meaning a minimum of three to four playthroughs is generally required to see everything the demo has to offer. The game tracks metrics such as total damage taken, time elapsed, and specific weapon usage to determine which illustration appears during the final summary.

| Illustration No. | Condition Name | Achievement Criteria |

> **💡 Tip:** For Illustration No. 1, you must fire exactly one shot at the very beginning to trigger the first cutscene. Immediately after, unequip your firearm and rely solely on Diana’s hacking and support skills to progress.

## The Secret of Ending No. 2: The Elite Elevator Sequence

Illustration No. 2 is widely considered the rarest **プラグマタ体験版 エンディング** reward because it requires a specific "Variety Kill" sequence during the final elevator ascent to the boss arena. Normally, the elevator spawns standard drones and bipedal robots. However, if you perform a specific set of actions, the game rewards you by spawning elite Orange Robots.

Follow these steps precisely during the elevator ride:
1.  **Eliminate the Drones:** Use your standard rifle or pistol to quickly clear the flying units as they appear.
2.  **Command Diana:** Target the first ground robot and use Diana’s hack ability to disable it completely.
3.  **The Green Gun Finish:** Switch to the Green Gun (Static/Control) and use it to deliver the finishing blow to the second standard robot.
4.  **The Elite Spawn:** If performed correctly, two high-level Orange Robots will spawn. Defeating them guarantees the No. 2 illustration at the end of the run.

Achieving the No. 5 **プラグマタ体験版 エンディング** requires a "Speed Demon" approach. This means ignoring non-essential combat and mastering the layout of the lunar facility. The most significant time-wasters are the manual restarts for the power cells and the "Emergency Lockdown" sequences.

| Segment | Objective | Time-Saving Tactic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Initial Breach** | Clear the first wave | Activate **Overdrive** immediately to wipe the room in seconds. |
| **Power Cell 1** | Reroute protocols | Ignore side corridors; sprint directly to the three main terminals. |
| **Power Cell 2** | Manual restart | The rabbit-type enemies are slow; dash past them instead of fighting. |
| **Final Elevator** | Clear intruders | Prioritize drones first to prevent them from stalling the elevator's ascent. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Once you drop into the lower maintenance shafts to reach the power terminals, you cannot return to the upper levels. Ensure you have gathered any necessary resources before making the jump if you are attempting a combined run.

## Exploration and Scavenging: Finding Every Yellow Cube

For players aiming for the "Completionist" (No. 3) or "Scavenger" (No. 7) results, exploration is key. The lunar base is filled with verticality, often requiring Hugh to boost Diana up to high ledges or use her "Lift" ability to reach hidden rafters. Yellow Cubes contain vital lore and high-grade materials, while large chests often hide behind destructible walls.

To secure the No. 7 **プラグマタ体験版 エンディング**, you must be thorough. While the HUD doesn't provide a live counter, you should aim to pick up every glowing material drop in the hallways. Breaking crates and barrels is essential, as these contribute significantly to your total resource count.

## Combat Mastery: The Pacifist and Cloud Control Runs

The combat in *Pragmata* is designed around the partnership between Hugh and Diana. In the "Cloud Control" (No. 6) run, you must demonstrate mastery of the Green Gun. This weapon fires a tether that can link two enemies together, causing them to collide or share damage. To trigger this condition, wait until two robots are in close proximity—ideally during the second major encounter after the first lockdown—and fire a single shot that connects both.

The Pacifist run (No. 1) is perhaps the most unique way to experience the **プラグマタ体験版 エンディング**. It forces you to play as a protector, using Hugh’s physical bulk to shield Diana while she performs environmental hacks to neutralize threats. You will need to manage Diana’s energy levels carefully, as her support abilities have cooldowns that can leave you vulnerable if mismanaged.

For more technical details on weapon scaling and frame data, visit the [official Pragmata website](https://www.capcom-games.com/pragmata/) to stay updated on the latest mechanical changes for the retail release.

## Post-Clear Content and the Doodle Suit Reward

Beyond the eight illustrations, clearing the demo for the first time unlocks the "Doodle Suit" (らくがきスーツ) for Hugh. This cosmetic item features various drawings—presumably by Diana—all over Hugh’s EVA suit. It adds a touch of levity to the otherwise somber atmosphere of the lunar base.

Additionally, the demo hints at the deep bond between Hugh (voiced by Mio Tanaka) and Diana (voiced by Nao Toyama). Players who pay close attention to their idle dialogue during exploration will find clues about their past and their ultimate goal of returning to Earth. The "Escape Hatch" mechanic, though limited in the demo, suggests a "Return to Base" loop in the full game where players can interact with Diana in a safe zone to upgrade gear and view unlocked sketches.

## FAQ

**Q: Is it possible to unlock multiple プラグマタ体験版 エンディング illustrations in a single run?**

A: Yes, certain conditions can overlap. For example, you can achieve a "Perfect Guard" (No. 4) while also hitting the "Speed Demon" (No. 5) time requirement. However, you cannot combine the "Pacifist" (No. 1) and "Cloud Control" (No. 6) conditions in the same playthrough as they require contradictory weapon usage.

**Q: Does taking damage from environmental hazards void the No. 4 illustration?**

A: Yes, any reduction in your health bar from the start of the demo until the final boss defeat will disqualify you from the "Perfect Guard" reward. It is highly recommended to use Diana’s shield ability whenever moving through areas with steam vents or electrical discharges.

**Q: Where can I view the illustrations I have already unlocked?**

A: Currently, in the Sketchbook demo, the illustrations are only displayed on the final results screen after the credits. To see them again, you must either complete the demo under those conditions again or use your console/PC’s built-in screenshot function to save them to your gallery.

**Q: How do I unlock the Doodle Suit for Hugh?**

A: The Doodle Suit is rewarded automatically after your first successful completion of the demo, regardless of which **プラグマタ体験版 エンディング** illustration you receive. You can equip it from the main menu before starting a new game.
