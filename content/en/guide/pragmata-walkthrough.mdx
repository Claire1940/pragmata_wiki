## Mission Objectives: Restoring the Power

The primary goal of the opening sequence is to restore functionality to a locked-down facility. This requires navigating through several floors, rebooting terminals, and managing the "EDS" (Emergency Defense System) that has labeled you as an intruder.

| Objective Phase | Key Task | Primary Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1** | Disable Emergency Lockdown | Protect Diana during the hack |
| **Phase 2** | Power Terminal Reboot | Locate 3 sub-terminals via Scanning |
| **Phase 3** | Elevator Activation | Hack the lift while holding off walkers |
| **Phase 4** | Sector Guard Battle | Deplete the EDS boss health bar |

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep Diana within your line of sight. While she is capable of hacking advanced systems, she is vulnerable to bot attacks and requires your protection to finish her sequences.

## Navigating the Lunar Facility

Upon entering the facility, your path is immediately blocked by a security lockdown. To progress, you must utilize Diana’s hacking abilities. Approach the locked bulkhead and interact with it to initiate the sequence. During this time, rogue maintenance bots will attempt to interrupt.

### Using the Scanner
If you find yourself lost in the maze-like corridors, use the **Scanning** tool. This highlights interactive terminals and provides a waypoint to the next objective. The facility is vertically oriented, meaning you will often need to drop down into maintenance shafts or find elevators to reach the "Top Floor" where the main power terminal resides.

### Terminal Interaction
Throughout the facility, you will find terminals that offer temporary combat boosts. Interacting with these allows your weapons to hit harder and cycle through ammunition faster.

| Terminal Type | Effect | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| **Combat Terminal** | Increases damage output and reload speed | 60 Seconds |
| **Reboot Terminal** | Restores 10% of facility power | Permanent |
| **Scanning Array** | Highlights all nearby enemies and loot | 30 Seconds |

## Combat Mechanics and Enemy Types

Combat in Pragmata is a blend of long-range gunplay and tactical positioning. You are equipped with a versatile rifle, but the real power comes from the "Overdrive" system and environmental interactions.

### Enemy Breakdown
The bots you encounter are not standard service units; they have been reprogrammed to neutralize "unregistered guests."

*   **Walkers:** Basic bipedal units. They are weak but often attack in swarms. Aim for the head or the glowing power core on their backs.
*   **The Rat:** A fast-moving, low-profile bot that scurries through vents. Use the scanner to track its movement before it leaps for a melee strike.
*   **EDS Sector Guard:** The first major boss. This unit is heavily armored and equipped with a multi-missile launcher.

> **Warning:** Do not attempt to engage the Sector Guard in an open field. Use the surrounding pillars for cover when you hear the missile lock-on alert.

## Boss Strategy: Defeating the Sector Guard

The Sector Guard (EDS) appears once the power levels reach 60%. This encounter is the climax of the initial **pragmata walkthrough** segment and requires a mix of defensive play and aggressive "Overdrive" usage.

1.  **Phase 1: Shield Depletion.** The Guard uses a frontal shield. Circle-strafe to the left or right to find openings in its armor.
2.  **Phase 2: Missile Barrage.** When the Guard kneels, it is preparing a missile volley. Look for the red tracking lasers on your suit. Roll just before impact to avoid splash damage.
3.  **Phase 3: Overdrive Protocol.** Once the Guard’s health drops to 30%, Diana will enable the Overdrive protocol. This significantly boosts your damage. This is your chance to "kick" the unit into a stunned state and finish the fight.

## Restoring Primary Power

After defeating the Sector Guard, you can finally access the main power terminal on the top floor. This sequence involves a manual reboot where you must hold the interaction button while Diana stabilizes the energy flow.

| Power Level | Event Trigger |
|---|---|
| **30%** | First wave of Walkers spawn |
| **60%** | Unauthorized Access Detected (Boss Spawn) |
| **100%** | Lockdown Disengaged / Mission Complete |

Once the energy levels reach 100%, the facility will return to a neutral state, and the emergency bulkheads will open, allowing you to proceed to the next area of the moon. ## FAQ

**Q: How do I use Diana to hack doors in the Pragmata walkthrough?**

A: To hack doors, approach the terminal and press the interaction prompt. You must then defend Diana from incoming bots until the progress bar reaches 100%. If she takes too much damage, the hack will reset.

**Q: What should I do if I get lost in the power plant?**

A: Use the scan function (typically mapped to the 'Q' key or D-pad). This will highlight your current objective with a waypoint and show the path to the next reboot terminal.

**Q: Can I upgrade my weapons during the mission?**

A: Yes, you can find Combat Terminals throughout the facility. These don't provide permanent upgrades in the demo, but they offer significant temporary buffs to your damage and reload speed, which are essential for the Sector Guard fight.

**Q: Is there a way to fast-travel between floors?**

A: There is no traditional fast travel in the early facility levels. You must use the central elevator system, which Diana can hack once the local emergency lockdown has been disabled.

**Q: What is the Overdrive protocol?**

A: Overdrive is a special combat state triggered by Diana during boss fights or heavy encounters. It increases your movement speed and damage, allowing you to perform powerful finishing moves on elite enemies like the Sector Guard.
