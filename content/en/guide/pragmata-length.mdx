## Understanding the Gameplay Loop

To understand why the **pragmata length** falls into the 12-15 hour range, one must look at the density of the mechanics. Pragmata is not a simple "button masher." It requires a unique mental load that Capcom enthusiasts have compared to "using two parts of your brain at the same time."

### The Hacking System
While Hugh handles the heavy weaponry and shooting, Diana performs real-time hacking. This plays out as a mini-game where you navigate a small maze on the screen to hit critical nodes. Successfully completing a hack allows Hugh to deal massive damage or open up weak spots on AI androids. This strategic layer means encounters take longer than in a standard shooter, as you cannot simply "brute force" your way through elite enemies.

### Platforming and Exploration
Unlike the claustrophobic corridors of *Resident Evil*, Pragmata features larger, more vertical environments. Hugh is equipped with a thruster for boost-jumping, allowing for significant verticality. 

*   **Lifts and Grapples:** Many levels require activating elevators or grappling to higher platforms.
*   **Scanning:** Players must frequently use a scanner to reveal interactive objects in the environment.
*   **Hidden Loot:** Chests are tucked away in sub-layers of the levels, rewarding those who stray from the main path.
