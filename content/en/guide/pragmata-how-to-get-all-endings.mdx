## The Three Primary Narrative Endings

In the full release of Pragmata, the narrative branches into three distinct paths. These are determined by your "Synchronization Rate" with Diana and your completion of specific side objectives before the final act.

| Ending Name | Tone | Primary Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Default Ending** | Bittersweet | Complete the game with standard exploration (under 100% Synergy). |
| **Abandonment Ending** | Tragic | Low Diana Synergy (under 40%) and failing multiple escort sequences. |
| **True Ending** | Triumphant | **100% Synchronization Rate**, all 20 Memory Fragments, and 3 specific side missions. |

To avoid the "Bad" or Abandonment ending, you must prioritize Diana's safety during combat encounters. If she takes excessive damage or if you fail to utilize her hacking abilities during key environmental puzzles, your synergy will drop, leading to a much colder conclusion to their journey.

## Pragmata How to Get All Endings: The True Ending Path

Unlocking the True Ending is the ultimate goal for most players. This path provides the most context regarding the state of Earth and the true nature of the lunar colony. To secure this, you must be meticulous in your exploration.

### 1. Achieve 100% Synchronization
Your bond with Diana is tracked via the Synchronization Gauge. You can increase this by:
*   Interacting with Diana during downtime at safe zones.
*   Successfully completing "Dual-Tech" combat finishers.
*   Allowing Diana to hack terminals without taking damage.
*   Giving her "Old Earth" artifacts found in the residential ruins.

### 2. Collect All 20 Missing Memory Fragments
These holographic data drives are the most critical collectibles in the game. They are often hidden behind vertical traversal puzzles or zero-gravity zones. 

> **💡 Tip:** Many Memory Fragments are inaccessible during your first visit to an area. You will often need to backtrack after acquiring the **Kinetic Grapple** or upgraded **Exosuit Thrusters** to reach high-altitude caches.

### 3. Complete Key Side Missions
There are three specific side missions that provide the necessary "Story Flags" for the True Ending:
*   **The Deep Space Relay Calibration:** Found in Sector 4.
*   **The Residential Ruins Search:** Locating the 3 Encrypted Logs.
*   **The Sector 7 Armory Hacking:** Requires a fully upgraded Hacking Module.

## Crucial Points of No Return

Pragmata features a semi-open world, but as you progress through the narrative, certain sectors will become permanently locked. If you miss a Memory Fragment or an encrypted log in these areas, you will be unable to achieve the True Ending in that playthrough.

| Chapter / Sector | Environmental Trigger | Consequence of Proceeding |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Chapter 3: Sector 4** | Activating the Comm Relay | Locks the "Old Earth" residential ruins. |
| **Chapter 6: Sector 7** | Boarding the Mag-Lev | Prevents backtracking to the early industrial zones. |
| **Chapter 9: Sector 9** | **Entering the Orbital Elevator** | The final point of no return for the entire game. |

Before you interact with the Orbital Elevator command pod in Sector 9, the game will present a warning prompt. This is your last chance to use the fast-travel system to return to earlier sectors and mop up any missing collectibles.

## Unlocking the 8 Special Ending Pictures

In addition to the narrative conclusions, completionists can unlock eight unique "Ending Pictures" in the game's gallery. These were originally introduced in the trial version/demo but remain a core part of the 100% completion criteria for the 2026 full release. These pictures represent specific gameplay achievements rather than story choices.

| Picture # | Achievement Condition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Pacifist Run:** Only use Diana's abilities (no gun use after the intro). |
| **2** | **Technical Kill:** Kill the final boss robots using three different weapon types. |
| **3** | **Hoarder:** Collect every yellow cube and big chest in a single run. |
| **4** | **Master Combatant:** Complete the final gauntlet without taking damage. |
| **5** | **Speedrunner:** Finish the final chapter in under 10 minutes. |
| **6** | **Crowd Control:** Stun two heavy robots with a single Static Gun (green gun) shot. |
| **7** | **Scavenger:** Collect a high volume of resources (approx. 80% of area total). |
| **8** | **Standard Completion:** Simply finish the final chapter normally. |

To get **Picture #2**, many players struggle with the specific trigger. During the elevator ride to the final encounter, you will face a drone and two robots. To guarantee the "Orange Robot" spawn and the subsequent picture unlock, you must kill the drone with your pistol, kill the first robot with Diana’s ability, and kill the second robot using the Static Gun’s environmental damage.

## Troubleshooting the "Bad" Ending

If you find yourself stuck in the Abandonment Ending loop, it is usually due to a low synergy score. In Pragmata, the game calculates your relationship with Diana based on "Story Flags" triggered in Chapters 3, 6, and 9. If you have rushed the game, you may need to restart from an earlier save or enter New Game Plus to fix your narrative trajectory.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Attempting to manually edit save files in the RE Engine to "force" a synergy score often results in file corruption. It is highly recommended to use the in-game "Chapter Select" (unlocked after the first completion) to fix missed objectives.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the True Ending on my first playthrough?**

A: Yes, it is entirely possible to get the True Ending on your first run, but you must be extremely thorough. You need to collect all 20 Memory Fragments and ensure your synergy with Diana is at 100% before entering the Orbital Elevator in Chapter 9.

**Q: What happens if I miss a Memory Fragment in an early sector?**

A: If you haven't passed the Point of No Return in Chapter 9, you can fast-travel back to previous sectors. However, some areas in Sector 4 become locked after Chapter 3. If you miss those, you will need to collect them in New Game Plus to reach 100% completion.

**Q: Do difficulty settings affect which endings I can get?**

A: No, you can unlock all narrative endings on any difficulty setting. However, certain cosmetic items, like the N7-Vanguard suit, require a completion on "Hardcore" difficulty.

**Q: How many endings are there in total?**

A: There are three main narrative endings (Default, Abandonment, True) and eight collectible gallery pictures that serve as "mini-endings" for completionists. To fully master **pragmata how to get all endings**, you should aim to unlock all three story conclusions and all eight gallery images.
