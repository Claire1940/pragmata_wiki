## Detailed Pragmata Demo Ending Requirements

Unlocking the more obscure endings requires a shift in how you approach the demo's combat and exploration. Unlike traditional demos that simply end after a boss fight, Pragmata tracks your efficiency and utility usage.

### 1. The Hacking Specialist (Pacifist)
One of the most challenging **pragmata demo ending requirements** involves the "Diana-only" run. In this mode, the player must refrain from using the protagonist’s primary firearms or melee strikes against the robotic husks. Instead, you must position yourself to allow Diana to hack environmental hazards and enemy units.

*   **Constraint:** Zero shots fired from the main rifle.
*   **Strategy:** Use the "Signal Command" to direct Diana toward terminals.
*   **Reward:** A unique dialogue sequence where Diana comments on the player's "restraint."

### 2. The Under 10-Minute Speedrun
The speedrun ending is a favorite for those who have mastered the lunar gravity mechanics. To meet these **pragmata demo ending requirements**, you must skip all optional encounters and use the jet-dash ability off-cooldown.

| Segment | Target Time | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Opening Hallway** | 0:45 | Dash through the initial debris |

| **The Gravity Well** | 6:00 | Ignore the side rooms; stay on the main path |
| **Final Encounter** | 9:30 | Focus purely on the core weak point |

### 3. The No-Damage Challenge
This is arguably the hardest requirement to fulfill in the 2026 demo build. Because the lunar environment features chip damage from environmental hazards and stray projectiles, maintaining a 100% health bar requires perfect positioning.

## Unlocking the Extra Weapon and Secret Dialogue

During the demo, many players have stumbled upon a meta-commentary dialogue where the characters acknowledge they are in a demo. This is triggered by attempting to move past the invisible barriers at the edge of the lunar colony three times in a row.

The protagonist will ask, "How the heck am I going to get back to Earth?" to which Diana responds that they can't because "this is just the demo." This interaction is a prerequisite for the "Arsenal" ending, as it triggers the spawn of a secret weapon crate near the final elevator.

### How to Obtain the Secret Weapon:
1.  Reach the final elevator area but do not enter.
2.  Backtrack to the "Boundary Zone" (the blue shimmering wall).
3.  Trigger the fourth-wall-breaking dialogue.
4.  Return to the elevator; a new crate will have appeared containing the **Gravity Disruptor**.
5.  Finish the demo using this weapon to secure the "Arsenal" ending.
