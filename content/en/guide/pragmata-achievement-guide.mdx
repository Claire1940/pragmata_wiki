## Core Gameplay & Combat Achievements

The foundation of your achievement run lies in mastering the synergy between Hugh, a human in a high-tech suit, and Diana, a young android with advanced hacking capabilities. Most combat-related achievements focus on utilizing specific weapon types and managing your resources effectively during intense encounters with rogue "Walkers" and "Sector Guards."

### Essential Combat Mechanics

To unlock high-tier combat trophies, you must master the thruster gauge. Dodging and jumping consume this energy, and running out during a boss fight can lead to a quick incapacitation. Additionally, players should focus on the "Critical Shot" mechanic, which allows for a powerful follow-up attack when an enemy is downed.

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep an eye on your ammo recharge rate. Weapons in Pragmata recharge slowly over time, so switching between the Shockwave Gun and the Stasis Net is vital for maintaining constant pressure on enemies.

## Hacking & Exploration Milestones

A significant portion of the **pragmata achievement guide** revolves around Diana’s hacking abilities. Throughout the solar power plant and the Cradle, you will encounter hacking matrices. Successfully navigating these mini-games while hitting specific "Nodes" is the key to unlocking several silver and gold trophies.

### Navigating the Hacking Matrix

Hacking is not just about reaching the end of a node; it is about efficiency. Green executable nodes are your primary targets, but yellow nodes provide temporary buffs that are essential for higher difficulty settings.

1.  **Identify the Matrix:** Move the cursor across the grid to find the optimal path.
2.  **Collect Yellow Nodes:** Passing through multiple nodes of the same type increases their effectiveness, such as boosting damage dealt to internal systems.
3.  **Execute the Hack:** Hold the reset or confirm button once the path is clear to trigger the effect.

| Node Type | Effect | Max Stacks |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Green (Executable)** | Completes the objective/unlocks doors | N/A |
| **Yellow (Disruptor)** | Temporarily increases damage dealt | 3 |
| **Blue (Shield)** | Boosts suit integrity during hacks | 2 |

## Boss Strategy: Defeating Edith & Sector Guards

The first major hurdle in your quest for the 100% completion is the encounter with Edith, the administrator of the sector guard protocol. This fight takes place at the top of the solar power plant and requires a mix of all learned mechanics.

### Phase 1: The Missile Barrage
Edith will fire Luna-made missiles that track your movement. Use your thrusters to dodge laterally. To earn the *Aegis* achievement, you must complete this phase without taking direct hull damage.

### Phase 2: Overdrive Protocol
When Diana’s hacking gauge is full, you can activate the **Overdrive Protocol**. This immobilizes the boss and deals massive internal damage. This is the perfect window to use the Shockwave Gun at point-blank range to maximize your DPS.

| Boss State | Recommended Weapon | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mobile/Ranged** | Primary Unit | Continuous chip damage while dodging missiles. |
| **Stunned/Immobilized** | **Shockwave Gun** | Close the distance for maximum stagger. |
| **Shielded** | Stasis Net | Break the electromagnetic barrier. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to hack the lift until the Sector Guard is fully neutralized. Attempting to interact with the terminal mid-fight will leave Hugh vulnerable to a critical strike.

## Collectibles and the Cradle Lore

To fully satisfy the requirements of a comprehensive **pragmata achievement guide**, players must hunt for hologram surveillance logs and text files scattered throughout the facility. These items provide context for the Delphi Corporation and the "Cradle" abnormalities.

### Finding Escape Hatches
Escape Hatches serve as your checkpoints. Activating all hatches in a single run rewards the *Lunar Surveyor* trophy. These hatches also allow you to return to the "Shelter," where you can:
*   Recover suit integrity.
*   Upgrade your weaponry using materials found in the field.
*   Interact with Diana to unlock hidden dialogue achievements.

## Advanced Tactics for Completionists

Once you have cleared the main story, several "Limit Break" achievements require you to play on higher difficulty settings. In these modes, enemy resistance to hacking increases, and the timing for the Overdrive Protocol becomes much tighter.

### Gear Optimization
Prioritize upgrading your **Shockwave Gun** for damage and your **Stasis Net** for duration. On the moon, crowd control is often more valuable than raw power, especially when dealing with multiple "Walker" units simultaneously.

1.  **Resource Farming:** Revisit the lower floors of the power plant to collect scrap.
2.  **Node Efficiency:** Practice hitting three yellow nodes in a single hacking sequence.
3.  **Perfect Dodges:** Time your thruster bursts right before an impact to trigger a slow-motion window.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I miss any achievements in a single playthrough?**

A: While most combat achievements can be farmed, certain story-related trophies and dialogue interactions with Diana at the Shelter can be missed if you progress past specific "Point of No Return" markers. It is recommended to keep multiple save files.

**Q: How does the Overdrive Protocol work for achievements?**

A: The Overdrive Protocol is activated by filling the hacking gauge through successful mini-games. For the *Overdrive Overlord* trophy, you must kill at least 50 enemies while they are immobilized by this protocol.

**Q: Is there a reward for collecting all hologram surveillance logs?**

A: Yes, collecting all logs unlocks the *Historian of the Moon* achievement and provides a secret cinematic that explains the origins of the Delphi Corporation and Diana’s creation.

**Q: What is the hardest achievement in the pragmata achievement guide?**

A: Most players find *Lunar Ghost* to be the most challenging, as it requires you to reach the top floor of the solar power plant without being detected by a single surveillance drone or Walker unit.
