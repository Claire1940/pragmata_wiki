## Combat and Hacking Mechanics

Once you move past the **プラグマタ 年齢 入力** screen, you are thrust into the lunar research facility where the core gameplay loop begins. Pragmata utilizes a unique "Buddy System" where Hugh handles the direct combat while Diana provides support through advanced hacking.

### The Dual-Action Battle System

In *Pragmata*, combat is not just about shooting; it is a rhythmic dance between protection and offensive hacking. Players must manage Hugh’s ammunition and health while simultaneously guiding Diana through digital security grids.

| Mechanic | Input (Standard) | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aimed Shot** | ZL + ZR | Hugh aims his firearm and fires at robotic husks. |
| **Diana's Hacking** | ABXY Buttons | Move a cursor through a grid to reach the "Green Power" node. |
| **Evasive Maneuver** | B / Circle | Perform a quick roll to avoid incoming projectiles or melee strikes. |
| **Buddy Link** | L / L1 | Diana hitches a ride on Hugh's back for mobility during intense sequences. |

The hacking mini-game is a standout feature. While Hugh is busy fending off enemies, a small grid appears on the side of the screen. You must navigate a blue cursor through "Door" icons to reach the "Power" icon. Successfully completing this puzzle exposes enemy weaknesses, allowing Hugh to deal massive damage.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my age after the initial プラグマタ 年齢 入力 screen?**

A: No. The game explicitly warns players that once the age is entered and confirmed, it cannot be modified. If you made a mistake, you would need to delete your save data and start the setup process from the beginning.

**Q: Is there any "adult content" that requires the age input?**

A: As of the 2026 release, *Pragmata* is rated for a broad audience and does not contain explicit adult content. The age input is primarily for data collection and ensuring compliance with regional gaming regulations.

**Q: Does the game play differently if I enter a very old or very young age?**

A: Currently, there are no documented differences in gameplay or story based on the age entered. Whether you are 18 or 80, the combat, puzzles, and dialogue remain identical in the standard version of the game.

**Q: What happens if I skip the age input?**

A: You cannot skip the **プラグマタ 年齢 入力** prompt. It is a required field in the initial setup menu. You must enter a numerical value to proceed to the main game.

**Q: Is the 2026 demo still available for download?**

A: Yes, the trial version remains available on the PlayStation Store, Xbox Marketplace, and the Nintendo eShop for Switch 2. It allows you to play through the first 45 minutes of the game, including the initial setup and the first major boss battle.
