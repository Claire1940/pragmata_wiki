## The Customization Hub: Cabin and Edus

In the world of Pragmata, customization is handled through the "Cabin" companion system. As seen in the latest 2026 updates, the Cabin serves as your one-stop shop for shelter, upgrades, and visual modifications. Within the Cabin, players can access the Wardrobe Terminal to swap between different layers of their EVA suits and the girl’s outfits.

Customization is divided into three primary categories:
1.  **Full Body Skins:** Complete overhauls of the character models.
2.  **Modular Components:** Individual pieces like helmets, jetpacks, and boots.
3.  **Texture Overlays:** Shaders and patterns that can be applied to existing gear.

## How to Unlock New Costumes

Unlocking **pragmata skins and costumes** requires a mix of exploration, currency management, and skill-based challenges. Capcom has designed the system to reward players who engage with the optional "Cabin" side-quests and the "Control Violence" combat protocols.

### 1. The Cabin Shop
The primary way to acquire new cosmetics is through the Cabin Shop. By collecting "Digital Residue" (the game's primary currency) from defeated enemies and environmental scanning, you can purchase a variety of shaders and accessory items.

### 2. Achievement-Based Unlocks
Certain prestigious skins are locked behind specific gameplay feats. For example, completing the game on "Hardcore Mode" grants access to the **Golden Edus Skin**, a metallic gold variant of the prototype suit that signifies a player's mastery over the game's mechanics.

### 3. Hidden Data Fragments
Throughout the lunar base, players can find glowing data fragments. Collecting these doesn't just provide lore; gathering a full set for a specific region usually unlocks a regional-themed skin, such as the **Deep Crater Camo**.

## FAQ

**Q: Are there any gameplay benefits to specific pragmata skins and costumes?**

A: Most skins are purely cosmetic. However, some "Legendary" grade suits come with minor utility perks, such as increased battery life for your flashlight or a 5% boost to Digital Residue collection. Always check the item description in the Wardrobe Terminal for specific details.

**Q: Can I change my costume at any time during a mission?**

A: No, you can only change your skins and costumes while inside the Cabin or at specific "Save Terminals" found in safe zones. Once you begin a major combat encounter or a narrative set-piece, your appearance is locked until you reach the next checkpoint.

**Q: How do I access the pre-order skins?**

A: Pre-order items like the **Terran Exile** suit are typically found in the "Special Deliveries" tab of the Cabin Shop once you have completed the tutorial prologue. If they do not appear, ensure that the DLC license is correctly installed on your platform.

**Q: Is there a "Transmog" system in Pragmata?**

A: Yes, Pragmata features a "Visual Overlay" system. This allows you to equip the high-stat armor you need for late-game bosses while maintaining the visual appearance of any other skin you have unlocked in your collection.
