## Combat Mechanics: Engaging Both Sides of the Brain

Capcom describes the combat in the **pragmata sketchbook** as a system that requires players to balance raw firepower with technical precision. Hugh is equipped with various weapons, including a "Grip Gun" and heavy-duty firearms, but brute force is rarely enough. Many enemies, particularly the larger "Walkers," possess shields or armor that must be bypassed using Diana’s hacking abilities.

### Hugh’s Arsenal and Tactics
Hugh’s movement is deliberate and weighty. His suit allows him to drop from heights safely and engage in melee combat when enemies get too close. However, his primary strength lies in his ranged capabilities.

*   **Standard Fire:** Effective against smaller, flying drones.
*   **Charged Shots:** Necessary for knocking out larger tasks or breaking through heavy plating.
*   **Scanning:** Using the 'Q' key (on PC) or the designated controller button allows Hugh to identify terminals and enemy weak points.

### Diana’s Hacking Support
Diana is not just a companion to be protected; she is your greatest tactical asset. Throughout the **pragmata sketchbook**, you will encounter locked doors and disabled elevators that only Diana can interact with.

> **💡 Tip:** When a terminal is identified, always position Hugh to cover Diana. She is vulnerable while hacking, and enemies will prioritize her to stop the bypass.

## Lunar Station Walkthrough: Restoring the Power

The demo mission requires you to navigate through several floors of the power plant. The primary goal is to reach the top floor and reboot the main power terminal.

### Step-by-Step Objectives
1.  **Disable Emergency Lockdown:** You must find and scan three separate terminals spread across the initial area. Diana will need to hack these while Hugh fends off waves of bots.
2.  **Navigate the Maze:** The station's layout can be confusing. Use the scanning pulse frequently to locate the next objective marker.
3.  **The Elevator Defense:** Once the first two terminals are active, you must reach a central lift. This section introduces "The Lost," a tougher variant of enemy that requires precise shooting to neutralize.
4.  **The Edith Protocol Boss Fight:** Upon reaching the main terminal, the sector guard protocol "Edith" will engage. This fight requires you to use the "Overdrive Protocol," a powerful state where Hugh and Diana's abilities sync for massive damage.

| Objective | Key Requirement | Reward/Result |
|---|---|---|
| **Terminal 1** | Clear 5 basic drones | Area lockdown partially disabled |
| **Terminal 2** | Protect Diana for 30 seconds | Access to the upper maintenance shafts |
| **Terminal 3** | Defeat the Heavy Walker | Full access to the Power Plant Controller |
| **Main Reboot** | Defeat the Edith Guard | Demo completion and reward unlock |

## Expert Tips for Mastering the Demo

To get the most out of your time with the **pragmata sketchbook**, keep these advanced strategies in mind:

*   **Replay for Secrets:** The demo encourages multiple playthroughs. You might discover new dialogue or hidden data logs by exploring side rooms instead of rushing the main objective.
*   **Manage Your Overdrive:** Don't waste the Overdrive Protocol on basic enemies. Save it for the final encounter with Edith to end the fight quickly and secure a higher rank.

*   **Stay Close to Diana:** While Diana can defend herself to a degree, Hugh's proximity often triggers co-op finishers that are more efficient than standard attacks.
