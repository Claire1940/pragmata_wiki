## Official Pragmata Minimum System Requirements

Capcom has historically been praised for the optimization of the RE Engine, and this title appears to continue that trend. The minimum requirements are surprisingly approachable, targeting players who may still be using mid-range hardware from several years ago. To run the game at 1080p at approximately 45 FPS using the "Performance" preset, you will need at least a six-core processor and a GPU with 6GB of VRAM.

| Component | Minimum Requirement |

## Ray Tracing and Path Tracing Requirements

One of the standout features of the RE Engine in 2026 is its implementation of advanced lighting. The game supports both ray-traced reflections and indirect lighting. However, enabling these features significantly increases the demand on your Video RAM (VRAM). Based on technical testing, 8GB cards may struggle with texture swapping when ray tracing is enabled, making 12GB or 16GB cards the preferred choice for enthusiasts.

| Feature Target | Recommended GPU | VRAM Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1080p Ray Tracing** | RTX 3060 or RX 6700 XT | **12 GB** |
| **1440p High + RT** | RTX 5060 Ti or RX 960 XT | **16 GB** |
| **4K Ultra + Path Tracing** | RTX 5080 or RX 9800 XTX | **16 GB+** |

Path tracing is a more recent addition to the PC version, offering even higher levels of immersion by simulating the true behavior of light in the sterile, high-tech environments of the lunar base. If you plan to use path tracing, ensure you are utilizing upscaling technologies like DLSS 4 or the machine-learning-based FSR 4 to maintain playable frame rates.

## Performance Analysis: Benchmarking the Demo

Early performance tests from the demo version indicate that the game is exceptionally well-optimized for closed-in environments. Unlike open-world titles that often suffer from CPU bottlenecks, the lunar research station's corridors allow the RE Engine to shine. 

Testing on an RTX 3060 at 1080p native resolution (no upscaling) with ray tracing turned on showed an average of 70-80 FPS. When moving to more powerful hardware like the RTX 5060 Ti at 1440p, frame rates remained comfortably in the 90s. Interestingly, the difference between having ray tracing on versus off is not as drastic as in other titles; while turning it off does provide a boost, it doesn't "double" the performance, suggesting the base lighting engine is already quite sophisticated.

> **Warning:** Players on 8GB VRAM cards should be cautious when enabling Ray Tracing. You may experience "stuttering" or low-resolution texture pop-in as the card runs out of memory to store both high-res assets and ray-tracing data.

## Key Gameplay Mechanics and Technical Integration

The **pragmata specs** aren't just about pretty pictures; they support a complex dual-protagonist system. As Hugh, you handle the firearms and movement, while Diana, who remains on your back, assists with hacking armored enemies and overriding security locks. This "tactile combat system" requires low input latency to feel responsive.

1.  **Hacking Matrix:** Grid-based puzzles that occur in real-time during combat.
2.  **Lunum Resource:** A mineral unique to the moon used for shelter upgrades.
3.  **Thruster Gauge:** Manages dodging, jumping, and hovering mechanics.
4.  **Tactile Feedback:** Optimized for both controller and mouse/keyboard inputs.

The developers have stated that "comfort comes first," meaning they have prioritized stable frame rates over extreme graphical flourishes in certain areas. This philosophy ensures that even during chaotic battles, the frame time graph remains smooth, preventing the "micro-stuttering" that plagues many modern Unreal Engine titles. You can find more details on the [official Capcom website](https://www.capcom.com/) regarding their commitment to RE Engine optimization.

## FAQ

**Q: Does Pragmata support older Windows versions?**

A: No, the game requires a 64-bit version of Windows 10 or Windows 11 to utilize DirectX 12, which is mandatory for the game's rendering engine.

**Q: Is 8GB of VRAM enough for the pragmata specs at 1440p?**

A: While 8GB is sufficient for 1440p at Medium to High settings with upscaling (DLSS/FSR), you will likely run into VRAM limitations if you attempt to enable Ray Tracing or Path Tracing at this resolution.

**Q: Does the game feature a shader compilation step?**

A: Yes, upon first launch, the game performs a shader compilation process. This is designed to prevent stuttering during gameplay, a common issue in many modern PC releases.

**Q: Can I play Pragmata on a handheld PC like the Steam Deck?**

A: Given the well-optimized nature of the RE Engine and the modest minimum requirements, the game should be playable on most modern handhelds at 720p/800p using the Performance preset and FSR upscaling.
