## Hugh’s Arsenal: Combat and Weaponry

In Pragmata, combat is a dual effort. While Hugh handles the heavy ordnance, he relies on Diana’s support to create openings. The demo introduces a "temporary weapon" system where specialized firearms are found throughout the environment. Once their ammunition is depleted, Hugh discards them, forcing players to constantly adapt to the gear available on the battlefield.

| Weapon Type | Range | Primary Effect | Best Used Against |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Unit** | Mid-Range | Standard rapid-fire projectiles | Low-tier drone units |
| **Shockwave Gun** | Close-Range | Powerful blast that staggers enemies | Shielded or heavy enemies |
| **Status Net** | Mid-Range | AOE impact that immobilizes targets | Groups of fast-moving robots |
| **Armor-Piercing Unit** | Long-Range | Charged shots with high piercing damage | Bosses and armored cores |

> **💡 Tip:** Do not hoard your specialized weapons. Since they are discarded after use, it is better to use the Shockwave Gun or Status Net immediately to clear difficult waves rather than saving them for a "perfect" moment that may never come.

## Mastering Diana’s Hacking Matrix

One of the most innovative aspects of the **pragmata demo all content** is the real-time hacking system. Diana sits on Hugh's back, and while you are dodging projectiles as Hugh, you must simultaneously navigate a hacking matrix to disable enemy defenses. Successfully connecting to the "EXE" nodes puts enemies into an "Open State," significantly increasing the damage they receive from Hugh’s firearms.

### Hacking Node Variations
The matrix is not just about reaching the end; it is about the path you take. Passing through specific nodes provides temporary buffs that can turn the tide of a difficult encounter.

*   **Green EXE Nodes:** These are your primary targets. Connecting to these completes the hack and vulnerable states.
*   **Yellow Damage Nodes:** Passing through these disrupts internal enemy systems, granting Hugh a temporary damage boost.
*   **Multiple Node Bonus:** If you pass through several nodes of the same type in a single hacking sequence, the effectiveness of the buff is multiplied.

## Resource Management and Suit Integrity

Survival on the moon requires more than just good aim. Hugh’s suit is his only protection against the harsh environment and the aggressive AI of the Cradle. The demo introduces "Lunum" (or Luna Filib), a unique lunar mineral that acts as the primary currency for upgrades and repairs.

### Healing and Checkpoints
Unlike traditional shooters with regenerating health, Pragmata requires active maintenance.
1.  **Suit Integrity (Q Key):** Hugh can use stored energy to repair his suit. This takes a few seconds and leaves you vulnerable, so timing is critical.
2.  **Escape Hatches:** These serve as the demo's checkpoints. Activating an escape hatch fully restores your suit integrity and recharges your shields.
3.  **Thruster Gauge:** Jumping and dodging consume your thruster gauge. If you deplete this, you lose your mobility, making you an easy target for the Sector Guard’s missiles.

| Action | Resource Consumed | Recovery Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Healing** | Suit Energy | Collecting Lunum / Escape Hatches |
| **Dodging** | Thruster Gauge | Automatic recharge over time |
| **Special Attacks** | Hacking Gauge | Successful hacking sequences |

## Boss Strategy: Defeating Sector Guard Edith

The finale of the **pragmata demo all content** is a confrontation with Edith, the solar power plant’s security protocol. This boss utilizes massive missile volleys and area-of-effect slams that can quickly deplete Hugh’s suit integrity.

To defeat Edith, you must focus on the glowing brown weak points located on its chassis. However, these points are often shielded. You must use Diana to initiate a hack while dodging Edith’s projectiles. Once the hack is successful, Edith will enter a staggered state.

### The Overdrive Protocol
When your hacking gauge is full, you can activate the **Overdrive Protocol (X Key)**. This is a "cinema-style" finisher that deals massive AOE damage and immobilizes all nearby threats. During the Edith fight, using Overdrive is essential for bypassing the final phase of its armor.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Edith’s missiles are made from Luna Filib, meaning they can track your movement. Use your thrusters to hover mid-air to avoid the ground-based explosion radius.

## Unlockable Outfits and Bonus Content

*   **Neo Bushido Set:** A samurai and ninja-inspired outfit for Hugh and Diana, featuring a striking black and red color scheme.

To view these outfits, navigate to the "Suit" menu in the demo's main hub. Completing the demo also unlocks the "Shelter BGM" and a digital data library containing concept artwork of the Cradle.
