## How to Unlock the Hidden Boss and Secret Rewards

One of the most elusive items on the **pragmata demo rewards list** is Diana’s second drawing, which is directly tied to a hidden enemy encounter. This encounter occurs during the elevator ascent, a segment that many players breeze through without realizing there is a secret trigger.

To unlock the hidden boss, you must adhere to a specific "Weapon Variety" rule during the bot ambush. There are three primary bots that descend onto the elevator platform. To trigger the secret, you must finish each bot with a different weapon type.

### Step-by-Step Hidden Boss Trigger

1.  **First Bot:** Reduce its health using any method, but ensure the "Finishing Blow" is delivered with the **Pistol**.
2.  **Second Bot:** Use the **Shockwave Gun** to deliver the final hit.
3.  **Third Bot:** Lower its health with standard fire, then finish it using the **Hack** ability.

If performed correctly, the "Intruders Detected" alarm will change frequency, and a hidden elite bot will phase into the arena. Defeating this enemy adds a unique entry to your rewards list and unlocks Diana’s second drawing in the Sketchbook.

## Strategies for Completionists

To ensure you have checked off every item on the **pragmata demo rewards list**, we recommend a minimum of two playthroughs. The first playthrough should focus on the narrative and basic controls, while the second should be a "Challenge Run" focused on the following:

1.  **Weapon Discipline:** Practice switching between the Pistol and Shockwave Gun rapidly to prepare for the elevator sequence.
2.  **Environmental Awareness:** Search every corner of the Cradle before triggering the "Unregistered Guest" protocol. Once the combat starts, certain areas become inaccessible.
3.  **Difficulty Scaling:** The Prototype Suit Skin is only available to those who complete the demo on the highest available difficulty setting. This requires mastering the dodge mechanic, as IDUS units become significantly more aggressive.

The hidden boss encounter is particularly difficult because the "Hidden Sentinel" has a high resistance to standard ballistic fire. Players should prioritize using the Shockwave Gun to deplete its stamina bar before attempting to use the Hack ability for a high-value data reward.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the pragmata demo rewards list carry over to the full game?**

A: While Capcom has not confirmed a 1:1 transfer of all items, they have stated that players with a "Completed Sketchbook" save file will receive an exclusive emblem and starter resource pack in the full 2026 release.

**Q: How many drawings are in Diana’s Sketchbook within the demo?**

A: There are currently three unlockable drawings. The first is story-related, the second requires defeating the hidden elevator boss, and the third is found by interacting with all lore terminals in the Cradle.

**Q: What happens if I kill the elevator bots with the same weapon?**

A: If you use the same weapon to finish off more than one bot, the hidden boss will not appear. You will still complete the demo, but you will miss out on the "Hidden Sentinel" combat data and the second drawing.

**Q: Is the Shockwave Gun available from the start?**

A: No, you must pick it up from the security locker located just before the elevator sequence. It is a mandatory pickup, but it is easy to miss if you are rushing through the corridor.
