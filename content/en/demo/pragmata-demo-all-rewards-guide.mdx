## How to Unlock the Hidden Boss and Drawing #2

One of the most elusive rewards in the sketchbook demo is the hidden boss encounter. This encounter is directly tied to obtaining Diana’s second drawing. To trigger this event, you must pay close attention to the elevator sequence involving the three maintenance bots.

To unlock the hidden boss, you must "finish off" each of the three bots on the elevator using a **different weapon or method**. The game tracks the final blow, not the total damage dealt.

### Steps to Trigger the Hidden Boss:
1.  **Bot 1:** Reduce its health and finish it using your standard **Pistol**.
2.  **Bot 2:** Weaken the enemy and deliver the killing blow with the **Shockwave Gun**.
3.  **Bot 3:** Use your pistol to lower its health, then finish it using the **Hack** command (Diana’s assistance).

If performed correctly, a specialized "Hidden Bot" boss will spawn once the elevator reaches the destination. Defeating this boss automatically grants you **Drawing #2** for the sketchbook.

## Unlocking New Weapons and Abilities

While the demo provides a basic kit, exploring the solar plant reveals powerful upgrades that drastically change combat. These are essential for players following this **pragmata demo all rewards guide** to achieve the fastest clear times.

### The Charge Piercer (Railgun)
This is a powerful attack unit that fires armor-piercing rounds. Its damage increases the longer you charge the shot, and it is unaffected by range, making it the best weapon for the final EDIS boss fight. You can find it in a side hallway just before the final elevator; look for a terminal that requires a manual override.

### Multi-Hack and Overdrive Protocol
The Multi-Hack allows Diana to link multiple enemies together, sharing damage across the entire group. This is unlocked by interacting with a specific hacking node during the "Emergency Lockdown" sequence. Once you have this, you can more easily fill your meter for the **Overdrive Protocol**, an ability that immobilizes all nearby enemies in a stasis field.

| Ability/Weapon | Primary Use | Best Against |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shockwave Gun** | Crowd control and knockback | Swarms of small maintenance bots |
| **Charge Piercer** | High-damage precision shots | EDIS (Sector Guard) Boss |
| **Witch Time Dodge** | Slows down time on perfect timing | Fast-moving melee attackers |
| **Stasis Net** | Holds enemies in place | Flying drones and agile targets |

## Speedrunning for Diana’s Drawings (#6, #7, #8)

Capcom has encouraged players to replay the demo by tying specific rewards to clear times. These drawings are essentially the "Ending Screens" of the demo. 

*   **Drawing #6:** This is the most difficult to obtain, requiring a clear time of **less than 10 minutes**. To achieve this, you must skip all optional combat and use the Charge Piercer to phase the final boss as quickly as possible.
*   **Drawing #7 & #8:** These are awarded for standard play. Drawing #7 usually appears for clears between 15-20 minutes, while #8 is the default reward for players taking their time to explore.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Using the "Retry" option from the pause menu does not reset your internal timer. If you are going for Drawing #6, a single death usually means you should restart the entire demo from the main menu.

## The Scribble Suit: The Ultimate Cosmetic Reward

The most visual reward in the demo is the **Lunar Suit (Scribbles)**. This is a special version of Hugh’s space suit that is covered in colorful drawings made by Diana. 

To equip this suit:
1.  Complete the demo at least once on any difficulty.
2.  Return to the Main Menu.
3.  Select "Options" and then "Appearance."
4.  Toggle the Lunar Suit to the "Scribble" variant.

This suit doesn't just look different; it also changes some of the dialogue in the demo. During the cutscene where Hugh asks Diana what she was working on, she will point to the suit itself if you have it equipped, adding a charming layer of meta-commentary to the experience.

## Combat Tips for 100% Completion

Achieving everything in this **pragmata demo all rewards guide** requires mastering the "Glad Dad" gameplay loop. Unlike traditional action games, *Pragmata* emphasizes protecting Diana while she assists you with hacks.

*   **Master the "Witch Time" Dodge:** Once unlocked, the perfect dodge is your best friend. Dodging just before an impact slows time, allowing you to charge a full Charge Piercer shot for massive damage.
*   **Use Diana as a Tool:** Diana isn't just an NPC; she is your secondary weapon. Use her Multi-Hack to clear rooms of "goop" and bots simultaneously to save time during speedruns.
*   **Scan Frequently:** The scan pulse (activated with Q or the designated controller button) highlights hidden crates and terminals through walls. This is the only way to find the refined minerals needed for the highest rank.
