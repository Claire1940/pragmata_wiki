> **💡 Pro Tip:** Always prioritize Diana’s hacking on shielded enemies. Once she overrides their locks, Hugh can finish the encounter with significantly less ammunition.

## Mastering the Hacking and Shooting System

The "Hacking and Shooting" system is described as a strategy-heavy approach to action. ### Tactical Combat Loop
1. **Engagement:** Hugh initiates combat to draw aggro and position enemies.
2. **Analysis:** Diana identifies which robots can be overridden or disabled.
3. **Execution:** Diana hacks the target, temporarily stunning it or removing its armor.
4. **Conclusion:** Hugh utilizes high-damage firearms to destroy the exposed core.

## Dealing with the Ethers and AI Threats

The Ethers represent a nefarious AI force that views Hugh and Diana as anomalies to be purged. They control the station’s automated defenses and can manifest physical forms through lunar filaments. In any **pragmata trailer breakdown**, the sheer variety of these enemies is a standout feature.

*   **Scout Drones:** Fast-moving units that call for reinforcements.
*   **Filament Sentinels:** Heavy-hitting melee units that require Diana to "crack" their shells.
*   **Station Overlords:** Boss-level AI that can manipulate the room's gravity and layout.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Lunaquakes can occur at any time during exploration. These events deal massive environmental damage and can separate Hugh and Diana, forcing a temporary defensive playstyle.
