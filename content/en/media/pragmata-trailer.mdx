## Gameplay Mechanics: Controlled Violence and Exploration

One of the most striking lines from the **pragmata trailer** involves the granting of "permission to commit acts of controlled violence." This phrase suggests a highly regulated world where even self-defense is governed by AI protocols. From a gameplay perspective, this translates into a combat system that feels heavy, deliberate, and tactical.

The protagonist utilizes a high-tech suit that appears capable of deploying various gadgets and weaponry. Unlike the fast-paced action of *Devil May Cry*, this title seems to favor a more grounded approach, emphasizing positioning and the protection of the young girl companion. The relationship between the two characters is not just narrative; it is a mechanical pillar of the experience.
