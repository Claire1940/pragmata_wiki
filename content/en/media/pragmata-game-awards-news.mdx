## Why the Delays Were Necessary

Since its initial reveal in 2020, the project has faced several public delays. The latest **pragmata game awards news** suggests these were not due to development hell, but rather a shift in scope. Capcom reportedly expanded the "Cradle" environment to be a fully seamless open-world experience rather than a series of linear levels. This expansion required significant rework of the game's physics engine, particularly regarding the low-gravity combat mechanics.

The developers have expressed their commitment to delivering a polished experience that establishes a new franchise. By taking the extra time, Capcom has been able to integrate feedback from early internal playtests, specifically improving the interaction between the protagonist and Diana to ensure the emotional stakes feel earned.
