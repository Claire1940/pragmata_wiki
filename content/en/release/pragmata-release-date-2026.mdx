The narrative of Pragmata centers on a heavy-suited protagonist and a young girl named Diana. ## Gameplay Mechanics and Combat Systems

Pragmata is an action-adventure title that balances exploration with intense combat encounters. The protagonist utilizes a high-tech suit capable of deploying various gadgets and weapons to neutralize "Cradle" security forces. A unique mechanic involves the protection and cooperation of Diana, who seems to possess abilities that can influence the digital environment around her.

| Mechanic | Description |
|---|---|
| **Attack Protocols** | Players can authorize "acts of controlled violence" to dismantle robotic threats. |
| **EDS Management** | Repairing and navigating the damaged Environmental Defense System is a core puzzle element. |
| **Companion Synergy** | Diana can hack certain terminals and provide buffs during high-intensity fights. |
| **Lunar Physics** | Combat is affected by low gravity, allowing for verticality and unique projectile trajectories. |

The combat has been described as "deliberate," where players must manage their resources and suit integrity. Taking too much damage can lead to suit breaches, which are fatal in the vacuum of the moon's surface.

> **Tip:** Always keep an eye on your oxygen levels and suit pressure when venturing outside the pressurized zones of the Cradle.
