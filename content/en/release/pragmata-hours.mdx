## Pragmata Playtime Breakdown

The time you spend on the moon varies significantly based on your playstyle. Pragmata is structured as a series of sectors connected by a central hub known as the Shelter. Players who ignore the optional "REM" (Read Earth Memory) files and skip the Cabin’s Stamp Club activities will find a much shorter experience than those who aim to unlock every suit modification.

| Playstyle | Average Time | Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Main Story** | **7 - 10 Hours** | Core narrative missions, essential bosses only. |

## Factors Influencing Pragmata Hours

Several core gameplay systems directly influence how many **pragmata hours** you will record. Unlike traditional shooters, Pragmata relies heavily on a "hack and blast" rhythm that can be time-consuming if not mastered early.

### The Hacking Mini-Game
To deal significant damage to high-level bots, Hugh must use Diana’s hacking abilities. This opens a grid-based mini-game where you navigate a node to open the "robotic gills" of your enemies.
*   **Blue Nodes:** Increase damage output and keep the enemy's weak points exposed longer.
*   **Yellow Nodes:** Lower enemy defenses or provide tactical buffs.
*   **Green Nodes:** The "executable" that finishes the hack.

Mastering this "Snake-like" interface early in the game can shave hours off the more difficult combat encounters in the later sectors of the moon base.

## Exploration and Collectibles

If you are aiming to maximize your **pragmata hours**, you will need to engage with the game's various collectibles. These are not just for show; they provide tangible benefits to your character's progression and the relationship between Hugh and Diana.

### The Shelter Hub
The Shelter acts as your base of operations. Here, you can interact with the "Cabin Staff"—square-headed robots that offer various services.
1.  **Unit Printer:** Upgrade your suit and weapons using collected filaments.
2.  **Cabin's Stamp Club:** A bingo-style reward system where you trade "Cabin Coins" for outfits and bot data.
3.  **Jukebox:** Customize the soundtrack of your hub experience.
4.  **Gift System:** Give Diana items found in the world, such as a basketball or crayons, to unlock new interactions and upgrade points.

### REM and Mini-Cabin Figures
Scattered throughout the 3D-printed version of New York and the lunar power plants are REM files. These provide the backstory for why the moon base was abandoned and what happened to the original "Cradle" crew. Finding all of these is a requirement for the Platinum trophy and adds roughly 5-7 hours to a standard playthrough.

## Difficulty Settings and Replayability

Pragmata offers two primary difficulty settings at the start: **Standard** and **Hard**. The developer-intended experience is Standard, which provides a balanced challenge for your first set of **pragmata hours**. 

Hard mode significantly increases enemy aggression and reduces the timing window for the "Critical Shot" node during hacking. For those who want the ultimate challenge, completing the game unlocks a "New Game Plus" mode and a hidden difficulty level that tests your mastery of the thruster mechanics and combat combos.

| Difficulty | Enemy Health | Hacking Window | Recommended For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard** | 100% | Generous | First-time players. |
| **Hard** | 150% | Tight | Action veterans. |
| **NG+** | Scaling | Variable | Completionists & Trophy Hunters. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** High-difficulty runs require a deep understanding of the "Overdrive" mechanic. Do not attempt Hard mode until you have unlocked the advanced Hacking Gauge upgrades in the Shelter.

## Maximizing Your Efficiency

To get the most out of your **pragmata hours** without getting frustrated, follow these expert strategies:
*   **Scan Frequently:** Use the scan ability to find hidden illusory walls and yellow upgrade nodes.
*   **Prioritize Mobility:** The first upgrades you should purchase are for your thrusters. Increased hover time makes platforming sections much safer.
*   **Lure Enemies:** Use the "Decoy Generator" to group enemies before using a Multihack. This allows you to clear rooms in seconds rather than minutes.
*   **Check the Map:** The minimalist UI hides the map in the pause menu. It highlights uncollected items in orange, making it easy to track your 100% progress.

## FAQ

**Q: How many pragmata hours does it take to beat the story?**

A: For most players, the main story of Pragmata takes between 7 and 10 hours. This involves completing the primary missions and mandatory boss fights across the lunar sectors.

**Q: Is there a New Game Plus mode?**

A: Yes, completing the game once unlocks New Game Plus. This allows you to carry over your suit upgrades, weapon levels, and collected Mini-Cabin figures into a new, more challenging run.

**Q: Can I go back to previous areas to find missed collectibles?**

A: Absolutely. The Shelter serves as a hub that allows you to revisit any previously cleared sector. This is essential for players looking to find all REM files and achieve 100% completion.

**Q: Does difficulty affect the length of the game?**

A: While the content remains the same, playing on Hard mode can increase your total **pragmata hours** due to the increased health of enemies and the precision required for the hacking mini-games. Expect to spend more time in combat and resource farming on higher difficulties.
