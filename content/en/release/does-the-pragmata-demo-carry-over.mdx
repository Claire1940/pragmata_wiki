## Combat Mechanics: Hacking and Fatalities

The combat in Pragmata is less about traditional "run and gun" mechanics and more about solving environmental and enemy-based puzzles. If you don't utilize the hacking system, you will find yourself overwhelmed and dealing minimal damage.

### The Hacking Priority List
When entering a combat encounter, you must decide which targets to prioritize. Hacking an enemy makes them vulnerable to your standard weapons and can even turn their own ordnance against them.

| Enemy Type | Hacking Benefit | Priority Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Missile Launchers** | Redirect missiles back at the source. | **High** |
| **Signal Jammers** | Restores your ability to use companion skills. | **Critical** |
| **Standard Cyborgs** | Stuns them and opens them up for a "fatality." | **Medium** |
| **Nodes/Turrets** | Clears paths and provides cover. | **Low** |

### Executing Fatalities
A new feature highlighted in the 2026 gameplay is the "Fatality" or finisher move. Once an enemy’s health is whittled down through a combination of hacking and physical damage, a prompt appears to execute a high-damage finisher. This is essential for clearing the map quickly and managing large groups of enemies.

## Boss Fight Strategy: The Signal Jammer Giant

The demo culminates in a massive boss fight that tests everything you have learned about the game's puzzle-combat hybrid system. This boss is a "larger-than-life" enemy that uses signal jammers to disable your most powerful abilities.

### Phase-by-Phase Breakdown
*   **Phase 1: Node Hunting.** The boss will protect itself with four signal jammers located in different quadrants of the map. You must navigate the traversal puzzles to reach these nodes and hack them.
*   **Phase 2: Verticality.** Once the jammers are down, the boss begins using ground-based shockwaves. You must use the "top of buildings" strategy to avoid damage while whittling away at its life bar.
*   **Phase 3: Missile Redirection.** In the final stage, the boss launches volleys of missiles. The most effective strategy is to hack the individual missiles mid-air and send them back to the boss to trigger a final fatality window.

> **Tip:** Use the decoy weapon during Phase 2 to distract smaller adds while you focus on the signal jammer nodes. This prevents you from being overwhelmed while you are vulnerable during the hacking animation.
