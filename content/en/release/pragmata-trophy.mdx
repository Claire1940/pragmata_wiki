## Combat Mechanics and Teamwork Achievements

Combat in this lunar setting is a dance between the protagonist's raw power and Diana's tactical assistance. To earn a high-tier **pragmata trophy** related to combat, you must learn to time your parries and utilize Diana’s spotting abilities.

One of the most satisfying achievements involves the "Missile spotted" mechanic. When an enemy launches a projectile, Diana will alert you, allowing for a "Right back at you" counter-attack. Successfully performing this maneuver 20 times unlocks the "Return to Sender" trophy.

## Exploration and Environmental Milestones

The world is filled with "spots" that Diana will identify. When she asks, "See the spot? You think we can get here in one piece?", it usually indicates a nearby collectible or a trophy-related landmark. Following her lead is the most reliable way to find the "Lunar Memories" items required for the "Historian of the Moon" achievement.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Some exploration trophies are location-specific. If you progress past certain story gates without interacting with Diana’s "spots," you may need to restart a chapter to claim the missed **pragmata trophy**.

## Advanced Strategies for the Platinum

To secure the final Platinum, you must complete the game on the "Vanguard" difficulty setting. This mode scales enemy health and reduces the availability of backup power in the Shelter. 

Follow these steps to prepare for the final challenge:
*   Max out the "Crack" and "Jump" abilities to maintain mobility.
*   Keep your Shelter's backup power at 100% before starting "The Spot" mission.
*   Ensure Diana's "Missile Spotting" is upgraded to provide a longer parry window.
*   Use the "Cube" to create temporary cover during boss fights where environmental shielding is sparse.

## FAQ

**Q: Are there any missable trophies in the 2026 version of the game?**

A: Yes, several exploration trophies tied to Diana's "spots" can be missed if you move too quickly through the main story chapters. It is recommended to explore every dialogue prompt Diana gives before leaving an area.

**Q: How do I unlock the "Right Back at You" pragmata trophy?**

A: This trophy requires you to deflect 20 missiles back at enemies. Wait for Diana to shout "Missile spotted," then use your melee strike or "Cube" shield at the precise moment the projectile nears your character.

**Q: Can I earn trophies while playing in the Shelter?**

A: Yes, the "Shelter" is where you earn all crafting and upgrade-related trophies. Specifically, the "Backup Power" and "Master Craftsman" achievements are unlocked by interacting with the Shelter's main terminal after gathering enough resources.

**Q: Does difficulty affect the pragmata trophy list?**

A: Yes, there is a specific trophy for completing the game on the "Vanguard" difficulty setting. However, most combat and upgrade trophies can be earned on lower difficulties to make the process easier.
