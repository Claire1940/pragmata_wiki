## Navigating the Primary Sectors

When asking **how many sectors in Pragmata** are required for completion, it is important to note that while there are five main narrative sectors, each contains sub-levels and "Shelter" points where you can modify your gear. The RE Engine allows for seamless transitions between these areas, making the journey feel like one continuous, desperate escape.

### Sector 01: Lunar Arrival
This serves as the game's tutorial zone. Here, players learn the weight of Hugh’s movements in the lunar gravity and the basics of his multi-tool weaponry. The focus is on environmental traversal and surviving initial skirmishes with basic drone units. It is the shortest sector but sets the cinematic tone for the rest of the adventure.

### Sector 02: The Residential Hub
This is where the game’s heart truly lies. You will spend a significant amount of time here navigating abandoned malls and apartments that look eerily like Earth. This sector introduces the "Shelter" mechanic, a safe zone where you can interact with Diana and upgrade your equipment. 

### Sector 03: Industrial Foundry
The difficulty ramps up here. This sector is characterized by tight corridors and massive machinery. You will face the "Cradle's robot legions" in large numbers, requiring you to master the synergy between Hugh’s firepower and Diana’s hacking abilities. Understanding the layout here is vital, as there are many environmental hazards like molten metal and high-voltage rails.

## The Role of the Shelter in Sector Progression

Between the intense combat encounters, the Shelter provides a moment of respite. Found at the end of every major sector, the Shelter is more than just a save point. It is where the narrative unfolds through small, character-driven moments between Hugh and Diana. 

The Shelter allows you to:
1.  **Customize Loadouts:** Swap out weapon modules based on the upcoming sector's enemy types.
2.  **Review Data Logs:** Collectibles found in the sectors provide context for the fall of the lunar colony.
3.  **Upgrade Diana:** Improve her hacking speed and the duration of her defensive buffs.
4.  **Cosmetic Changes:** While Pragmata avoids "battle pass" bloat, there are several unlockable suits for Hugh that offer minor stat variations.

> **Tip:** Always check the terminal in the Shelter for "Sector Briefings." These provide hints about the boss mechanics found at the end of each zone.

## FAQ

**Q: How many sectors in Pragmata are there in total?**

A: There are five primary narrative sectors in the main campaign: Lunar Arrival, Residential Hub, Industrial Foundry, Research Core, and Atmospheric Gateway. Each sector is densely packed with unique challenges and story elements.

**Q: Can I revisit previous sectors after completing them?**

A: Yes, once you have cleared a sector, you can access it via the "Simulation Terminal" in the Shelter. This allows you to hunt for missed collectibles or complete specific combat challenges to earn more upgrade materials.

**Q: Does the game have a New Game Plus mode for these sectors?**

A: Absolutely. Upon completing all five sectors, you unlock New Game Plus, which increases enemy aggression and adds new "Elite" variants to the robot legions in every sector.

**Q: Are there secret sectors or hidden zones?**

A: While there are no "secret" full sectors, each of the five main areas contains hidden sub-rooms and "Data Vaults" that require high-level hacking from Diana to access. These vaults contain the best gear modules in the game.
