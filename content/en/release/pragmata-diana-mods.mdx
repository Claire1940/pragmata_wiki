## Installation Guide for Diana Modifications

Installing **pragmata diana mods** follows the standard procedure for modern Capcom titles. Most users prefer using a dedicated mod manager to handle the file injection process, which prevents the need for manual file overwriting.

1.  **Download the Mod Manager:** Most 2026 mods are compatible with the Fluffy Mod Manager (Version 4.0 or higher).
2.  **Locate Game Directory:** Ensure the manager points to your *Pragmata* installation folder on Steam or the Capcom Launcher.
3.  **Place Mod Files:** Move the downloaded `.rar` or `.zip` files into the `Games/Pragmata/Mods` folder within the mod manager directory.
4.  **Toggle the Mod:** Open the manager, refresh the list, and toggle the desired Diana outfit to "On."
5.  **Launch the Game:** Start the game through the manager to ensure the assets load correctly.

## Performance and Compatibility Considerations

When running multiple **pragmata diana mods**, you must be mindful of VRAM usage. *Pragmata* uses high-fidelity ray tracing for its lunar surfaces and glass-heavy "New York" environments. Adding a 4K texture mod to Diana can lead to frame rate drops on mid-range hardware if the VRAM limit is exceeded.

## FAQ

**Q: Do pragmata diana mods disable achievements?**

A: Generally, no. Cosmetic mods that replace models and textures do not interfere with the game's achievement tracking on Steam or other platforms. However, mods that alter gameplay scripts or give you an unfair advantage in combat might be flagged.

**Q: Can I use multiple Diana mods at the same time?**

A: You can only use one mod that replaces the same file at a time. For instance, you cannot have two different "Full Body" costumes active simultaneously. However, you can combine a costume mod with an independent texture mod for her eyes or hair if they modify different asset paths.

**Q: Where can I find the latest pragmata diana mods?**

A: Most developers host their work on Nexus Mods or specialized Discord servers dedicated to Capcom RE Engine modding. Always check the "Latest Uploads" section for 2026 to find the most up-to-date versions compatible with the latest game patches.

**Q: Will modding Diana affect the story or cutscenes?**

A: Mods are primarily visual. While Diana will appear in her modified outfit during real-time cutscenes, her voice-over and the narrative path remain identical to the vanilla experience. Some pre-rendered video files may still show her in her original suit.
