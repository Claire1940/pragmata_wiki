## Technical Ambition and the RE Engine

One of the primary factors contributing to the **pragmata development time** is the technical ambition of the project. Built on a heavily modified version of the RE Engine, Pragmata aims to push the boundaries of ray-tracing and physics-based rendering. The trailers have showcased highly detailed environments, from abandoned modern city streets to the vacuum of space, all featuring realistic textures and lighting.

The game is designed to take full advantage of next-generation platform features, specifically focusing on ultra-high-speed SSDs and advanced haptic feedback. Developing a new IP with this level of fidelity is significantly more time-consuming than iterating on an existing franchise. Capcom’s internal teams have reportedly been focused on ensuring the "digital echo" mechanics and the interaction between the astronaut and the girl feel seamless and innovative.
