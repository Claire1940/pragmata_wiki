## The Standard Edition: The Purist's Choice

The Standard Edition is the baseline version of Pragmata. It is designed for players who are primarily interested in the narrative and the core gameplay loop. Available on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, PC, and the Nintendo Switch 2, this version provides the complete 2026 lunar adventure without any additional "fluff."

If you opt for a physical copy of the Standard Edition, you may find retailers offering slight discounts compared to the digital storefronts. It is highly recommended to shop around at major electronics retailers to save on the base price.

**Who should buy this?**
*   Players focused on the story and combat mechanics.
*   Budget-conscious gamers.
*   Physical collectors who prefer a disc or cartridge for their shelf.

> **💡 Tip:** If you buy the Standard Edition and later decide you want the extra cosmetics, Capcom has indicated that the Deluxe content may be sold as a separate "Upgrade Pack" on digital stores post-launch.

## The Digital Deluxe Edition: Customization & Extras

For fans who want to maximize their immersion in the "Shelter" (the game's central hub), the Digital Deluxe Edition offers several aesthetic upgrades. For an additional $10, players receive the "Shelter Variety Pack," which is the centerpiece of this edition.

### What is in the Shelter Variety Pack?
1.  **Exclusive Outfits:** Two additional sets of clothing for both Hugh and Diana. These include the "Bumblebee" set and a secondary tactical outfit.
2.  **Grip Gun DS Skin:** A unique visual overhaul for Hugh’s primary utility tool, giving it a more premium, high-tech finish.
3.  **Shelter BGM:** Three alternate music tracks that can be played while resting or upgrading gear in the lunar hub.
4.  **Diana Gestures:** A set of unique emotes and interactions for Diana, adding more personality to her AI-driven behavior.
5.  **Digital Artwork Data Library:** A high-resolution gallery featuring concept art, character renders, and world-building notes from the development of Pragmata.

## Pre-Order Bonuses: The Neo-Shinobi Set

Regardless of which of the **pragmata editions** you select, pre-ordering the game before the April 24, 2026 release date grants access to the "Hugh and Diana Outfit Set." This set is inspired by traditional Japanese stealth and combat attire, reimagined for a sci-fi setting.

*   **Hugh Outfit:** The "Neo Bushido" set (Samurai-inspired).
*   **Diana Outfit:** The "Neo Kunoichi" set (Ninja-inspired).

It is important to note that these outfits are **purely cosmetic**. They do not provide any stats, damage resistance, or special abilities. They are visual skins designed to let you play through the story with a specific aesthetic flair.

## Platform Exclusives and the Nintendo Switch 2 Amiibo

While the digital content remains consistent across PlayStation, Xbox, and PC, the Nintendo Switch 2 version features a unique physical tie-in. While there is no traditional "Collector's Edition" with a statue for the other platforms, Nintendo fans can purchase a standalone **Diana Amiibo**.

| Item | Platform | Function | Price |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Diana Amiibo** | Nintendo Switch 2 | Unlocks daily loot and exclusive in-game rewards | **$29.99** |
| **Standard Disc** | PS5 / Xbox Series X | Physical ownership and resale value | **$59.99** |
| **Digital Key** | PC (Steam/Epic) | Instant pre-load and play at midnight | **$59.99** |

The Diana Amiibo is sold separately from the game software. When scanned, it provides Hugh and Diana with various "Cradle" security protocol rewards, which can include crafting materials or consumable items to help during the more difficult environmental puzzles.

To determine the best value for your 2026 gaming budget, consider your history with Capcom titles and your preference for digital collectibles. 

1.  **The "No-Regret" Choice:** Buy the **Standard Edition**. You get the full game, and if you pre-order, you still get the best-looking ninja outfits. This version offers the highest value-to-cost ratio.
2.  **The "Super Fan" Choice:** Buy the **Digital Deluxe Edition**. If you enjoy spending time in the game's hub, changing music, and looking through concept art, the $10 premium is a fair price for the additional content.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the Digital Deluxe Edition include any story DLC or future expansions?**

A: No. The Digital Deluxe Edition only includes cosmetic items, music, and an art library. Any future story content or gameplay expansions would likely be sold separately for all users.

**Q: Are the pre-order outfits exclusive to pre-orders forever?**

A: Capcom has stated that the Neo Bushido and Neo Kunoichi outfits may be made available for purchase on digital stores at a later date. However, pre-ordering is the only way to guarantee them for free at launch.

**Q: Is there a physical Collector's Edition with a statue?**

A: As of April 2026, there is no official Collector's Edition featuring a statue or physical art book. The closest equivalent is the Diana Amiibo available for the Nintendo Switch 2 version.

**Q: Can I upgrade from the Standard Edition to the Deluxe Edition later?**

A: Yes, players who purchase the Standard version of the **pragmata editions** will be able to buy the "Shelter Variety Pack" as a standalone DLC on their respective platform's store.
