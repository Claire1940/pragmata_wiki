| Playstyle | Estimated Time (Hours) | Focus |

## Combat Mechanics and the Role of Diana

The gameplay in Pragmata revolves around the synergy between the protagonist, Hugh, and the young girl, Diana (officially designated as DI0367). Unlike traditional escort missions, Diana is a powerful asset who facilitates the hacking mechanics necessary to defeat advanced robotic threats.

The combat is a "hacking and shooting combo" that requires more than just fast reflexes. Players must navigate blue nodes to strengthen hacks, making enemies vulnerable to Hugh’s military-grade weaponry. This adds a layer of "brainpower" to encounters, preventing the game from becoming a mindless shooter.

### Core Gameplay Systems

*   **Hacking:** Managed by Diana to disable enemy shields and environmental hazards.
*   **Shooting:** Hugh utilizes various printed weapons, ranging from standard rifles to high-output energy cannons.
*   **Mobility:** Hovering, evading, and jumping are essential for surviving seismic shifts on the moon.
*   **Tactical Depth:** Passing through specific nodes during a fight increases your hack potency.

## The Shelter: Progression and Upgrades

A significant portion of the **durata pragmata** involves interacting with the Shelter. This hub serves as your safe haven from the lunar surface's dangers. Here, you can spend resources collected during missions to improve your combat effectiveness.

| Resource | Usage | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Lunar Filament** | Upgrading weapons and hacking nodes. | Found during exploration and combat. |
| **Cabin Coins** | Spent at the "Cabin" assistant for extra unlocks. | Reward for completing specific sector challenges. |
| **Firmware Updates** | Improves Hugh’s performance and Diana’s hacking speed. | Earned via the Shelter's main terminal. |

The Shelter itself levels up as you progress. Bringing back the right materials unlocks new functions, such as the tram system for traveling around the "Cradle" and advanced weapon printing stations. This loop of exploring, gathering, and upgrading is what extends the game beyond a simple 10-hour run.

## Save Systems and Technical Performance

While the **durata pragmata** is favorable for busy adults, the save structure requires some planning. Pragmata does not currently feature a "save anywhere" manual system. Instead, progress is primarily recorded when you enter a Shelter or complete a major story chapter.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Quitting the game mid-mission may result in losing 5–10 minutes of progress. Always wait for the auto-save icon or return to a Shelter before closing the application.

Technically, the game is a visual powerhouse. In 2026, it remains one of the most stunning titles available, especially on high-end hardware like the RTX 5080. The "Cyberpunk New York" vibe of the lunar cities is crisp and realistic, with stable performance even during intense boss fights. If you die during a boss encounter, the game allows you to restart, though it may occasionally send you back to the hub to re-gear, which can be a minor point of friction.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the average durata pragmata for a casual player?**

A: A casual player focusing mainly on the story should expect a **durata pragmata** of approximately 12 to 15 hours. This includes some light exploration and basic weapon upgrades.
