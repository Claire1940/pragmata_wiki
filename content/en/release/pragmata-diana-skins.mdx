## The Cabin Stamp Card System Explained

The primary method for obtaining **pragmata diana skins** is through the Cabin Stamp Card system. As you progress through the game's five main sectors, you will level up your Shelter. Each level granted to your Shelter provides a new Stamp Card. To claim rewards, you must spend Cabin Coins, which are primarily earned by completing Training Sim missions and exploring the lunar environment.

There are two types of rewards on these cards:
1.  **Standard Rewards:** These are unlocked by placing stamps on specific nodes on the card.
2.  **Blackout Rewards:** These are premium skins granted only after you have filled every single slot on a specific Stamp Card (achieving a "Blackout").

> **💡 Tip:** You will often earn more Cabin Coins than there are slots on a single card. Do not hoard them; spend them as soon as you level up your Shelter to unlock new outfits early.

## How to Level Up Your Shelter Fast

To gain access to the later Stamp Cards (specifically Cards 4 and 5), you must reach Shelter Level 5. This requires more than just finishing the story. You must engage with the post-game content to reach the maximum tier of rewards.

1.  **Clear the Five Sectors:** Each sector completion naturally levels up your home base.
2.  **Defeat the Final Boss:** This is mandatory to unlock the fourth and fifth cards.
3.  **Unknown Signal Missions:** After the main story, look for "Unknown Signal" prompts on your map. Clearing at least one of these is required to hit Shelter Level 5 and unlock the final tier of **pragmata diana skins**.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I miss any of the pragmata diana skins during a single playthrough?**

A: No, none of the skins are strictly missable. You can continue to earn Cabin Coins and complete Stamp Cards in the post-game. Even if you miss a "Blackout" reward initially, you can return to previous sectors to farm the necessary coins.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get Cabin Coins?**

A: The most efficient method is replaying Training Sim missions. These missions are designed to be speed-run, and higher rankings (S-Rank) provide a significant bonus to your coin yield.

**Q: Do the skins provide any gameplay advantages or stat boosts?**

A: In Pragmata, skins are purely cosmetic. They do not change Diana's health, speed, or abilities. They are intended for visual customization and enhancing the experience in the game's robust Photo Mode.

**Q: How do I change Diana's outfit once I unlock a new skin?**

A: You can change outfits at any time while inside the Shelter. Approach the wardrobe terminal near Diana's sleeping quarters to browse your unlocked collection and equip new gear.
