## How to Unlock and Access Outfit Customization

Before you can begin swapping between your various **pragmata deluxe outfits**, you must progress through the initial stages of the game. Customization is not available immediately upon starting a new save file. Instead, it is tied to the narrative progression of the prologue.

To access the wardrobe features, you must complete the opening sequences on Earth and successfully reach the lunar surface. Once you have navigated the initial hazards, you will unlock the **Shelter**. This location serves as your primary hub for upgrades, rest, and, most importantly, visual customization. 

> **Important Note:** If you purchased the Deluxe Edition or pre-ordered the game, your bonus content will be automatically flagged in your account, but you cannot equip them until the Shelter terminal is powered on.

### Steps to Change Your Costumes

Follow these specific steps once you have gained access to the Shelter:

1.  **Locate the Outfit Storage:** Navigate to the specialized section of the Shelter dedicated to gear maintenance. You will see a terminal labeled "Outfit Storage."
2.  **Interact with the Terminal:** Approach the terminal and press the **Y button** (on Xbox/Controller) or the **F key** (on Keyboard and Mouse).
3.  **Select Your Character:** The menu allows you to toggle between Here and Diana. 
4.  **Browse the Collection:** Use the D-pad or arrow keys to scroll through your available suits.
5.  **Confirm Selection:** Press the **A button** (or Enter) to equip the chosen outfit.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my pragmata deluxe outfits at any time during the game?**

A: No, you cannot change outfits in the middle of a mission or while exploring the lunar surface. You must return to the Shelter hub and use the Outfit Storage terminal to swap your gear.

**Q: Are there any stat bonuses associated with the Deluxe Edition clothes?**

A: The **pragmata deluxe outfits** are purely cosmetic and do not provide any gameplay advantages, such as increased health, faster movement, or better defense. They are designed to allow for visual personalization without affecting the game's balance.

**Q: How do I get the Neo Bushido and Neo Kunoichi outfits if I didn't pre-order?**

A: Typically, pre-order bonuses are made available as a separate "Legacy Pack" or "Pre-order Upgrade" on digital storefronts a few months after the initial 2026 launch. Check your platform's store for available DLC bundles.

**Q: Do the outfits appear in cutscenes?**

A: Yes, Pragmata uses real-time rendering for its cinematics. Any outfit you have equipped via the Outfit Storage terminal will be reflected in all story cutscenes, providing a consistent visual experience.
