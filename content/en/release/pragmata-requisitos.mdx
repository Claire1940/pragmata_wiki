## Advanced Performance and Ray Tracing Details

Pragmata is a technical showcase for Capcom. The game features a unique dual-character control system where you guide Hugh through physical obstacles while Diana manages digital threats. This simultaneous processing places a unique load on the CPU. If you find your frame rate dipping during "Hack and Slash" sequences, consider lowering the "AI Logic" or "Physics" settings in the options menu.

While checking the **pragmata requisitos**, many users overlook the importance of VRAM. The game utilizes high-resolution textures to depict the "remains of human civilization" on the moon. Having 8GB of VRAM is the baseline for high settings, but 12GB is the sweet spot for 1440p resolutions.

### Performance Modes Explained
1. **Performance Mode:** Prioritizes frame rate by scaling internal resolution. Ideal for users at the minimum spec level.
2. **Balanced Mode:** Offers a mix of high-quality shadows and stable 60fps performance.
3. **Quality Mode:** Maximize draw distance and texture filtering. Recommended only for RTX 30-series or RX 6000-series and above.

## Storage and Installation Insights

The game requires 40 GB of space, which is relatively modest for a 2026 AAA title. However, the data streaming requirements are intense. The game uses a "zero-loading" philosophy when transitioning from the lunar surface to the interior of the research stations.

> **💡 Tip:** Always install Pragmata on an NVMe SSD if possible. Traditional SATA SSDs are sufficient, but NVMe drives significantly reduce the occasional micro-stuttering seen during fast-travel or character-swapping animations.

If you are playing on a [Steam Deck](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3357650/PRAGMATA/), you will be pleased to know that the game is **Verified**. It runs remarkably well on the handheld using the "Low-Medium" hybrid preset, maintaining a stable 30-40 fps which is perfect for on-the-go lunar exploration.

## Gameplay Mechanics: Mastering Hugh and Diana

Successfully meeting the **pragmata requisitos** is only the first part of the journey. Once the game is running, you must master the synergy between the two protagonists. Hugh is your primary combatant, utilizing kinetic firearms and physical agility to navigate the lunar ruins. Diana, the mysterious android, provides utility by hacking environmental objects and creating shields.

- **Simultaneous Control:** You aren't just switching between them; you are often managing both. While Hugh provides cover fire, Diana may need to bypass a security terminal in real-time.
- **3D Printing Mineral:** The story revolves around a new mineral that allows for instant 3D printing. In gameplay, this translates to Hugh being able to "print" temporary cover or specialized ammunition on the fly.
- **Lunar Gravity:** The physics engine calculates movement based on low-gravity environments. Jumping and dodging feel floaty but precise, requiring a different timing than standard terrestrial action games.

## FAQ

**Q: Does Pragmata require an always-online connection?**

A: While the game is primarily a single-player experience, a broadband internet connection is required for initial activation (via Denuvo) and for accessing certain community-driven "Data Library" features.

**Q: Can I run Pragmata on Windows 10?**

A: The official **pragmata requisitos** specifically list Windows 11 (64-bit). While it may launch on Windows 10, Capcom does not officially support it, and you may experience compatibility issues or reduced performance with DirectX 12 Ultimate features.

**Q: Is there a demo available to test my PC performance?**

A: Yes, you can download the "PRAGMATA SKETCHBOOK" demo on Steam. It allows you to play through a short lunar research station segment to see how your hardware handles the combat and hacking systems before purchasing the full game.

**Q: What are the best settings for a mid-range PC?**

A: For a balanced experience on a mid-range rig (like an RTX 3060), set the Global Illumination to "Medium," enable DLSS or FSR in "Quality" mode, and keep Texture Quality at "High." This should maintain a steady 60 fps at 1080p.

**Q: Does the game support ultrawide monitors?**

A: Yes, Pragmata supports 21:9 and 32:9 aspect ratios natively, providing an even more cinematic view of the lunar landscape. Just ensure your GPU meets the recommended **pragmata requisitos** to handle the increased pixel count.
