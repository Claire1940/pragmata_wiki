## How the Outfits Affect Gameplay

It is important to clarify that the **pragmata deluxe edition outfits** do not alter the core mechanics of the game. Pragmata is built on a "systems-driven" engine, meaning the interactions between Hugh’s tools and the environment are governed by physics and logic.

*   **Combat:** Whether Hugh is wearing the Neo Bushido armor or his standard gear, his damage output and health pool remain the same.
*   **Exploration:** Diana’s movement speed and puzzle-solving abilities are unaffected by her choice of clothing.
*   **Cutscenes:** The game uses real-time rendering, so your chosen **pragmata deluxe edition outfits** will appear in all cinematic sequences, adding a layer of personal continuity to the story.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Some physical editions of the game may not include the pre-order DLC code inside the box. Always verify with your retailer that the "Hugh and Diana Outfit Set" is included if you are buying a physical copy for PS5 or Xbox Series X.

**Q: Are there any gameplay-altering items in the Deluxe Edition?**
