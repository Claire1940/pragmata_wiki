## The Final Confrontation: Defeating Eight

The path to the **pragmata fin** begins with a grueling battle against Eight, a character whose motivations are tied to the "purpose" of the Pragmatics. This encounter is a multi-phase fight that tests your coordination with Diana and your mastery of the suit's heavy weaponry. Unlike previous bosses, Eight utilizes "Alpha Centauri" protocols that can overwhelm your sensors if not managed correctly.

The fight is divided into three distinct phases, each requiring a shift in tactics. You must balance offensive pressure with defensive hacking, all while protecting Diana as she assists in charging the final blow.

> **Warning:** During the Cannon Charge phase, do not stop moving. Eight will launch a series of "out of control" Billamin attacks that can instantly deplete your suit's integrity if you remain stationary.

## Gameplay Strategies for the Final Escape

Reaching the **pragmata fin** involves a series of Quick Time Events (QTEs) and environmental puzzles. While the combat is over, the tension remains high as you navigate the crumbling tower. 

1. **Optimize Hacking:** When Diana asks to help, allow her to initiate the hacking matrix. This speeds up the door sequences and prevents unnecessary damage from environmental hazards.
2. **Manual Rail Push:** During the shuttle sequence, pay attention to Q's stamina bar. Do not spam the push button; instead, follow the rhythmic prompts to maximize efficiency without overheating the suit's servos.
3. **Dialogue Choices:** While the ending is largely linear, the dialogue choices you make throughout the game regarding Diana’s "humanity" will subtly alter the flavor of the final conversation on the shuttle.

> **Tip:** Make sure to look at the cargo ship's interior before the final push. There are several environmental storytelling cues that hint at the history of the lunar colony and the original purpose of the Pragmatics.

## FAQ

**Q: Is there a secret ending to the pragmata fin?**

A: While the main narrative path is consistent, players who collect all of Diana’s "Memory Fragments" throughout the game will unlock an extended post-credits scene that provides more context regarding the voice that greets her on Earth.

**Q: Can Q survive the ending?**

A: In the current 2026 version of the game, Q’s sacrifice is a fixed narrative point. He remains on the station to ensure the shuttle clears the gravity well. However, his final status is technically "unknown," as the station's destruction is not explicitly shown on screen.

**Q: What does "Pragmatic" actually mean in the game?**

A: Within the lore, Pragmatics appear to be advanced bio-mechanical entities or humans modified for deep-space survival. The conflict in the **pragmata fin** arises from the tension between those who see themselves as mere tools (like Eight) and those who seek independent life (like Diana and Q).

**Q: How do I beat the out-of-control Billamin?**

A: The Billamin in the final sequence is a test of evasion. Focus on using the environment for cover and wait for Diana to signal when the Hacking Matrix is ready. Once the matrix is active, the Billamin’s shield will drop, allowing you to use the Heavy Cannon to end the fight.
