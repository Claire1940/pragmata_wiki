A: Yes, the **プラグマタ 体験版 エンディング 条件** for Illustration No. 4 requires a "No Hit" run from the moment you gain control of Hyu until the final result screen. If you are hit at any point, including the boss encounter, you must restart the demo to try again.

**Q: Where can I view the illustrations I have already unlocked?**

**Q: Is the Elite Orange Robot spawn random?**

A: While it may seem random at first, the community has confirmed it is a deterministic spawn triggered by using three different kill methods (Standard Gun, Diana Ability, and Green Gun) on the first three enemies in the elevator. Following this specific sequence is the most reliable way to meet the requirement.
