## Facing the Executor Bots

While the **pragmata samurai skin** looks formidable, it does not make you invincible. The lunar environments are crawling with "Executor" bots—humanoid machines that utilize aggressive dash attacks and laser targeting. When wearing the samurai armor, you should adopt a "counter-striker" mentality.

The key to defeating these foes lies in their red antennas. These are their primary sensors and communication arrays. If an Executor bot jams your house or attempts a heavy charge, aiming for these antennas will stun the unit, allowing you to follow up with a high-speed Riot Blaster shot.
