## Technical Innovations and the RE Engine

One of the standout features mentioned during the **pragmata announcement** cycle is the use of the RE Engine. While originally designed for the Resident Evil series, the engine has been heavily modified to handle the vast, open-space environments and complex physics required for a lunar setting.

### Expected Technical Specifications

| Feature | Specification | Impact on Gameplay |
|---|---|---|
| **Ray Tracing** | Full Path Tracing | Realistic light bounces on lunar surfaces and glass. |
| **Physics** | Dynamic Gravity | Objects react differently based on atmospheric pressure. |
| **Audio** | 3D Spatial Sound | Essential for tracking enemies in silent vacuum environments. |
| **Loading** | Near-Instant | Seamless transitions between Earth-simulations and the Moon. |

The integration of ray tracing is particularly important for a game that features so many reflective surfaces, from the astronaut’s visor to the metallic structures of the lunar base. In 2026, these features are no longer just "nice-to-haves" but are central to the immersion that Capcom is striving for.

## Gameplay Mechanics: Protection and Exploration

While details were sparse following the **pragmata announcement**, subsequent updates have clarified that the game revolves around a dual-protagonist system. You control the armored astronaut, whose primary goal is to protect the young girl, Diana. This dynamic creates a "bodyguard" style of gameplay that requires strategic resource management.

1.  **Environmental Interaction**: Use the astronaut’s specialized tools to manipulate the environment and create safe paths for Diana.
2.  **Combat Systems**: Combat appears to be a mix of ranged weaponry and high-tech gadgets designed to neutralize robotic threats.
3.  **Resource Scavenging**: Collect materials from the lunar surface to upgrade your suit and Diana’s protective gear.
4.  **Puzzle Solving**: Many areas require the unique abilities of both characters to progress, emphasizing the bond between them.

> **Warning:** Ignoring the girl's safety during combat encounters can lead to an immediate mission failure. Always keep her within your line of sight.
