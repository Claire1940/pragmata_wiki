## Detailed Breakdown of Deluxe Content

For those considering the Deluxe Edition, the value lies in the **Shelter Variety Pack**. Since Pragmata is a single-player, narrative-driven game, these additions are meant to personalize your journey.

1.  **Additional Outfits:** Beyond the preorder bonuses, the Deluxe Edition offers even more clothing options for Hugh and Diana.
2.  **Grip Gun Skin:** A unique visual overhaul for Hugh’s primary utility tool, the Grip Gun.
3.  **Diana Gestures:** A set of emotes and animations for Diana, adding personality to your interactions in the lunar base.
4.  **BGM Tracks:** Alternate music that can be played while resting in the Shelter, featuring atmospheric lo-fi and synth-heavy tracks.
5.  **Small Artwork Data Library:** A digital gallery featuring concept art and world-building notes from the development of Pragmata.

> **Tip:** None of the Deluxe Edition content impacts game difficulty or progression. If you are on a tight budget, the Standard Edition provides the exact same gameplay experience.
