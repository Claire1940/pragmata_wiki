## Technical Specifications and Performance

Pragmata is built on a modified version of the RE Engine, which has been optimized for the unique lighting requirements of a lunar setting. Expect full support for ray-traced reflections and global illumination, which are essential for capturing the stark contrast between the darkness of space and the sterile, white corridors of the lunar base.

*   **Resolution:** 4K Target on PS5 and Xbox Series X.
*   **Frame Rate:** 60 FPS performance mode confirmed.
*   **Haptic Feedback:** Extensive support for DualSense triggers to simulate suit pressure and weapon recoil.

The game also utilizes advanced SSD streaming to eliminate loading screens between the vast lunar surface and the interior sections of the Cradle. This seamless transition is vital for the sense of scale Capcom is aiming for.
