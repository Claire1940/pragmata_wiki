## Power Restoration & Puzzle Solutions

Throughout the game, you will encounter emergency lockdowns. Restoring power is a recurring objective that often involves navigating multi-floor terminals. The process usually requires a manual reboot of the power plant controller, which can be guarded by aggressive AI.

1.  **Locate the Main Terminal:** Use your scan function to trace power lines through the walls.
2.  **Defend Diana:** While Diana is hacking the lift or terminal, you must protect her from incoming waves of bots.
3.  **Manual Reboot:** Interact with the terminal and complete the timing-based mini-game to restore energy levels to 100%.

Failure to protect the terminal during the "Unauthorized Access Detected" phase will result in a reset of the energy levels, making it harder to earn the "Efficiency Expert" trophy.

## Advanced Strategies for Hard Difficulty

To earn the Platinum, you must complete the game on the "Vanguard" difficulty setting. Enemies hit harder, and the EDS security is much more aggressive with missile barrages. 

*   **Prioritize Ranged Combat:** Use your long-distance weapons for bots that hover or fire missiles from afar. 
*   **Hack the Environment:** Use Diana to turn automated turrets against the enemies. This is often the only way to survive the Sector Guard encounters without depleting your ammo.
*   **Master the Dodge:** The invincibility frames on your dash are generous, but timing is everything when the screen is filled with projectiles.

## Troubleshooting Common Trophy Issues

Some players have reported that the "Master Hacker" trophy does not always trigger correctly. To ensure this unlocks, make sure you are the one initiating the hack command manually rather than letting the AI auto-trigger it during scripted sequences. 

Additionally, the "Speed of Light" trophy (completing the game in under 5 hours) is best attempted on New Game Plus. With your fully upgraded suit and knowledge of the map layouts, you can bypass most non-essential combat encounters to save time.

## FAQ

**Q: Are there any missable trophies in the Pragmata platinum guide?**

A: Yes, there are several missable trophies related to specific environmental interactions and "hidden" side objectives within the lunar base. It is recommended to keep multiple save files at the start of each major sector.

**Q: Can I earn the Platinum in a single playthrough?**

A: While technically possible if you start on the highest available difficulty, it is not recommended. A second playthrough on New Game Plus is much faster and allows you to focus on the speedrun and cleanup requirements without the stress of a first-time experience.

**Q: Does Diana need to survive every encounter for a specific trophy?**

A: There is a silver trophy called "Guardian Angel" which requires you to complete the game without Diana taking significant damage (her health bar must never turn red). This requires careful positioning and quick elimination of "leaper" type bots.

**Q: What is the hardest trophy in the Pragmata platinum guide?**

A: Most players find "Sector Guard Slayer" to be the most difficult. It requires defeating the final EDS protector on Vanguard difficulty without using any healing items during the fight. Success requires mastering the parry and overdrive systems.
