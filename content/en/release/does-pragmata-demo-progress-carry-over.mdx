## Gameplay Mechanics and Character Roles

The Pragmata demo introduces a unique dual-character dynamic. You primarily control Hugh Williams, a spacefarer who relies on traditional firearms and tactical movement. ### Combat and the Hacking System

The "Bots" found within the lunar station possess near-impenetrable armor. To defeat them, you must utilize Diana’s hacking skills. This involves a mini-game where you move a cursor through various panels toward a green goal. Depending on the path you take through the nodes, you can significantly alter the outcome of the battle.

| Node Type | Effect on Hack | Strategic Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Green Goal** | Success | Exposes the Bot's weak points for Hugh to shoot. |
| **Damage Nodes** | Increased DPS | Deals immediate chip damage to the Bot's core. |
| **Stun Nodes** | Crowd Control | Temporarily disables the Bot's offensive capabilities. |
| **Energy Nodes** | Resource Gain | Refills Diana's hacking meter for subsequent uses. |

By mastering these nodes during the demo, you prepare yourself for the much more complex encounters in the full 2026 release. The split-second decisions made during a hack can mean the difference between a successful "Overdrive Protocol" and a quick trip back to the last checkpoint.
