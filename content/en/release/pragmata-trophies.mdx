### Step 1: Story Completion and Basic Exploration
Your first priority should be completing the main campaign. ## Combat and Skill-Based Pragmata Trophies

The combat in Pragmata is tactical, often requiring you to manage resources while keeping Diana safe. Several trophies are tied to specific combat feats and maximizing your gear's potential. To earn these **pragmata trophies**, you must experiment with different weapon types and environmental hazards.

| Trophy Name | Requirement | Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Protocol Breaker** | Defeat 50 IDUS units using environmental traps. | Silver |
| **Lunar Ace** | Maintain a 50x combo during a single encounter. | Silver |
| **Unregistered Legend** | Complete the game on "Extinction" difficulty. | Gold |
| **Guardian Spirit** | Complete a major combat encounter without Diana taking damage. | Silver |

To unlock the "Unregistered Legend" trophy, you must have a deep understanding of the enemy attack patterns. The IDUS units are particularly aggressive on higher difficulties, and their presence is a constant violation of your safety. Utilizing the "Cradle" defense mechanisms against them is often more effective than direct confrontation.

## Exploration and Collectible Guide

Pragmata’s world is filled with hidden details that harken back to life on Earth. Finding these is the primary way to earn the exploration-themed **pragmata trophies**. Most of these items are located in off-the-beaten-path areas of the lunar base, often hidden behind puzzles that require Diana's assistance to solve.

### Finding the "House with a View"
One of the most poignant trophies involves finding the protagonist's simulated Earth home. This area contains multiple collectibles and provides a significant amount of lore.

1.  **Locate the Sector 7 Access Port:** This is found after the second IDUS boss fight.
2.  **Disable the Security Protocol:** Use the terminal near the airlock to bypass the Cradle's lockdown.
3.  **Search the Living Room:** Look for the "Faded Photograph" on the mantle to trigger a unique dialogue sequence.
4.  **Check the Balcony:** The view of the simulated sea is essential for the "Nostalgia" trophy.

> **💡 Tip:** Use the scanner frequently. Collectibles emit a faint blue pulse that can be seen through walls once you have upgraded your visor to Level 3.

## Advanced Strategies for Platinum

To truly master the **pragmata trophies** list, you must optimize your playstyle for efficiency. The "Extinction" difficulty run is the biggest hurdle for most players. On this setting, health is scarce, and enemies are significantly more resilient to standard attacks.

| Strategy Component | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Weapon** | Focus on the **Pulse Rifle** for long-range safety. |
| **Diana's Abilities** | Prioritize **Shield Projection** to minimize chip damage. |
| **Resource Management** | Save **Energy Cells** exclusively for boss encounters. |
| **Movement** | Use the **Low-G Dash** to avoid IDUS area-of-effect attacks. |

The "Cradle Security Protocol" trophies are often the last ones players earn. These require you to interact with specific terminals scattered throughout the game. If you miss one in the early chapters, you will likely need to start a New Game+ run to collect it. Fortunately, the game tracks your progress across multiple playthroughs, making the hunt for all **pragmata trophies** much more manageable.

## FAQ

**Q: Are there any missable pragmata trophies?**

A: Yes, several trophies related to specific environmental interactions and "Cradle" security terminals can be missed if you progress the story too quickly. It is recommended to explore each sector thoroughly before moving to the next.

**Q: How many playthroughs are required for the Platinum?**

A: A minimum of two playthroughs is generally required. One for the initial story and collectibles, and a second on the highest difficulty setting to unlock the "Unregistered Legend" trophy.

**Q: Can I earn trophies in New Game+?**

A: Yes, Pragmata fully supports trophy progression in New Game+. This is the best way to clean up combat-related achievements and max out your gear upgrades.

**Q: What is the hardest trophy in the game?**

A: Most players find "Unregistered Legend" (completing the game on the highest difficulty) to be the most challenging, followed by "Lunar Ace," which requires a very high combat combo that can be easily broken by the aggressive IDUS AI.
