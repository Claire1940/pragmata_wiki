## Analyzing the Pragmata Girl Age Theories

Determining the exact **pragmata girl age** requires looking past her physical shell. Some environmental logs found within the Cradle facility suggest that Diana may have been active for decades. If she was created during the early days of the Luna research station, her chronological age could be 30 or 40 years, even if her "body" has been maintained in a child-like state. This would explain her deep knowledge of "Old Earth," a place she speaks of with a nostalgia that a seven-year-old shouldn't possess.

As a "Pragmata" species android, Diana might not age in the traditional sense. Instead, her "age" could be measured in software iterations. ### 3. The Biological-Digital Hybrid

## The PEGI 16 Rating and Diana’s Safety

Despite her appearance, *Pragmata* is rated PEGI 16. This rating is due to "strong realistic violence" depicted throughout the game. While Diana is an android, she is frequently targeted by the Cradle's rogue AI guardians.

In certain cutscenes, Diana can suffer significant damage. However, because she is a Pragmata android, these injuries do not show blood or gore. Instead, players will see antimatter and complex circuitry when her limbs are damaged. This distinction is crucial for understanding the **pragmata girl age**—she is a machine built to look like a child, making the violence against her a narrative tool to highlight the cold, mechanical nature of the lunar colony.

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize Diana’s positioning during "Cradle Quakes." If she is separated from Hugh, her hacking abilities are temporarily disabled, making combat significantly harder.
