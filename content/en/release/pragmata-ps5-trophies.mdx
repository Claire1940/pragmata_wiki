## Exploration and Collectibles

Capcom has hidden numerous data logs and artifacts throughout the game world. To secure the Platinum, you must find all "Memories of Earth" items. These collectibles provide lore regarding the house with a view and the history of the lunar colony.

Follow these steps to ensure you find every collectible:
*   **Scan every room**: Use your suit's visor to highlight interactable objects.
*   **Backtrack after upgrades**: Some areas are locked behind security levels that require late-game clearance.
*   **Listen for audio cues**: Diana will often hum or point toward nearby points of interest.

The **pragmata ps5 trophies** list includes several achievements for finding specific sets of items. For example, the "Historian of the Moon" trophy requires finding all 50 data logs scattered across the five main sectors of the Cradle.

## Advanced Strategies for Platinum

For the "Hardcore Protocol" trophy, which requires completing the game on the highest difficulty setting, you must master the parry system. The IDUS units have predictable patterns, but their damage output is significantly increased on higher tiers.

*   **Cleanup Phase**: Use Chapter Select to find any missed **pragmata ps5 trophies** related to specific combat feats or hidden interactions with Diana.
*   **Final Run**: Start a New Game+ on "Cradle Overload" difficulty. Your previous upgrades will carry over, making the early game much more manageable.

## FAQ

**Q: Are there any missable pragmata ps5 trophies?**

A: Yes, several trophies related to Diana's interactions and specific environmental kills can be missed if you progress past certain "Points of No Return." However, the game features a robust Chapter Select menu after the first completion, allowing you to go back and clean up missed achievements.

**Q: How long does it take to get the Platinum trophy?**

A: Depending on your skill level and how much time you spend exploring, the Platinum typically takes between 35 and 50 hours. A focused second run on New Game+ can be completed much faster once you know the enemy patterns.

**Q: Can I unlock trophies while playing in offline mode?**

A: Yes, all **pragmata ps5 trophies** can be earned while playing offline. However, you will need to sync with the PlayStation Network once you go back online to have them appear on your public profile.

**Q: Is there a trophy for completing the game without dying?**

A: While there is no trophy for a literal "Zero Death" run, there is a Gold trophy for completing the game on the highest difficulty with fewer than five total continues. This is widely considered the hardest trophy in the game.
