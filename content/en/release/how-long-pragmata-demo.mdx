## Gameplay Mechanics and Demo Content

The Pragmata demo is more than just a walking simulator; it introduces several core pillars of the gameplay loop. You play as Hugh Williams, a suit-clad investigator navigating a lunar base. The demo focuses on the "Cradle" incident, where Hugh meets Diana. Together, they must navigate a solar power plant to call Earth for assistance.

### Hacking and Combat
The combat in Pragmata is unconventional. Instead of traditional "run and gun" mechanics, you must utilize a hacking matrix to make enemies vulnerable. By holding the mouse button (or controller trigger) and connecting nodes to an "EXE" node, you place enemies in an "Open" state. Once hacked, they become susceptible to gunfire from your primary unit or the powerful **Shockwave Gun**.

### Resource Management and Upgrades
Throughout the demo, you will encounter a mineral known as **Lunum**. This resource produces Luna filament, which is used for suit upgrades and equipment enhancements in the shelter. Collecting these is vital for players who want to see how the progression system functions in the full 2026 release.

> **💡 Tip:** Use the Scan function (C key) frequently. Diana will highlight nearby resources and hacking nodes that are otherwise easy to miss in the dark corridors of the power plant.

## Performance Benchmarks: RT On vs. RT Off

Testing the demo on various configurations reveals that the RE Engine thrives in the closed-in environments of the solar plant. On an RTX 3060 at 1080p, players can expect an average of 70-80 FPS with Ray Tracing enabled. Turning Ray Tracing off bumps the performance by roughly 15-20%, though the visual trade-off is noticeable.

### Ray Traced Reflections
With RT enabled, the glass surfaces and metallic floors of the Cradle reflect the player character and the environment with high accuracy. While some "graininess" typical of RE Engine reflections is present, it is far less distracting than in previous titles like *Dragon's Dogma 2*.

### Upscaling Technologies
Pragmata supports the latest versions of DLSS and FSR. In the 2026 demo, FSR 3.1.4 (and its machine-learning-based successors) allows AMD users to achieve high frame rates even at 1440p. On a 960 XT, using "Balanced" upscaling can push the frame rate well over 100 FPS, providing a buttery-smooth experience for high-refresh-rate monitors.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Shader compilation occurs the first time you launch the game. Do not skip this process, or you will experience significant stuttering during the opening cutscenes and combat encounters.

## Controls and Maneuverability

Hugh's suit is equipped with a thruster gauge that governs your mobility. Managing this gauge is the difference between life and death during the demo's final combat arena.

1.  **Dodge (Ctrl):** Consumes a small portion of the gauge to avoid incoming projectiles.
2.  **Jump (Space):** A standard vertical leap.
3.  **Hover (Hold Space):** Allows you to stay airborne, which is useful for avoiding ground-based shockwaves.
4.  **Repair (Hold Q):** Uses a repair cartridge to restore suit integrity. Note that this takes several seconds and leaves you vulnerable.

The hacking mechanic also requires precision. You must prompt buttons (WASD) in order from the outer ring inward while holding the interact button. It is a rhythmic puzzle that adds a layer of tension to the otherwise standard third-person shooting.

## Expert Tips for Completing the Demo

To get the most out of your time in the Pragmata trial, follow these strategic suggestions:

*   **Pathing through Yellow Nodes:** During the hacking phase, look for yellow nodes. Passing through these increases your damage dealt to that specific enemy. Repeated use on the same enemy reduces effectiveness, so spread your hacks across the group.
*   **Shockwave Gun Range:** The Shockwave Gun is a frontal suppression tool. It is incredibly powerful for staggering enemies, but its effectiveness drops off sharply with distance. Save it for when enemies close the gap.
*   **VRAM Management:** If you are playing on an 8GB card (like the 5060 Ti 8GB), consider disabling Ray Tracing if you notice textures failing to load. The game is still visually impressive at native resolutions without RT.
*   **Check the Solar Plant Top Floor:** The demo ends shortly after reaching the top floor. Make sure you have explored all side rooms for Lunum before interacting with the final power console.
