## The Pragmata Final Boss Encounter: Dead Billamin

The ultimate challenge in the game is the confrontation with the out-of-control "Dead Billamin." After the confrontation with Connie and Eight, the entity becomes a mindless, destructive force that threatens to prevent your escape to Earth. This fight is unique because it relies heavily on the synergy between Hue’s combat capabilities and Diana’s supernatural hacking and support abilities.

The battle takes place near the transport rail, requiring you to defend a cargo shuttle while preparing a massive railgun-style cannon to finish the beast.

## Combat Mechanics: Using the Cannon

To succeed in the **pragmata final** battle, you must master the rhythmic timing of the specialized cannon. Unlike standard weapons used earlier in the game, this device requires a synchronized "Lock and Shoot" mechanic.

1.  **Charging:** Hold the secondary action button to allow Diana to funnel energy into the weapon.
2.  **Locking:** Wait for the reticle to turn red. Shooting early deals negligible damage and resets the cooldown.
3.  **The Killing Blow:** In the final phase, you must wait for the "Incoming Alpha Centauri" prompt to time your shot with the environmental shift.

## FAQ

**Q: Can Hue survive the Pragmata final sequence?**

A: In the standard True Ending path of the 2026 release, Hue’s sacrifice is a scripted narrative event. While he does not accompany Diana to Earth, some lore theorists point to the final "Welcome Back" message as a hint that his consciousness may have been uploaded to the Earth-side network.

**Q: How do I beat the Dead Billamin's shield phase?**

A: You cannot break the shield with standard weapons. You must wait for Diana to announce that the "Hacking Matrix" is optimized. Once she gives the signal, initiate the hack and defend her from the incoming Alpha Centauri spores until the shield drops.

**Q: What happens if I choose to "Stay with Connie" earlier in the game?**

A: This choice leads to an early game over or a "False Ending." To see the **pragmata final** boss and the journey to Earth, you must choose to "Find your own purpose" when prompted during the tower confrontation.

**Q: Are there multiple endings based on performance?**

A: While the main narrative beats remain the same, your performance during the final boss influences the dialogue in the credits. A "Perfect" rating (taking minimal damage) unlocks an additional post-credits scene featuring a holographic message Hue left for Diana.
