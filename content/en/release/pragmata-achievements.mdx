## The Shelter and Upgrade System

A significant portion of the **pragmata achievements** are linked to the "Shelter," your central hub for repairs and upgrades. Here, you can use resources gathered during missions to enhance your suit, weapons, and Diana's abilities. Upgrading the "Starting Hat" and the "Cube" utility is vital for surviving the later, more difficult stages of the game.

Strategic **pragmata achievements** in this category often require "Max Level" status for specific gear. For instance, the "Full Arsenal" trophy requires every weapon type—from the heavy pulse rifle to the tactical mines—to be upgraded to their final tier. This requires a significant amount of "Lunar Credits" and rare materials found in hidden areas of the map.

### Key Equipment Upgrades

1.  **Suit Integrity**: Increasing your health and shield capacity is essential for the "Indestructible" achievement (completing a mission on Hard without dying).
2.  **Diana’s Hacking Rig**: Upgrading Diana allows her to interact with more complex machinery, which is often required to reach missable **pragmata achievements** located behind locked doors.
3.  **Jump-Jet Efficiency**: Better thrusters allow for longer "Out of Bound" exploration, which is where many of the game's secret collectibles are hidden.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Some upgrade materials are limited per playthrough. Focus on your preferred playstyle first to ensure you don't get stuck on a difficult boss while chasing a specific gear achievement.

## Speedrunning and Efficiency

For the most dedicated players, the "Lunar Speedster" achievement is the ultimate test. Inspired by the speedrunning community, this trophy requires you to finish the main story in under five hours. While this may seem daunting, the game's movement mechanics, such as "Cancel Jumps" and "Slide Boosts," make it possible to bypass large sections of the environment.

Players aiming for these time-sensitive **pragmata achievements** should focus on skipping optional combat encounters and memorizing puzzle solutions. Using Diana’s "Pathfinder" ability can also highlight the quickest route to the next objective, saving precious minutes during a run.

| Speedrun Milestone | Target Time | Tips |

## FAQ

**Q: Are there any missable pragmata achievements in a single playthrough?**

A: Yes, several achievements related to specific combat feats and collectible sets can be missed if you progress past certain "Points of No Return." It is highly recommended to keep multiple save slots or utilize the Chapter Select feature unlocked after the first completion.

**Q: Does difficulty affect the pragmata achievements list?**

A: Yes, there are specific achievements for completing the game on "Hard" and "Real Survival" modes. These modes significantly increase enemy damage and limit the resources found in the lunar environment.

**Q: Can I unlock achievements while using gameplay modifiers or cheats?**

A: Typically, using "Assist Modes" or certain "Infinite Ammo" unlocks (if available) will disable the ability to earn S-rank related achievements and difficulty-based trophies. Always check the modifier description before activating it.

**Q: Is there a co-op achievement for Pragmata?**

A: While Pragmata is primarily a single-player experience focused on the bond between the protagonist and Diana, some "Asynchronous" features may have associated trophies, such as viewing other players' "Ghost" data or lunar messages.
