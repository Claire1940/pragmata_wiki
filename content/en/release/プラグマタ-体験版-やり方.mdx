## Demo Walkthrough: The Solar Power Plant

The "Sketchbook" demo follows a linear path that introduces the primary traversal and combat mechanics. Most players will finish their first run in approximately 30 minutes, but the **プラグマタ 体験版 やり方** encourages multiple playthroughs to unlock hidden gear.

### Phase 1: Environmental Hacking
The demo begins in the lower levels of the lunar facility. You must use your hacking pulse to activate power terminals. This involves a mini-game where you align frequency waves while fending off small robotic drones.

### Phase 2: The Vertical Shaft
After activating the first three terminals, you will enter a large vertical shaft. Here, the "unique sense of floating" mentioned by Capcom becomes apparent. You must use jump pads and hacking-controlled elevators to ascend. Keep an eye out for "Lead Earth Memories"—collectible items that provide lore about the facility's collapse.

### Phase 3: The Sector Guard Boss
The demo concludes with a fight against the Sector Guard. 
1.  **Break the Shield**: Use Hacking Nodes scattered around the arena to lower the boss's digital barrier.
2.  **Focus Fire**: Once the shield is down, target the glowing core on its back.
3.  **Dodge the Laser**: When the boss charges its central eye, hide behind the pillars or use a Stasis Net to interrupt the attack.
