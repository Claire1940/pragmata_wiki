## Diana’s Role in Gameplay and Combat

In *Pragmata*, Diana is not merely an NPC to be protected; she is a functional partner. Her role changes based on the level of "Sync" you have achieved with her. When exploring the lunar base, she can identify hidden caches of resources and warn the player of incoming security drones.

### Key Gameplay Mechanics with Diana
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Diana can phase through certain barriers that the protagonist's heavy armor cannot.
*   **Security Override:** She can temporarily disable "Cradle" security protocols, allowing for stealthy passage through high-threat zones.
*   **Resource Pinging:** Diana will point out rare materials needed for upgrading the protagonist's suit and weaponry.

> **Warning:** If Diana is separated from the protagonist for too long, the "Sync" meter will drop, causing her to become vulnerable to IDUS interference. Always maintain a close proximity during combat encounters.
