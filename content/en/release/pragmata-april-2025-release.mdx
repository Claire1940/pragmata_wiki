## FAQ

**Q: Is Pragmata a sequel to any existing Capcom franchise?**

A: No, it is a completely new intellectual property. While fans have compared its atmosphere to games like *Resident Evil* or *Dino Crisis*, it is a standalone sci-fi adventure with its own unique lore and mechanics.

**Q: What happened during the Pragmata April 2025 release window?**

A: April 2025 was the period when Capcom transitioned the game from its long development silence into a public-facing launch phase, including a significant gameplay demo that introduced the "Cradle" setting and the thermal management systems.

**Q: Can you play the game entirely offline?**

A: Yes, the core campaign is a single-player experience. However, there are online "Echo" features where players can see the thermal paths taken by others, helping them navigate dangerous sections of the lunar colony.

**Q: Is Diana a playable character?**

A: While you primarily control the armored protagonist, certain segments allow you to utilize Diana's abilities directly to solve puzzles. In the 2026 updates, her role in combat has become much more active, though she remains an AI-driven partner for the majority of the game.
