## FAQ

**Q: Does a pragmata diana death result in a permanent Game Over?**

A: In standard difficulty, a **pragmata diana death** will reset you to the nearest checkpoint. However, in "Lunar Hardcore" mode, her death can lead to a permanent save file corruption or a "Bad Ending" state where Hugh must finish the journey alone.

**Q: What does the "Transferring dead body" line mean?**

A: This line indicates that Diana's physical form has been destroyed. Because she is a unique entity, her consciousness can be "transferred" back to the hub, but the mission is considered a failure because the physical progress on the moon is lost.

**Q: Can I heal Diana during combat?**

A: Yes, Hugh can use "Nano-Repair Sprays" to restore Diana's health. It is highly recommended to keep at least three in your inventory at all times to prevent a sudden **pragmata diana death** during boss encounters.

**Q: Who is Hugh Williams in relation to Diana?**
