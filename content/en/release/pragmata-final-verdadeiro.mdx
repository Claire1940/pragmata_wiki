## Mastering the Alpha Centauri Encounter

As you approach the limit of the Alpha Centauri sector, the game shifts from traditional exploration to a frantic survival shooter. ## Diana's Sacrifice and the Return to Earth

The emotional climax of the **pragmata final verdadeiro** occurs when the automated systems of the cargo shuttle fail. Because the explosions damaged the rail's automation, Hue is forced to make a choice. He cannot board the shuttle if he wants to ensure Diana reaches Earth safely.

## Final Boss Strategy: The Billamin Remnant

The final boss is a "dead" Billamin that has gone out of control without Eight's guidance. This creature acts as a physical manifestation of the colony's decaying logic.

*   **Avoid the Red Orbs:** These drain your energy and prevent Diana from hacking.

*   **Hacking Matrix:** When the screen turns blue, follow the prompts to assist Diana in her optimization. This will temporarily stun the boss, allowing for a "Lock On" attack.
