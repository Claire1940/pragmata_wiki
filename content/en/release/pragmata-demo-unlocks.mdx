## Combat Mechanics and Weapon Variety

To successfully navigate the **pragmata demo unlocks**, you need to understand the behavior of the three primary tools at your disposal. Each weapon has a specific "impact" value that determines how quickly an enemy reaches the destruction threshold.

### The Pistol (Standard Issue)
The pistol is your most reliable tool for precision damage. In the context of the elevator puzzle, it is best used for the first bot because it has the lowest risk of "over-damaging" and accidentally destroying the second or third bots prematurely.

### The Shockwave Gun (Heavy Utility)
This weapon deals significant area-of-effect damage. When attempting the secret unlock, ensure the other bots are at a safe distance before using the Shockwave Gun to finish your target. If the splash damage kills a second bot, the "different weapon" streak is broken.

### The Hack Command
The Hack is more than just a finishing move; it’s a tactical tool that can turn enemies against each other. However, for the purpose of the **pragmata demo unlocks**, you should use it strictly as a finisher for the third bot. This ensures the game registers a "Hack Kill" as the unique third damage source.

> **Tip:** If you are struggling with the timing, try kiting the bots to opposite corners of the elevator. This prevents accidental multi-kills and allows you to focus on the specific weapon requirement for each unit.

## Troubleshooting Your Unlocks

If you have followed the "three weapon" rule and the secret enemy still hasn't appeared, check the following common issues:
* **Environmental Damage:** If a bot dies by falling off the elevator or hitting a hazard, it does not count as a weapon kill.
* **Status Effects:** If a bot dies from residual shock damage while you have a different weapon equipped, the game may misidentify the source of the killing blow.
* **Check the UI:** Watch the "Intruders Detected" message. If it stays white, you haven't triggered the secret. If it turns red and flashes, the hidden bot is about to spawn.
