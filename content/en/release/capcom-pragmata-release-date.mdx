## Gameplay Mechanics: Astronauts and Androids

Pragmata is a third-person science fiction action-adventure that centers on the relationship between two distinct protagonists: Hugh, a heavily armored astronaut, and Diana, a mysterious android girl with reality-warping capabilities. The gameplay loop focuses on "dual-brain" combat, where players must manage Hugh’s heavy ballistics and Diana’s advanced hacking skills simultaneously.

### The Lunar Research Station
The game takes place on a cold, abandoned lunar research station known as "Cradle." Following a massive "moonquake" that damaged the station's Environmental Defense System (EDS), the duo must fight their way through rogue security protocols and robotic "unregistered guest" hunters to reach Earth.

### Key Combat Features
*   **Hacking Twist:** Diana can intercept enemy signals to turn robotic foes against each other or disable environmental hazards.
*   **Heavy Ordnance:** Hugh utilizes a variety of futuristic firearms, including the "Grip Gun DS," to deal direct damage.
*   **Cooperative Synergy:** Many puzzles and boss encounters require Hugh to protect Diana while she performs critical system overrides.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the official capcom pragmata release date?**

A: The game is officially scheduled to release on April 17, 2026, for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, and Switch 2. The PC version on Steam will unlock on April 16, 2026.

**Q: Is Pragmata a sequel to an existing Capcom franchise?**

A: No, Pragmata is a completely new intellectual property (IP) for Capcom. It is an original science fiction adventure that is not narratively linked to Resident Evil, Devil May Cry, or Monster Hunter.

**Q: Can I play Pragmata on PlayStation 4 or Xbox One?**

A: No, Pragmata is designed exclusively for current-generation hardware (PS5, Xbox Series X/S) and high-end PCs to take advantage of advanced rendering techniques and fast loading speeds.

**Q: Does the game feature multiplayer or co-op?**

A: Pragmata is primarily a single-player action-adventure. While you control two characters (Hugh and Diana), the gameplay is designed for a single player to switch between or command both protagonists simultaneously. There is currently no confirmed online co-op mode.
