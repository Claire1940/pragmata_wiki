## PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S Features

Console players are not being left behind in terms of technical sophistication. Capcom has confirmed that the **pragmata plataformas** on the console side will feature dedicated modes to prioritize either visual quality or fluid motion. 

On the PlayStation 5, the game fully utilizes the DualSense controller’s haptic feedback and adaptive triggers. Players will feel the resistance of the lunar surface through the triggers and the subtle vibrations of the protagonist's life-support systems. Xbox Series X players will benefit from Quick Resume, allowing them to jump back into the lunar wasteland in seconds.

### Console Performance Modes

1.  **Fidelity Mode**: Focuses on native 4K resolution with maximum ray-tracing settings and enhanced crowd density, locked at 30 FPS.
2.  **Performance Mode**: Targets a steady 60 FPS by utilizing dynamic resolution scaling and reduced ray-tracing effects.
3.  **Balanced Mode**: Available for 120Hz displays, targeting 40 FPS to provide a smoother feel than Fidelity mode without sacrificing as much visual clarity.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Playing in Fidelity Mode on the Xbox Series S may result in significant resolution drops during intense combat sequences. Performance Mode is recommended for a more consistent experience.

## The Role of the RE Engine in 2026

The RE Engine has evolved significantly since its debut with Resident Evil 7. In Pragmata, the engine handles massive open-scale environments on the moon, which is a departure from the claustrophobic corridors of Capcom's horror titles. The engine's ability to scale across different **pragmata plataformas** is what makes the simultaneous release possible.

Key technical pillars of the RE Engine in Pragmata include:
*   **Real-time Global Illumination**: Essential for the stark lighting conditions of the lunar surface.
*   **Advanced Particle Systems**: Used for the "cataclysm" events where digital reality and physical space collide.
*   **AI-Driven Animation**: Ensuring that the character Diana interacts realistically with the environment and the player.

For more information on the development of this engine, you can visit the [official Capcom developer portal](https://www.capcom.com/us/) to see how they approach multi-platform optimization.

## Why Pragmata Skips Previous Generation Consoles

A common question among fans is whether the game will eventually see a "legacy" release. However, the architecture of the **pragmata plataformas** chosen for this release suggests that a downgrade is highly unlikely. The game relies heavily on high-bandwidth memory and rapid asset decompression.

The CPUs in the PS4 and Xbox One are simply unable to process the complex physics calculations required for the game’s destructible environments. Furthermore, the lack of hardware-accelerated ray tracing on older machines would fundamentally change the visual identity of the game, which Capcom is unwilling to compromise.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Pragmata coming to Nintendo Switch?**

A: There is currently no version of Pragmata planned for the standard Nintendo Switch due to the hardware's technical limitations. However, a "Cloud Version" or a release on the rumored Switch successor in late 2026 remains a possibility.

**Q: Does the PC version support ultra-wide monitors?**

A: Yes, Capcom has a history of supporting 21:9 and 32:9 aspect ratios in the RE Engine. The various **pragmata plataformas** on PC will include full support for ultra-wide resolutions and FOV adjustments.

**Q: Can I play Pragmata offline?**

A: While the game features online community elements, the core story campaign is fully playable offline once the initial activation is complete.

**Q: Will there be a physical release for all pragmata plataformas?**

A: Physical editions are confirmed for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X. The PC version will be a digital-only release primarily distributed through Steam and the Capcom Store.
