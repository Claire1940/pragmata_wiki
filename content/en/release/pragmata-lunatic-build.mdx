## Why Choose a Heat-Based Strategy?

In *Pragmata*, combat variety is vast, but few methods offer the same level of crowd control and burst damage as a dedicated heat build. The core loop involves building up heat on enemies until they overheat, leaving them vulnerable to powerful reposts. This strategy not only clears smaller mobs quickly but also makes short work of tougher bosses.

### Essential Hacking Nodes for Success

Your hacking setup is the foundation of this build. To succeed on Lunatic difficulty, you must prioritize nodes that amplify your heat generation.

| Hacking Node | Effect | Role in Build |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Combust Mode** | Converts blue nodes to heat gauge build-up | Primary damage enabler |
| **Heat Node (Lv 3)** | +500 Heat Build-up | Essential for rapid overheating |
| **Decode Node** | 1.5x Damage Multiplier | General DPS boost |
| **Expose Node** | Increases critical damage on weak points | Boss-killing utility |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Because the Heat Node (Level 3) is so potent, you are limited to two stacks. Always reserve these charges for elite enemies or bosses to ensure you aren't caught without your primary damage buff during critical moments.

## Weapon and Tactical Synergy

Your primary weapon selection should favor high-heat generation over raw bullet damage. The Pulse Carbine is the gold standard here, providing nearly five times the heat build-up compared to the standard pistol. 

### Recommended Arsenal
*   **Primary:** Pulse Carbine (Consistent heat accumulation).
*   **Long-Range:** Charge Piercer (Precision for distant threats).
*   **Close-Quarters:** Shockwave Shotgun (Burst damage and knockdown).
*   **Tactical Units:** Stasis Net (Crowd control) and Riot Blaster (Damage/Knockdown).
*   **Defense:** Drone Hive (Passive damage support for managing large groups).

For more information on the game's mechanics, visit the [official Pragmata website](https://www.pragmata.com) to stay updated on future patches and balance changes.

## Mastering the Playstyle

The key to this build is the "chaining" effect. Use your Stasis Net to lock down problematic robots, then switch to the Pulse Carbine to rapidly fill their heat gauge. Once they are exposed, execute the repost animation. If you are surrounded, the Collateral Damage mod ensures that your repost damage ripples through nearby units, effectively clearing groups in seconds.

## FAQ

**Q: Is this pragmata lunatic build viable for early-game players?**

A: While some mods like "Unknown Signal" are locked until you beat the game once, the core structure—using the Pulse Carbine and the Heat hacking node—is highly effective from the moment you unlock them. Focus on the base Heat and Decode nodes early on.

**Q: Why choose the Pulse Carbine over the Photon Laser?**

A: The Pulse Carbine offers superior heat build-up scaling. When combined with the Sympathetic Hacking mod, you can keep the Carbine firing almost indefinitely, whereas the laser’s downtime often breaks the flow of the combat loop.

**Q: How do I handle bosses that are resistant to hacking?**

A: If you encounter an enemy that is difficult to hack, shift your focus to the Charge Piercer. Aim strictly for weak points to maximize the Target Weakness mod, and rely on the Drone Hive to provide steady, passive damage while you dodge incoming attacks.

**Q: What is the best way to practice this build?**

A: New Game Plus is the ideal environment to refine your timing. Because you retain your mod slots, you can experiment with the specific rotation of quick-hacks and reloads without the stress of limited resources.
