## Troubleshooting Common Connection Issues

Even with the best setup, cloud gaming can occasionally encounter hiccups. If you experience blurring or "blocky" graphics while using **pragmata geforce now**, it is likely due to network congestion or bandwidth throttling.

*   **Check Background Apps:** Ensure that no other devices on your network are performing heavy downloads or streaming 4K video.
*   **Server Selection:** While the "Auto" server setting is usually best, manually selecting a data center with the lowest ping can sometimes resolve persistent lag.
*   **ISP Throttling:** Some internet service providers may throttle long-duration UDP streams. Using a gaming-optimized VPN can occasionally bypass these restrictions, though it may add a few milliseconds of latency.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid playing on public Wi-Fi networks (like those in cafes or airports) for this specific game. The high-action sequences require more stability than most public hotspots can provide.
