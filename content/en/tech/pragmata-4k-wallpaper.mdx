## How to Set Up Your Pragmata 4K Wallpaper

Setting up a 4K background is straightforward, but to ensure the best quality on your 2026 hardware, follow these specific steps:

1.  **Download the Raw File:** Always choose the "Download Original" option rather than "Save Image As" from a preview.
2.  **Match Resolution:** Ensure your display settings match the wallpaper resolution. If you have a 1440p monitor, a 4K wallpaper will still look better than 1080p due to downsampling.
3.  **Disable Compression:** In Windows 11 and later, the OS sometimes compresses JPEG wallpapers. To avoid this, convert your 4K image to a PNG or TIFF format before setting it as your background.
4.  **Multi-Monitor Spanning:** If you have two monitors, look for "Dual 4K" Pragmata images that span 7680 pixels in width for a seamless transition.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be cautious of "Free Wallpaper" sites that require you to download an .exe file to get the image. Native images should only ever be in .jpg, .png, or .webp formats.
