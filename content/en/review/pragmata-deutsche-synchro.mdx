## How to Change Language Settings

If your game didn't automatically start with the **pragmata deutsche synchro** enabled, or if you wish to experiment with different audio tracks, follow these steps to adjust your settings:

1.  Navigate to the **Main Menu** or open the **Pause Menu** during gameplay.
2.  Select the **Options** (Optionen) tab.
3.  Go to **Language Settings** (Spracheinstellungen).
4.  Under **Voice Language** (Sprachausgabe), cycle through until you find **German** (Deutsch).
5.  Ensure **Subtitles** (Untertitel) are toggled to "On" if you want text support.
6.  Save your changes and return to the game.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Changing the voice language during an active mission may require the game to reload to the last checkpoint to properly sync the new audio files.
