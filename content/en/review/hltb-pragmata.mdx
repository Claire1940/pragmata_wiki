## Factors That Influence Your Playtime

Not every playthrough of *Pragmata* will be the same. Several factors can either speed up or slow down your journey through the lunar landscape.

### Difficulty Settings
*Pragmata* offers three initial difficulty levels. On "Explorer" (Easy), combat is trivial, allowing you to focus entirely on the story and potentially finish the game in under 8 hours. On "Survivor" (Hard), enemies are much more aggressive, and resources are scarce, which will naturally inflate your playtime as you approach encounters with more caution.

### Exploration and Backtracking
The game encourages backtracking once you unlock new abilities for Diana. Certain areas in the **Solar Power Plant** are inaccessible until you obtain the high-frequency pulse upgrade later in the game. If you choose to return to previous sectors immediately upon unlocking these tools, your **hltb pragmata** time will lean closer to the 15-hour mark.

### Combat vs. Stealth
While *Pragmata* is a third-person shooter, many encounters can be avoided through clever use of the environment and stealth. Players who engage in every fight will find their playtime extended by several hours, whereas those who use Hugh’s cloaking tech to slip past guards can shave significant time off their run.

If you are aiming for a specific **hltb pragmata** target, keep these strategies in mind:

**Q: Does Pragmata have a New Game Plus mode?**

A: Yes, *Pragmata* features a New Game Plus mode where you carry over all your upgrades and equipment. This makes subsequent runs much faster, often allowing players to finish the main story in under 5 hours.

**Q: How many sectors are there in total?**
