## Gameplay and Technical Specs

Pragmata is a systems-driven action game. You play as Hugh, an "unregistered guest" in a lunar facility known as the Cradle. The gameplay revolves around protecting Diana while navigating environments that shift in gravity and physical state. 

For PC players, the game demands modern specs. You can view the latest requirements and community discussions on the [Official Pragmata Steam Page](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1594350/PRAGMATA/).

### Key Gameplay Pillars:
1.  **The Grip Gun:** Use this tool to manipulate the environment, create platforms, or tether enemies.
2.  **Environmental Puzzles:** Many areas require you to use Diana's unique abilities to bypass security protocols.
3.  **The Shelter:** A safe zone where players can decompress, change outfits, and dive into the lore via the data library.
