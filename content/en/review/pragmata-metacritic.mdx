## Gameplay Mechanics: Combat and Hacking

The gameplay in Pragmata is split between heavy-duty combat and environmental puzzle-solving. You play as a pilot who must protect Diana, but she is far from a helpless NPC. Diana provides essential support through hacking and area-of-effect abilities.

### The Role of Diana
Diana acts as your primary tool for interacting with the lunar base's ancient technology. When facing the "Sector Guards" or the security AI known as Edith, you will often find yourself outnumbered and outgunned. This is where Diana's "Overdrive Protocol" becomes essential.

1. **Hacking Terminals**: Diana can disable emergency lockdowns and reconnect primary power sources.
2. **Combat Support**: When the pilot is overwhelmed, Diana can trigger a "Hack" to stun robotic enemies.
3. **Overdrive Protocol**: A high-energy mode that increases the pilot's damage output and speed for a limited duration.

### Enemy Types and Threats
The primary antagonists are the automated defense systems of the lunar colony. These range from basic bipedal drones to the massive "Edith" security protocol.

| Enemy Type | Danger Level | Weakness | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sector Guard** | Low | Core Hacking | Use Diana to stun, then melee the core. |
| **Limb Drones** | Medium | Overdrive | Focus fire on the multiple limbs to disable movement. |
| **Edith (AI Host)** | **High** | **Primary Connection** | Sever the power lines before engaging the main unit. |

> **Warning:** Attempting to fight Edith without first disabling the primary power connection will result in an infinite spawn of Sector Guards.

## Optimization Guide for PC Players

To achieve a score that matches the **pragmata metacritic** benchmarks on your own machine, you may need to tweak several hidden settings. The game is highly dependent on CPU single-core performance and VRAM capacity.

1. **Texture Quality**: If you have less than 10GB of VRAM, keep this at "High" rather than "Ultra" to avoid stuttering during sector transitions.
2. **Ray Tracing**: Turn off "Ray Traced Reflections" first if you need a performance boost; "Ray Traced Global Illumination" is much more impactful for the game's lunar aesthetic.
3. **Emergency Lockdown Settings**: There is a specific post-processing setting labeled "Lockdown Bloom" that can be lowered to improve visibility during intense combat sequences.
