## Factors Influencing the Pragmata Lenght

Several core gameplay pillars contribute to how long you will spend on the moon. *Pragmata* is not just about shooting; it is a game of "codependent cooperation" between Hugh and Diana.

### 1. The Hacking Loop and Combat Synergy
Hugh’s gunplay is intentionally limited by the enemy's armor systems. Because Idis’ rogue AI units are resistant to standard gunfire, you must rely on Diana to hack into their systems. This grid-based minigame requires precision and timing. If you struggle with the hacking interface, combat encounters will take significantly longer. Mastering this synergy is the key to reducing your overall playtime.

### 2. Weapon Degradation and Maintenance
Hugh’s space suit features four weapon units. Higher-tier weapons are powerful but come with limited ammunition and can eventually break. This forces players to return to the Shelter—a safe zone on the station—to perform maintenance and 3D print new components using Lunafilament. Frequent trips back to the Shelter and the management of these resources add a layer of tactical depth that extends the **pragmata length**.

### 3. Navigation and the Tram System
The station is vast, and you will often find yourself unlocking escape hatches that double as checkpoints and fast travel points. The "Tram" allows you to zip between these hatches and the Shelter. Players who choose to walk and explore every "nook and cranny" for upgrade materials will find their playtime stretching toward the 30-hour mark.

## Exploring the Shelter and Upgrades

The Shelter is more than just a menu; it is a physical space where you interact with Cabin, an affable robot connected to the station’s larger systems. Spending time here is essential for progression.

| Upgrade Category | Material Required | Effect on Gameplay |
|---|---|---|
| **Weapon Slots** | Lunum Ore | Increases the variety of weapons Hugh can carry. |
| **Hacking Nodes** | Lunafilament | Simplifies Diana's hacking minigames. |
| **Thruster Units** | Tech Scraps | Increases hover time and dash distance for Hugh. |
| **Shelter Expansion** | Cabin Credits | Unlocks new 3D printing blueprints and lore entries. |

By engaging with the 3D printing systems, you can create potent combinations of attacks. For example, synergizing Hugh's slowdown effect (triggered by a perfect dodge) with Diana’s high-speed hacking can turn a ten-minute boss fight into a five-minute encounter.
