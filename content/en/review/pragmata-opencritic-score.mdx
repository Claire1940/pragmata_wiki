## Hacking Puzzles and Environmental Interaction

A significant portion of the gameplay involves navigating the lunar station's security systems. Diana plays a crucial role here, as she can interface with ports to lower enemy defenses. These puzzles are not merely busywork; they often provide the tactical advantage needed to overcome overwhelming odds.

Critics contributing to the **pragmata opencritic score** have noted that the complexity of these puzzles scales significantly. By mid-game, you will be managing Hugh's physical defense of Diana while she completes multi-stage uploads.

### Hacking Strategies
*   **Prioritize the Port:** Always identify the upload port before engaging the first wave of robots.
*   **Use Decoys:** Hugh can deploy holographic decoys to draw fire away from Diana during the hacking process.
*   **Environmental Kills:** Look for overhead canisters or loose wiring that can be triggered via the hacking interface to clear groups of enemies instantly.

## Exploration and Upgrades

Outside of combat, the game focuses on "Shelters." These are safe zones where Hugh and Diana can rest and upgrade their equipment. 

*   **Scavenging:** Collect "Lunar Shards" from fallen robots to trade for weapon parts.
*   **Diana’s Abilities:** Investing in Diana’s skill tree allows her to hack more efficiently and even provide passive combat buffs to Hugh.
*   **Sketchbook Logs:** Finding hidden data pads adds lore entries to the Sketchbook, which are essential for players looking to achieve 100% completion.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Some shelters are temporary. If you leave a shelter before completing your upgrades, you may not be able to return to that specific location until New Game Plus.
