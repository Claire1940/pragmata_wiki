## Core Gameplay Mechanics: The Protagonist and Diana

The heart of the gameplay lies in the partnership between the armored soldier and Diana. Unlike traditional escort missions, Diana is an active participant in combat and exploration. She serves as the primary tool for environmental interaction, specifically through her "Overdrive Protocol" and hacking abilities.

### Cooperative Combat Systems

Combat in **pragmata switch 2** is a blend of ranged gunplay and heavy melee strikes. The protagonist handles the raw firepower, while Diana provides utility and crowd control. During the "Emergency Lockdown" sequences, you must protect Diana while she interfaces with terminals to restore functionality to the area.

### Navigating the Lunar Base

Exploration often involves verticality and puzzle-solving. You will frequently find yourself searching for the "Main Power Terminal" to bypass security lockdowns. The game encourages scanning the environment to identify hidden threats or resource caches.

1.  **Scan the Area:** Use the built-in HUD to detect haywire bots before they detect you.
2.  **Identify Terminals:** Look for the yellow power icons to find reboot stations.
3.  **Coordinate Hacking:** Position the protagonist to cover Diana’s blind spots while she works.
4.  **Restore Power:** Reconnecting the primary power levels (30% to 100%) usually triggers a boss encounter or a wave of reinforcements.

## Enemy Variations and Boss Encounters

The robotic enemies in Pragmata, often referred to as "Bots" or "Sector Guards," have gone haywire for reasons tied to the game's central mystery. These enemies range from small, agile drones to massive, multi-limbed guardians like "Ed."

### Sector Guard: Ed

One of the first major hurdles players face is the unauthorized access guardian known as Ed. This boss utilizes a "Sector Guard Pod" to scan and neutralize threats. Fighting Ed requires a mix of dodging his heavy ground-pound attacks and using Diana’s hacking to create openings in his armor.

> **Warning:** When the "Unauthorized Access Detected" warning appears, immediately seek cover. Ed's primary laser sweep can deplete your shields in a single hit.

## Advanced Strategies for Survival

To truly master the lunar surface, you must look beyond basic shooting. The environment is often your greatest ally. For instance, many rooms contain "Power Controllers" that can be overloaded to create an EMP effect, stunning all nearby robots.

### Using the Environment
*   **Elevators and Lifts:** Use these to gain high-ground advantages. Diana can hack lifts to move them even during combat.
*   **Manual Reboots:** If the power drops below 50%, certain doors will lock. Always prioritize the main power terminal to keep your escape routes open.
*   **Earth Contact:** Your ultimate goal is to contact Earth. Keep an eye on your "Signal Strength" meter in the HUD; higher signal strength often unlocks better map data.

### Character Synergy Tips
Diana isn't just a hacker; she is your eyes and ears. When she says, "Don't look now, but we got company," she is usually flagging an enemy spawn that hasn't appeared on your radar yet. Listening to her dialogue cues is just as important as watching the screen.

## FAQ

**Q: Does Pragmata Switch 2 support local co-op?**

A: While the game focuses on the relationship between the protagonist and Diana, it is primarily a single-player experience where the AI controls Diana's actions based on your commands. There is currently no local split-screen mode.

**Q: How does the Switch 2 version differ from other consoles?**

A: The **pragmata switch 2** version features exclusive motion control options for aiming and utilizes the console's haptic feedback to simulate the feeling of lunar gravity and suit malfunctions.

**Q: What should I do if Diana gets captured by a Sector Guard?**

A: If Diana is targeted, the "Emergency Lockdown" will often intensify. You must immediately use a heavy attack or a missile to stagger the enemy holding her. If she is incapacitated, you will lose access to hacking and scanning abilities until she is revived.

**Q: Are there multiple endings in Pragmata?**

A: Capcom has hinted that player choices regarding how they interact with the "Haywire Bots" and how quickly they restore power to the lunar base may influence the final outcome of the mission to contact Earth.
