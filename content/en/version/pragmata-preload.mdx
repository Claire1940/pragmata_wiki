## Maximizing Your Pragmata Preload Speed

A successful **pragmata preload** relies on more than just a fast internet connection; it requires proper configuration of your client’s download settings. Since the game utilizes the RE Engine's "Reach for the Moon" architecture, it handles file compression differently than standard Unreal or Unity titles.

1. **Clear Download Cache**: On Steam, go to Settings > Downloads > Clear Download Cache. This ensures no old data interferes with the new files.
2. **Disable ISP Throttling**: If possible, use a VPN or change your DNS to Google (8.8.8.8) to avoid regional traffic shaping during high-demand hours.
3. **Allocate Disk Space**: Ensure you have at least 110 GB of free space. The **pragmata preload** requires extra room for the temporary "depot" files used during the installation.
4. **Wired Connection**: Always use a Cat6 Ethernet cable. Wi-Fi 6 is stable, but a physical connection minimizes packet loss during the 85 GB transfer.

> **Warning:** Avoid using "Auto-Update" settings for the preload. Manually trigger the download to ensure you are pulling the latest version of the build, including the Day One patch.

## Platform-Specific Preload Instructions

Managing your **pragmata preload** files varies depending on whether you are on console or PC. While Steam users have the most flexibility with file verification, PS5 and Xbox players benefit from the "Background Download" feature which functions even when the console is in sleep mode.

### PlayStation 5
To begin the process on PS5, navigate to your Game Library and select the purchased title. Ensure "Auto-Download" is checked in the system settings. The PS5 version often includes a separate "Digital Artbook" download; you can choose to skip this to save space, but it is recommended to download the base game and the "High-Resolution Asset Pack" simultaneously.

### Xbox Series X/S
Xbox users can utilize the Xbox Mobile App to trigger a **pragmata preload** even before they have officially purchased the game. This "remote install" feature is perfect for those waiting for a physical disc to arrive but who want the data ready on their internal SSD.

### PC (Steam/Epic Games Store)
Steam users will see a "Preload" button replace the "Purchase" or "Coming Soon" button once the window opens. After the download completes, the files will appear in a folder named `depotcache`. Do not delete these files! They will be moved to the `common/Pragmata` folder automatically upon launch.

## Optimization and RE Engine Performance

Once the **pragmata preload** is installed and the game is unlocked, performance tuning becomes the priority. The RE Engine is known for its excellent optimization in enclosed spaces—such as the lunar base corridors—but can struggle in wide-open crater environments if settings aren't adjusted.

| Setting | Impact | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Ray Tracing** | High GPU/VRAM | Turn Off on 8GB cards to avoid graininess |
| **DLSS/FSR** | High Performance Gain | Set to **Quality** for 1440p displays |
| **Texture Quality** | Medium VRAM | Set to **High (4GB)** for most mid-range builds |
| **Mesh Quality** | Medium CPU | Keep at **High** to avoid "pop-in" |

Players who missed the initial **pragmata preload** window may find that the game takes longer to "verify" files on a standard HDD. If you are experiencing long loading screens or audio desync, moving the game to an NVMe SSD is the most effective fix.
