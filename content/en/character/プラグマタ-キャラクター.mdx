### Hugh Williams: The Relentless Auditor

Hugh Williams serves as the primary perspective for the player. As a system auditor dispatched to the lunar facility, he is trained in both technical diagnostics and high-stakes security. Following a massive "Moonquake" that leaves him stranded and severely injured, Hugh must rely on his wits and his companion to find a way back to Earth.

In terms of gameplay, Hugh is the combat specialist among the **プラグマタ キャラクター**. He is proficient with a wide array of firearms, ranging from standard-issue pulse rifles to heavy lunar-grade artillery. Despite his professional exterior, Hugh is known for being blunt—often speaking his mind even when it might be better to remain silent. However, his core nature is compassionate, as seen in his protective stance toward Diana.

> **💡 Tip:** Hugh’s mobility is limited by his heavy EVA suit, so use Diana’s hacking abilities to create environmental advantages before engaging in direct firefights.

### Diana (D-I-0336-7): The Soul of the Moon

Diana is perhaps the most mysterious of all the **プラグマタ キャラクター**. Officially designated as a "Pragmata"—a specialized android constructed entirely from Luna-filament—she represents the pinnacle of lunar technology. Diana is the one who rescues Hugh after the initial disaster, setting the stage for their partnership.

Her abilities are centered around utility and environmental interaction. Diana can hack into facility terminals, override hostile security bots, and manipulate the very fabric of the Luna-filament structures around her. While she possesses immense learning potential and curiosity, her data banks are initially sparse, leading to innocent and sometimes unpredictable reactions to the world around her. She is particularly fond of the name "Diana," which was given to her by Hugh.

## Support Systems and the "Cabin" Bot

Survival on the moon requires more than just combat prowess; it requires a base of operations. The interaction between the main **プラグマタ キャラクター** and their support systems is what allows for long-term exploration of the lunar surface.

### The Role of Cabin

While Hugh and Diana explore the ruins, "Cabin" manages the safety of their base. Cabin is a stand-alone support robot stationed within the shelter. Unlike the cold, functional drones found elsewhere in the facility, Cabin is programmed with a friendly and humorous personality.

## Combat Synergy and Character Interaction

The gameplay loop of Pragmata revolves around how these **プラグマタ キャラクター** complement each other. While Hugh handles the physical threats, Diana provides the tactical oversight necessary to bypass impossible odds.

1.  **Tactical Hacking:** During combat, players can command Diana to disable enemy shields or turn environmental hazards against foes.
2.  **Resource Gathering:** Diana’s Luna-filament body allows her to "sense" hidden caches of material that Hugh’s sensors might miss.
3.  **Dynamic Escort:** Protecting Diana is not just a mission objective; her survival is tied to the player's ability to access new areas of the map.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Diana’s "innocent" AI means she may occasionally wander toward interesting objects during quiet moments. Always keep an eye on her position in hostile zones.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I play as Diana in the game?**

A: While Hugh Williams is the primary playable character for combat, players can issue direct commands to Diana and utilize her specialized hacking skills to solve puzzles and navigate the environment. Some sequences may allow for more direct control of her unique abilities.

**Q: What exactly is a "Pragmata" in the context of the プラグマタ キャラクター?**

A: In the game's lore, "Pragmata" refers to a special class of androids made from Luna-filament. They are designed to copy and reproduce object information, making them highly adaptable and essential for lunar exploration.

**Q: Does the relationship between the characters affect the ending?**

A: While Capcom has not confirmed multiple endings, they have emphasized that the "data accumulation" and learning process of Diana are central to the narrative. How you interact with her throughout the journey will likely influence the story's emotional conclusion.

**Q: Are there other human survivors besides Hugh?**

A: Hugh was part of a larger investigation team that was scattered during the massive Moonquake. A significant portion of the game involves searching for his lost comrades while uncovering the mystery of why the lunar facility went silent.
