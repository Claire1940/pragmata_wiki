## Diana (D-I-0336-7): The Advanced Android

Diana is perhaps the most intriguing **pragmata main character** due to her unique biological and synthetic composition. Known formally as D-I-0336-7, she is a "Pragmata"—a specialized android created using a rare substance known as Lunafilament. Despite her appearance as a young girl, she possesses computational power and hacking capabilities that far exceed any human or standard worker bot.

Her personality is defined by an "endless curiosity." Because she has limited accumulated data regarding the world around her, she often reacts to her environment with a sense of innocent wonder. This makes her a compelling lens through which the player views the decaying lunar colony. However, players should not let her childlike appearance fool them; she is a formidable asset in the field.

### Diana's Core Abilities

1.  **System Hacking**: Diana can penetrate the firewalls of complex facility systems, opening doors and activating environmental traps.
2.  **Bot Overriding**: She has the specific ability to hack into the armor of worker and security bots, potentially turning enemies into temporary allies.
3.  **Data Analysis**: Her Lunafilament construction allows her to process environmental data in real-time, highlighting objectives and hidden paths for Hugh.

> **💡 Tip:** Protect Diana during hacking sequences. While she is powerful, she is vulnerable to physical damage while her processing power is diverted to system overrides.

## The World of Lunafilament

To understand any **pragmata main character**, one must understand Lunafilament. This substance is the lifeblood of the game's technology. It is what allows Diana to exist and what powers the various bots you encounter. The mystery of where this filament comes from—and why it seems to be reacting to Diana's presence—is a central theme of the 2026 storyline.

As you progress, you will find "Art Guides" and data logs that explain the history of the facility. These logs often mention the creation of the "Pragmata" units, suggesting that Diana might not be the only one of her kind, though she is certainly the most advanced. The "D-I" prefix in her serial number hints at a lineage of androids that preceded her, though few remain functional in the current timeline.

## FAQ

**Q: Is there a third pragmata main character players can control?**

A: At launch in 2026, the game focuses exclusively on the duo of Hugh Williams and Diana. While other NPCs exist within the lunar colony, the core gameplay loop revolves around the synergy between these two characters.

**Q: Can Diana be customized or upgraded?**

A: Yes. Throughout the game, you can collect Lunafilament shards to upgrade Diana's hacking speed and bot-override capabilities. These upgrades are essential for tackling the more difficult security systems found in the later stages of the lunar facility.

**Q: What is the significance of the name "Diana" for the pragmata main character?**

A: In the game's lore, the name was given to her by Hugh Williams. For an android like Diana, who began her existence as a serial number (D-I-0336-7), the name represents her burgeoning humanity and her unique bond with Hugh.

**Q: Does Hugh Williams ever remove his helmet?**
