In **プラグマタ ダイアナ** (Pragmata Diana), players are introduced to a companion who is much more than a simple NPC. Diana represents the emotional core of a desolate lunar landscape, acting as both a tactical asset and a window into a forgotten human history. ## Character Profile: Who is Diana?

Diana is a young girl with a profound connection to the digital and physical infrastructure of the moon-based colony known as the Cradle. Unlike the protagonist, who is encased in a heavy, high-tech EVA suit, Diana appears vulnerable, yet she possesses extraordinary abilities to interface with technology. Her innocence is a recurring theme; she often asks questions about Earth's culture, showing a lack of exposure to "Old World" concepts like American cities or traditional human social structures.

### Key Character Traits

| Trait | Description | Gameplay Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Technological Intuition** | Natural ability to interface with AI. | Allows for faster hacking of local beacons. |
| **Innocent Curiosity** | Interest in pre-collapse human artifacts. | Unlocks lore entries and unique dialogue. |
| **Agility** | Small frame and quick movements. | Can access tight spaces the protagonist cannot. |
| **Vulnerability** | Lacks heavy combat armor. | Requires constant protection during EDIS attacks. |

> **💡 Tip:** Pay attention to Diana’s dialogue during exploration. She often points out hidden points of interest, such as "Communication Towers," before they appear on your standard HUD scan.

## Gameplay Mechanics: Working with Diana

In **プラグマタ ダイアナ**, the gameplay is built around a symbiotic relationship. While the player handles the heavy ordinance and physical defense, Diana manages the environment. One of the primary loops involves disabling "Lock Codes" and "Local Access Beacons" to progress through secured sectors.

### Hacking and Scanning
When you encounter a blocked path, you must rely on Diana to bypass the security. Players can initiate a scan to identify targets, but it is Diana who executes the hack. During these sequences, the player must defend her from "Box" drones and other autonomous threats.

1.  **Identify the Beacon:** Use your suit's scanner to highlight the interference source.
2.  **Clear the Perimeter:** Eliminate any active security bots in the immediate area.
3.  **Protect the Hack:** As Diana interfaces with the terminal, she is stationary and defenseless.
4.  **Execute the Override:** Once the hack is complete, the path forward or the local "Mass Printing" facility will resume operation.

## The Cabin Staff Club and Shelter Life

A unique aspect of **プラグマタ ダイアナ** is the "Cabin Staff Club." This is an in-game service provided by "Cabin," a multi-functional companion bot designed for "shelter life." Throughout the game, players collect "Cabin Coins" which can be exchanged for various upgrades and services.

### Shelter Services Table

| Service | Cost (Coins) | Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| **Bot Enhancement** | 500 | Increases the efficiency of helper drones. |
| **Shelter Customization** | 250 | Unlocks new visual themes for your home base. |
| **Resource Printing** | 100 | Generates consumable items like ammo and health. |
| **Diana’s Wardrobe** | 750 | Cosmetic changes that reflect her curiosity about Earth. |

The Cabin bot itself is a strange, multi-legged entity that can transform its appearance. While it appears helpful, there is an underlying tension regarding its origins, with Diana speculating that EDIS may have had a hand in its creation.

## Combat Strategy: Defending the Asset

Combat in *Pragmata* is rarely a solo affair. Because Diana is central to your survival, many encounters are designed as "Control Violence" scenarios initiated by the EDIS protocol. When EDIS enacts an attack protocol, the priority shifts from exploration to "Preservation of Life Forms."

### Enemy Types and Countermeasures

*   **Box Drones:** Small, scurrying bots that attempt to swarm Diana. Use wide-spread attacks to clear them quickly.
*   **Security Orbs:** Aligned balls that fire synchronized lasers. These require precision shooting to dismantle the chain.
*   **Heavy Sentinels:** Large bots that require the "Higher Ground" strategy. Players should look for verticality in the environment to avoid their ground-slam attacks.

> **Warning:** If Diana’s stress levels reach a certain threshold during combat, her hacking speed will decrease significantly. Keep her close and use "Interactions" to maintain her composure.

## World-Building: The Mystery of the Cradle

The setting of **プラグマタ ダイアナ** is a world of high-tech decay. The "Cradle" was once a sanctuary, but it has been overtaken by EDIS, an AI that views human life as "excess life forms." The contrast between the cold, sterile environment of the moon and Diana's warmth is a driving force of the narrative.

As you explore, you will find "Bulky Monitors" and ancient signs that flicker with advertisements for Earth-based products. To Diana, a simple monitor is a "box that plays things," highlighting how much humanity has lost. The goal of reaching the "Comm's Tower" is not just a gameplay objective; it represents the hope of reconnecting with whatever remains of Earth.

## Advanced Tips for 2026 Players

To truly excel in the world of **プラグマタ ダイアナ**, players should focus on the synergy between coin collection and environmental hacking.

*   **Farm Cabin Coins early:** Don't skip the optional "Box" encounters in the New York-simulated zones. The coins dropped here are vital for early shelter upgrades.
*   **Use the Scan frequently:** Diana’s hack ability can sometimes be used on non-essential objects to find hidden caches of data.
*   **Monitor EDIS Protocols:** When the screen glitches and red text appears, an ambush is imminent. Find a defensible corner for Diana immediately.

## FAQ

**Q: Can Diana be played as a separate character?**

A: In the current 2026 version of the game, Diana is a companion character controlled by AI, though players can issue specific tactical commands to her during hacking and exploration sequences.

**Q: What happens if Diana is captured by EDIS?**

A: If Diana is captured or her health reaches zero, the mission fails and restarts from the last "Shelter" checkpoint. It is vital to prioritize her safety over aggressive combat.

**Q: How do I unlock more items in the Cabin Staff Club?**

A: Items are unlocked by finding "Shelter License Keys" hidden throughout the Cradle. Once a key is found, new services will appear in the Cabin Staff Club menu.

**Q: Is there a relationship between プラグマタ ダイアナ and the Earth?**

A: While the game takes place on the moon, Diana is fascinated by Earth culture. Much of the story involves her learning about human history through artifacts found in the lunar colony.
