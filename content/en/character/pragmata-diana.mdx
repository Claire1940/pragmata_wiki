## The Role of Diana in Pragmata

Diana is a young android who meets the protagonist, Hugh Williams, during an investigation of abnormalities at the Cradle. While Hugh provides the firepower and physical protection, Diana provides the technical expertise required to navigate the lunar facility. Her primary resource is **Luna Filament**, a unique mineral refined from the moon that can absorb and copy information.

In 2026, players will find that Diana is responsible for:
*   **Hacking Security Protocols:** Opening doors, activating lifts, and disabling area lockdowns.
*   **Combat Support:** Stun-locking enemies and applying debuffs through the interrupt matrix.
*   **Resource Management:** Identifying and collecting upgrades for use in the shelter hub.
*   **Navigation:** Scanning the environment to highlight objectives and hidden paths.

> **💡 Tip:** Pay attention to Diana's idle animations. She often provides subtle hints about nearby items or reacts to the environment, which can lead to discovering hidden "Sketchbook" drawings.

## Mastering the Pragmata Diana Hacking Matrix

The core gameplay loop involves Hugh protecting Diana while she interfaces with the "interrupt matrix." This is not a passive event; players must actively participate in a hacking mini-game that requires precision and speed. By moving a cursor across a grid to connect with specific nodes, you can change the tide of battle.

| Node Type | Color | Effect | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **EXE Node** | **Green** | Completes the hack and stuns enemies. | Prioritize this to end the mini-game quickly. |
| **Damage Node** | **Blue** | Increases damage dealt to the target. | Pass through these before hitting the EXE node. |
| **Debuff Node** | **Yellow** | Disrupts internal systems to increase stagger. | Best used on heavy "Walker" units. |
| **Multi-Hack** | **Yellow (Icon)** | Links multiple enemies for shared damage. | Essential for crowd control during ambushes. |

When utilizing **pragmata diana** during combat, the hacking matrix appears on the right side of the screen. This requires a "thinking man's" approach to combat, as you must balance aiming Hugh’s weapons with your thumb while navigating the matrix with the other.

## Combat Synergy and Overdrive Protocol

The **pragmata diana** Overdrive Protocol is an ultimate ability that becomes available once the hacking gauge is fully charged. When activated (typically by pressing L and R simultaneously), Diana releases a massive burst of lunar filament energy that "opens" all nearby enemies.

### Weapon Interactions
Hugh’s arsenal is designed to capitalize on the openings Diana creates. Certain weapons are more effective when used in tandem with specific hacking nodes.

| Weapon | Type | Diana Synergy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pistol** | **Infinite Ammo** | Best for picking off small bots while hacking. |
| **Shockwave Gun** | **Shotgun** | Use after a **Yellow Node** debuff for massive stagger. |

## Advanced Tips for Diana Synergy

To truly master **pragmata diana**, you must treat her as an extension of your own movement. In 2026, high-level play involves "Fast Moves"—dodging just before an attack to slow down time, providing a perfect window for Diana to initiate a complex multi-hack.

*   **Manual Rebooting:** When the power plant controller fails, use Diana to scan for the main power terminal rather than wandering.
*   **Thruster Management:** Hugh’s thrusters (stamina) are vital for verticality. Use Diana’s hacking to activate lift platforms so you can save your thruster gauge for combat maneuvers.
*   **Escape Hatches:** These serve as checkpoints and hubs. Always have Diana hack these immediately upon entry to ensure you can return to the shelter to upgrade your gear.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Diana a playable character in Pragmata?**

A: While you primarily control Hugh Williams, **pragmata diana** is an active participant in combat through the hacking matrix and Overdrive Protocol. You do not control her movement directly, but you control her technical actions.

**Q: How do I heal in Pragmata?**

A: You must hold the designated button (Circle/B) to repair "Suit Integrity." Diana also assists by administering cellular repair and biomedical diagnostics during quiet moments or at the shelter hub.

**Q: What happens if I fail the hacking mini-game?**

A: Failing to reach the EXE node in time often leaves Hugh vulnerable to counter-attacks. However, the game allows for quick retries, and you can pick up hacking modules as resources to make future attempts easier.

**Q: Are there different outfits for Diana?**

A: Yes, players can unlock various suits for both Hugh and Diana. Some, like the "Scribble Suit," are cosmetic rewards for exploration, while others like the "Delphi Suit" may be tied to story progression in the 2026 campaign.
