## Combat and Skill-Based Achievements

Pragmata introduces the "Red Zone," areas of high-intensity corruption where the environment itself becomes a weapon against the player. Dominating these zones is a significant part of the **プラグマタ トロフィー** experience.

### Conquering the Red Zone
The "Confident in Skill" (シルバー_腕に自信あり) trophy requires the player to suppress a Red Zone. This isn't just about killing enemies; it involves a combination of tactical movement, environmental hacking, and protecting your companion.

1. **Prioritize Hacking Nodes**: Use your scanner to identify nodes that can turn enemy turrets against them.
2. **Module Synergy**: Equip modules that increase your "Synchro Rate" with Diana to unleash powerful dual-attacks.
3. **Crowd Control**: Use "Lead Earth" canisters to create temporary barriers or traps for encroaching robotic threats.

### Mastery of Hacking and Gear
To survive the later stages of the game, you must invest in your equipment. The "Strongest Weapon" and "Max Module" trophies are earned by collecting materials found in the lunar ruins and Earth Memory fragments.

> **Warning:** Upgrading the wrong module early on can make the mid-game significantly harder. Focus on the "Hacking Speed" and "Energy Efficiency" modules first to ensure you can interact with the environment under pressure.

## Exploration and Collection Guide

A significant portion of the **プラグマタ トロフィー** list is dedicated to finding hidden items. The game world is filled with "Earth Memory" fragments—remnants of human culture that provide lore and upgrade points.

| Collection Item | Purpose | Trophy Tied to It |

Finding every "Mini Cabin" is essential not just for the trophy, but for survival. These cabins act as checkpoints where you can swap out your gear and interact with the Cabin Stamp Board. Completing a full board grants the "ALL Bingo!" trophy, which requires you to perform specific tasks like "Perfect Hacking" or "Zero Damage Combat" in specific sectors.

## Advanced Strategies for the Platinum

To secure the **プラグマタ トロフィー** Platinum, you will likely need at least one and a half playthroughs. While "Promise of Two" can be earned on any difficulty, the "Unknown Signal" often demands the stats and gear found in a New Game Plus (NG+) run.

1. **First Playthrough (Story Focus)**: Focus on enjoying the narrative and finding as many Earth Memories as possible. Do not worry about the "S-Rank" combat trophies yet.
2. **Cleanup Phase**: Before the final boss, revisit previous sectors to suppress any remaining Red Zones and finish your Cabin Stamp Boards.
3. **Post-Game/NG+**: Tackle the "Unknown Signal" with your fully upgraded gear. The "PRAGMATA" Platinum will trigger once the final challenge is completed.

## FAQ

**Q: Are there any missable trophies in the プラグマタ トロフィー list?**

A: Currently, most trophies can be earned via Chapter Select or in the post-game. However, certain "Cabin Stamp Board" tasks are easier to complete during specific story beats. It is recommended to check your stamp board every time you reach a new Mini Cabin to ensure you don't fall behind on sector-specific challenges.

**Q: What is the hardest trophy to obtain in Pragmata?**

A: "A Worthy Man" (Gold) is generally considered the hardest. It requires completing the "Unknown Signal," which features the most aggressive enemy AI and limited healing resources. Proper module optimization is a must for this achievement.

**Q: Do difficulty settings affect the プラグマタ トロフィー list?**

A: You can earn the "Promise of Two" trophy on any difficulty, including Easy. However, the game does not explicitly state if higher difficulties grant unique rewards, though playing on Harder settings typically provides more upgrade materials, which speeds up the "Max Upgrade" trophies.

**Q: How many Earth Memories are required for the collection trophy?**

A: There are 50 Earth Memory fragments scattered across the Data Center and Lunar surface. While you don't need all of them for basic upgrades, the "Memory Collector" trophy requires a 100% find rate for all unique fragments.
