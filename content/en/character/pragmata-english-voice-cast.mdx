## Technical Voice Directives in the "Cradle"

A unique aspect of the game's audio design involves the "unregistered guest" protocols. When the characters enter a restricted area, the voice acting becomes a gameplay mechanic. Players must listen for audio cues from the **pragmata english voice cast** to identify when a sector guard like Edith is about to initialize a scanning protocol.

1.  **Scanning Phase**: Voice becomes rhythmic and repetitive.
2.  **Target Identification**: A sharp increase in volume and aggression.
3.  **Neutralization**: Cold, monotone delivery indicating an attack is imminent.
