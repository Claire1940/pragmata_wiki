### Handling the "Heat" Mechanic

As seen in the official trailers, the word "Heat" is a warning of imminent danger. In gameplay terms, this refers to the suit's internal temperature rising due to over-exertion or environmental hazards. If the Heat gauge maxes out, Hugh becomes immobile for several seconds, leaving him vulnerable to the Cradle's security bots.

*   **Venting:** Use the environment's coolant leaks to rapidly lower suit temperature.
*   **Pacing:** Avoid constant sprinting and jumping during combat to keep Heat levels manageable.
*   **Coolant Packs:** Consumable items that provide an instant 50% reduction in Heat.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Engaging more than three security drones at once will cause a 200% increase in Heat generation. Use stealth or environmental traps to thin the numbers.
