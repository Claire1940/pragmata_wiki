The heart of Pragmata lies in the relationship between its two main leads. Unlike traditional action games with a sprawling roster, Capcom has focused on a deep, protective dynamic. This **pragmata all characters list** highlights how these two individuals complement each other's survival skills.

The primary playable character is a nameless astronaut clad in high-tech, pressurized armor. ## Supporting Entities and AI Systems

The world of Pragmata is governed by complex artificial intelligence. As the lunar base, known as "Shelter," falls into disrepair, these systems have become both allies and obstacles. Any thorough **pragmata all characters list** must include the non-human entities that dictate the flow of the game's world.

### Cabin: Your Companion for Shelter
As revealed in the 2026 trailer, "Cabin" is the official companion system for the Shelter facility. Cabin appears to be a multi-functional AI designed to assist inhabitants with logistics and survival. While Cabin presents a friendly, corporate-style persona, the breakdown of the lunar facility has made its role more complicated.

### IDUS: The Facility Overseer
IDUS is the primary system mentioned during the "moonquake" events. When IDUS is damaged, it triggers "attack protocols," which suggests that the system may inadvertently become an antagonist. The Astronaut and Diana must eventually shut IDUS down to secure their passage to Earth.
