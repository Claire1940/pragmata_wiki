## Performance Capture and the RE Engine

Pragmata utilizes a highly advanced version of the RE Engine, which requires the **pragmata voice actors** to undergo extensive motion capture sessions. Unlike traditional voice acting where lines are recorded in a booth, these actors wear specialized suits and head-mounted cameras to record their movements and dialogue simultaneously.

### Technical Casting Requirements

*   **Physicality:** Actors must be able to perform stunts and maintain the "weight" of their character's gear.
*   **Facial Nuance:** The RE Engine captures micro-expressions, meaning the **pragmata voice actors** must deliver subtle emotional cues.
*   **Vocal Range:** The ability to whisper or shout while maintaining clarity is essential for the game's dynamic audio system.
