### Hugh Williams (The Soldier)
Hugh is the primary physical force in the game. Encased in a massive mechanical suit, he provides the "controlled violence" necessary to survive the lunar surface. His primary motivation is protecting Diana and finding a way back to a world she has never seen: Earth.

### Diana (The Pragmata)
Diana is a unique android with the appearance of a young girl. She possesses the innate ability to hack into facility systems and manipulate physical matter via her digital interface. Despite her advanced capabilities, she is characterized by an innocent wonder and a desire to learn about human history.

> **💡 Tip:** Always monitor Diana's energy levels. If she overuses her digital manipulation powers to stabilize "glitches," she will require a cooldown period, leaving Hugh vulnerable.
